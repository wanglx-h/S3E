Subject: Puppet state
A '''puppet state''' is a country that is officially independence but is not so in practice. Puppet governments are usually kept in power by military force provided by an occupying country and are strongly controlled. Puppet states are generally not international relations by most countries, except by the countries which control them and by a few other countries.
