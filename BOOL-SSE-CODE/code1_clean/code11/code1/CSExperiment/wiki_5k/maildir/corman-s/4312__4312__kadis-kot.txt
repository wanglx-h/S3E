Subject: Kadis-kot
'''Kadis-kot''' is a fictional board game in the TV show ''Star Trek: Voyager''. It is played on a six-sided board with three sets of coloured stones. The characters of Naomi Wildman and Seven of Nine played the game sometimes, after the events of the episode "Infinite Regress". ==Other websites== * [http://memory-alpha.org/en/wiki/Kadis-kot A summary of the game] at Memory Alpha Category:Star Trek: Voyager Category:Board games
