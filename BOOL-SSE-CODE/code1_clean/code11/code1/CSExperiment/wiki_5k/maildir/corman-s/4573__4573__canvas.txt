Subject: Canvas
Image:Strainer bar corner 051907.jpg '''Canvas''' is a thick material usually made of Weaving cotton. It is used for tents and sails on boats and other places where a sturdy material would be good. It is similar to the material in blue jeans. It is often a white or pale Tan (color) color. It can also be used for painting or even making shoes.
