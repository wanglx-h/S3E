File:Black triangle2.svg A '''triangle''', otherwise known as a '''trigon''', is a shape, or a part of 2D Space (physics). It has 3 (number) straight sides and three Vertex (geometry). The three angles of a triangle always add up to 180째 (180 Degree (angle)). It is the polygon with the least possible number of sides. A triangle with vertices ''A'', ''B'', ''C'' is written as \triangle ABC. The study of geometry related to triangles is called trigonometry. Modern computers usually use triangles to make more complex graphic images or shapes. == Types of triangles == File:Isosceles-right-triangle.jpg Triangles can be grouped according to how many of their sides are equal: * if all the three sides of a triangle have the same length, then it is an '''equilateral triangle'''. * if a triangle has two sides with the same length, then it is an '''isosceles triangle'''. * if all the three sides of a triangle have different lengths, then we have a '''scalene triangle'''.
