Subject: Homonym
A '''homonym''' is a word that has more than one meaning. For example, ''right'' means moral, the opposite of left, and a personal freedom. Definition: one of a group of words that share the same orthography and/or pronunciation but have different meanings.[http://dictionary.reference.com/browse/homonym homonym], ''Random House unabridged dictionary'' at dictionary.com The point is that homonyms are either homophones (they sound the same) or homographs (they look the same) or both. ==Related pages== * Synonym == References == Category:Words
