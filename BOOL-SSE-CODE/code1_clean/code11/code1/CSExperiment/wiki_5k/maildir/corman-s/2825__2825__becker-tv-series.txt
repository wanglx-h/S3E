| last_aired = | num_episodes = 129 }} '''''Becker''''' was a 1998 United States television series. It was a sitcom on the CBS television network. It starred Ted Danson as Dr. John Becker, a very grumpy family Medical doctor who is unhappy with his life in The Bronx, New York (state). Becker became famous for his witty responses and constant negative outlook on all situations.
