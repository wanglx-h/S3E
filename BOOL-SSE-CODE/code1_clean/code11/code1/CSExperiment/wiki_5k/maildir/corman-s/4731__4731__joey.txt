Subject: Joey
'''Joey''' has the following meanings: * Joey is a common nickname for Joseph, Joe, Joanne, Joan, or Jo. * A joey (marsupial) is a baby kangaroo, or a baby of other marsupials (animals that are like kangaroos). * ''Joey (TV series)'' is a television sitcom (a comedy set in a situation) starring Matt LeBlanc as Joey Tribbiani his character from ''Friends''.
