'''11''' is a year in the 1st century. It was a common year starting on Thursday of the Julian calendar. At the time, it was known as the '''Year of the Roman consul of Manius Aemilius Lepidus (consul 11) and Titus Statilius Taurus'''. == Events == * Germania Inferior and the Rhine secured by Germanicus * Augustus ends his plan to create a defensive border at the Elbe. This is to reinforce the Ancient Rome defence along the Rhine and the Danube. * Artabanus III of the Arsacid Dynasty becomes ruler of Parthia * In India, Satakarni begins his reign as Emperor of the Andhra Empire (11-29) == Births == * == Deaths == * Marcus Antistius Labeo, Roman jurist Category:10s Category:11
