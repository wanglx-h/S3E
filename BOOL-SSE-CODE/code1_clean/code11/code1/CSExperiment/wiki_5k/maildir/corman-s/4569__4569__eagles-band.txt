They formed in Los Angeles, California in 1971. They are known worldwide for their hit songs ''Hotel California'' and ''Take it Easy''. Their self-titled debut album came out in 1972, and became a hit right away. The band broke up in 1980 due to internal tensions, but reunited in 1994. In 2006, they held the record for most albums sold, with their ''Greatest Hits, Volume I'' On January 18, 2016, Glenn Frey died of pneumonia at age 67.
