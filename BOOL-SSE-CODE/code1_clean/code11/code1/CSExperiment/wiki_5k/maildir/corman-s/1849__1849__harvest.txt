Subject: Harvest
Image:28-estate,Taccuino Sanitatis, Casanatense 4182..jpg image]] To '''harvest''' means to :wikt:collect what has been planted and wikt:grow in the ground. It is usually done by farmers. They can do this by picking crops. Crop failure (also known as harvest failure) is a loss or reduced crop yield due to plants being damaged, killed, or destroyed. Sometimes it brings famine. "Harvest" can also mean to collect other things than plants such as aquaculture bivalves including oysters, scallops, and other animals. Category:Agriculture
