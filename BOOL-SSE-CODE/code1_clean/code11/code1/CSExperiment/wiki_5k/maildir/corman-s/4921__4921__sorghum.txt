''Sorghum'' Hackel. }} '''''Sorghum''''' is a genus in the grass family (biology) ''Poaceae''. The plants are grown in warmer climates. Species grow naturally in tropical and subtropical regions of all continents in addition to Oceania and Australasia. Because many species of Sorghum are resistant to drought and high temperatures, it is a very important food source in the desert areas of Africa. ==References== Category:Grains
