Subject: Listen Up
'''''Listen Up!''''' was a 2004 CBS television programme. It was a sitcom starring Jason Alexander as Tony Kleinman, a sportscaster from the fictional "''Listen Up!''" His cohost is named Bernie, and is a hall of famer. Jason has a wife, Dana, and two kids, one named Mickey. Mickey is very good at golf, but gets low grades. The series only lasted for one season, 22 episodes.
