He has been in lots of movies. Neill has been in ''Jurassic Park'', ''Dead Calm'', ''Event Horizon'', ''Sirens'' and lots of other very famous movies. In recent years, he appeared in ''Thor: Ragnarok'' (2017) and ''Peter Rabbit (movie)'' (2018). Neill was born on 14 September 1947 in Omagh, County Tyrone, Northern Ireland to a New Zealander father and English people mother. He moved to New Zealand in 1954. He studied at Christ's College, Canterbury and at University of Canterbury. Neill was married to Lisa Harrow until they divorced in 1989. Then he was married to Noriko Watanabe. He has two children. Neill has New Zealand and Republic of Ireland citizenships.
