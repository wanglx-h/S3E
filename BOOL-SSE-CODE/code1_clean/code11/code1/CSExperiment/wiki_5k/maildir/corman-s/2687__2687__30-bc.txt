Subject: 30 BC
The year '''30 BC''' was either a common year starting on Wednesday, Common year starting on Thursday or Common year starting on Friday or a leap year starting on Thursday. At the time, it was known as the '''Year of the Consulship of Octavian and Crassus'''. == Events == === Roman Republic === * Caesar Augustus becomes List of Early Imperial Roman Consuls for the fourth time. His partner is Marcus Licinius Crassus (consul 30 BC). * Octavian leads his army to the Dardanelles. He marches into Syria (Roman province). Herod the Great sends him vows of loyalty and troops to help. * Cornelius Gallus arrives in Cyrene, Libya and occupies Paraetonium. * Mark Antony retreats with his army to Ptolemaic Egypt. He learns that Pelusium opened its gates to Octavian without resistance. * Octavian Caesar captures Alexandria. This marks the official annexation of Ancient Egypt to the Roman Republic. * Cleopatra wikt:evacuate to Berenice Troglodytica on the west coast of the Red Sea. King Malchus of Nabatea attacks from the desert and burns the Egyptian ships. * The children of Cleopatra are spared by Octavian. Octavia Minor raises Alexander Helios, Cleopatra Selene II and Ptolemy Philadelphus (Cleopatra) in her house in Rome. === Asia === * Common date given for Thiruvalluvar to write the ''Tirukkuṟaḷ'' * First possible date for the invention of the wheelbarrow == Births == * Maroboduus, king of the Marcomanni (d. 37) == Deaths == * Mark Antony, Roman Roman consul and general (b. 83 BC) * Marcus Antonius Antyllus, son of Mark Antony and Fulvia (b. 47 BC) * Hyrcanus II, king and high priest of Judea until 40 BC * August 12 Cleopatra VII of Egypt, last queen of Ptolemaic Egypt (b. 69 BC) * August 23 Caesarion, son of Julius Caesar and pharaoh of Egypt (b. 47 BC) Category:30 BC
