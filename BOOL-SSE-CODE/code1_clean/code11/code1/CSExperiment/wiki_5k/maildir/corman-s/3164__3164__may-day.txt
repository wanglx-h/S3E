Subject: May Day
File:Observance of International Workers' Day RGBY.svg May 1 is known in many countries as '''May Day''' or '''International Labor Day''' for human rights of workers and labour movement. This day is a holiday in most countries, including UK,China, Cuba, Czech republic, France, Russia, Brazil, Austria, Germany, Serbia, Hungary, North Macedonia, Romania, Montenegro, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Kenya, Slovenia, Slovakia, Norway, Sweden, Turkey, Portugal, Bulgaria, Vietnam and Argentina. May Day commemorates worker demonstrations for eight-hour workweeks in Chicago, May, 1886. More specifically, it commemorates the Haymarket affair and the execution of multiple anarchist and marxist leaders. Category:Holidays Category:May observances Category:Labor
