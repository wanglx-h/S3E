Subject: Governor-general
'''Governor General''' is a representative of a head of state. The term is mostly used in Commonwealth realm countries, where the governor general is a representative of the British monarch, who is the head of state of the Commonwealth realm countries. Sometimes the title is spelled as ''Governor General'', which is used in Canada, but sometimes it is spelled with a hyphen "''-''", ''Governor-General'', which is the spelling used in Australia. == Other articles == *Governor-General of Australia *Governor General of Canada == References == Category:Governors-general Category:Government occupations
