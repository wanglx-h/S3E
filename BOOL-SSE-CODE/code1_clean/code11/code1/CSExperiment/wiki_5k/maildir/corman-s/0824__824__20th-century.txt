The '''20th century''' began on January 1, 1901 and ended on December 31, 2000. The 20th century saw two World War and a Cold War, the rise and fall of the Soviet Union, the coming of the telephone, Sound recording and reproduction, movies and television, Airplane, Nuclear weapon, genetics and DNA, Computer and electronics in general. Science and industrialisation spread; medicine became more scientific.
