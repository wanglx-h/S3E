Subject: Trilogy
A '''trilogy''' ('''threelogy''')is a set of three dramatic or literary works.. For example, The Godfather series of movies is a trilogy because there are three movies in the series: ''The Godfather'', ''The Godfather Part II'', and ''The Godfather Part III''. The trilogy was originally a tradition of Theatre of Ancient Greece, where three Play (theatre) and one satyr play would be played. Category:Literary terms
