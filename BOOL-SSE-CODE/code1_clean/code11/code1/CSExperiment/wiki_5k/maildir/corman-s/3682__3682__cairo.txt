The city name can be translated as ''the one who won''. Cairo has 7,947,121 people. About 17,290,000 people live in its urban area. This makes it the biggest city of the Arab World. It also is the city with the biggest urban area in Africa The city is on the Nile River. == Geography == === Climate === The city of Cairo has a Desert climate (BWh). It has a hot, sunny and dry climate all year long. The city, however, has more humidity than other cities with the hot desert climate (BWh). }} ==History== The area around the city was an important focal point of Ancient Egypt. In 968, the Fatimid Caliphate entered Egypt and they made Cairo the capital of their caliphate.Beeson, Irene.
