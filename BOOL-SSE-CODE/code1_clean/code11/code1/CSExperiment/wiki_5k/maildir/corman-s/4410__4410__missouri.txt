Its capital (city) is Jefferson City, Missouri. Its largest city are Kansas City, Missouri and Saint Louis, Missouri. Some other cities are Columbia, Missouri (which is where the University of Missouri is), and Springfield, Missouri. Missouri officially became a state on August 10, 1821. Missouri has also been called the "Mother of the West", the "Cave State", and the "Show Me State".
