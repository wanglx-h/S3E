Subject: Canada Dry
Image:Bottle of Canada Dry.JPG '''Canada Dry''' is a Trademark name belonging to a company that produces beverages. Canada Dry makes ginger ale, club soda, and tonic water. Ginger ale is flavored with ginger root. Club soda is a clear drink sometimes used to remove stains from clothing. Tonic water is often used to make an alcoholic beverage known as ''gin and tonic''.
