Subject: Triple jump
The '''triple jump''' (also known in certain countries as a '''''triple hop, step and jump''''' or '''''hop, skip and jump''''') is a Athletics (track and field) event. It has been a part of the World championships (track and field) since 1995. The event consists of someone running up to a line, jumping and landing on the same foot (a hop), jumping onto the other foot (a skip) and then jumping into a shallow pit of sand. The winner is the person whose final jump lands the farthest from the line they started jumping at. It is a common game played on playgrounds.This is a bit Similar for long jump.
