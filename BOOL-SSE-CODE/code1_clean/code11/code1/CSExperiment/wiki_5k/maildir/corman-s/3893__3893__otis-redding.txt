Subject: Otis Redding
File:Otis Redding 1967.JPG '''Otis Ray Redding Jr.''' (September 9, 1941 - December 10, 1967) was an Americans singer and songwriter. He was an African American who was born in Dawson, Georgia. He wrote and sang many popular songs. He was killed in a airplane accident at the age of 26 in Madison, Wisconsin. Shortly after his death, his song "Sittin' on the Dock of The Bay", went to # 1 on the charts. His music was magnificent and his songs moved many. He was a great man and a talented artist.
