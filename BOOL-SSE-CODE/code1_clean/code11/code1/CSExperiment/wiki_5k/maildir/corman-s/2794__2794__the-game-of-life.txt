Subject: The Game of Life
File:The Game of Life 人生ゲーム DSCF2280.jpg '''The Game of Life''' is a popular board game. Players Rotation a spinner, which tells them where to go next. They then go through an imaginary "life," getting married and having children. The object of the game is to have more money than the other players by the end of the game. Category:Board games
