Subject: Joan of Arcadia
'''''Joan of Arcadia''''' was a CBS drama television series. It starred Amber Tamblyn. Tamblyn's character was Joan Girardi, a teenager with the ability to talk to God. In its second season, the show had 8.5 million viewers per episode. However, after declining ratings the show was canceled. A third season was never made.
