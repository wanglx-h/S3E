File:Amy Grant October 2008.jpg '''Amy Lee Grant''' (November 25, 1960) is an Americans singer and songwriter. She is known for her Contemporary Christian music, as well as pop music hits. Grant is the best-selling contemporary Christian music singer ever. She released her first album in 1977 at the age of 16. ==Early life== Grant was born in Augusta, Georgia.Amy, Grant (2007).
