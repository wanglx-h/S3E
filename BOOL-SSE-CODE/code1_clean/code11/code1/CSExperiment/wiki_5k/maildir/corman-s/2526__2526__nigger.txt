'''Nigger''' is a racial slur used towards black people. It is often used by Racism. ==Origin== The word comes from a slang way to say "negro". In Spanish language and Portuguese language, “negro” means the color black and means black people. ==History== Before 1865, most black people in the United States were Slavery in the United States. In this time, “nigger” often meant a slave. Writers like Joseph Conrad, Mark Twain, and Charles Dickens used this word in their books.
