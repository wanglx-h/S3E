File:Wuerfel5.jpg '''Dice''' are wikt:object used in games when something random needs to be done, such as moving a game piece a random number of places on a board. Most dice are cubes that have the numbers 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6 on the faces. Dice are sometimes made in other polyhedron shapes than cubes, but the six-sided die (''die'' is the singular of "dice") is the most common. A die is usually rolled by the playerâ€™s hand. When adding together opposite sides of typical cubical dice, the sum is always 7. Some games are played with two or more dice and some use only one. Some games that use dice are Monopoly (game), Yahtzee, Risk (game), and many more. Weights can be put inside a die to make it only land on a certain number once it is rolled. This is sometimes used as a way of cheating. == Types of dice == File:6dice(cropped).jpg There are many different types of dice. Some dice have different numbers of sides. Instead, they have letters, pictures, or other symbols on them. Some games use these when they need something random that is not a number.
