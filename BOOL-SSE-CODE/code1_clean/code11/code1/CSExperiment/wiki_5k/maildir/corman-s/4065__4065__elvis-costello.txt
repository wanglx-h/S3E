| birth_place = London, England | Instrument = SingingGuitar | Genre = Rock musicPop musicNew wave musicPub rock (UK)Power pop | Occupation = Singer-songwriter | Years_active = 1977–present | Label = Stiff Records, Radar Records, F-Beat Records, Demon Records, Columbia Records, Warner Records, Deutsche Grammophon, Lost Highway Records | Associated_acts = The Attractions, The Imposters, Burt Bacharach, Allen Toussaint | URL = [http://elviscostello.com/ Elvis Costello.com] | Notable_instruments = }} '''Elvis Costello''' (born '''Declan Patrick MacManus''', 25 August 1954) is an English people singer and songwriter. He was born at St Mary’s Hospital in London. He was an important musician in the late 1970s punk rock and new wave music styles. In the 1980s, he played an original style of rock and roll. Later he wrote and performed music in a number of different styles from classical to jazz. Costello has won multiple awards in his career. These include a Grammy Award. He has twice been nominated for the Brit Award for Best British Male.[http://www.brits.co.uk/artist/elvis-costello Elvis Costello BRITS Profile] BRIT Awards Ltd In 2003, Elvis Costello & the Attractions were inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.[http://rockhall.com/inductees/elvis-costello-the-attractions/ Elvis Costello and the Attractions] Rockhall.com. In 2004, ''Rolling Stone'' ranked Costello number 80 on their list of the 100 Greatest Artists of All Time.[https://www.rollingstone.com/music/lists/100-greatest-artists-of-all-time-19691231/elvis-costello-19691231 100 Greatest Artists: Elvis Costello] Rolling Stone. After two earlier marriages he married jazz pianist Diana Krall in 2003. ==Acting== File:Elvis Costello 1978.jpg Costello appeared with his friend Burt Bacharach in the 1999 Austin Powers movie The Spy Who Shagged Me. They played "I'll Never Fall In Love Again" during the scene where Mike Myers danced with Heather Graham. In 2010, Elvis Costello appeared as himself in David Simon's television series, ''Treme (TV series)''. In 2011, Elvis Costello appeared as himself on Sesame Street.
