File:GSMT Co. Marines shave heads for cancer 140505-M-MM729-001.jpg '''Shaving''' is removing hair from the face or other body part with a razor. "Shaving" often refers to men who cut their Beard with a razor or Hair clipper. Sometimes they just trim their facial hair while other men completely shave it. Some people do not shave the chin, this is called a goatee beard. Other men do not shave the upper lip, this is called a mustache. In addition to this, many men choose to shave their heads and be Baldness. In many Army, male soldiers are required (have to) shave their heads. Category:Hygiene
