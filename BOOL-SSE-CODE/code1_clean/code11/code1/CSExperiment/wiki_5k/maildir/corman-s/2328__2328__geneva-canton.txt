The capital (city) of the canton is Geneva City. The French language name is ''Genève'', The German language name is ''Genf'', in Italian language it is ''Ginevra''. Geneva is the seat of many institutions of the United Nations. It has about 186.000 inhabitants and a very old university. About 960.000 people live in the urban area. Some of the urban area is in France. Only Zürich Canton has more inhabitants in Switzerland. File:Logo Genf.svg == History == In 1815, the city-state of '''Geneva''' became a part of Switzerland. It had at one time been the capital of the Kingdom of Burgundy. The first traces of human civilization in Geneva, discovered on the shores of Lake Geneva , date from around 3000 BC. The hill of the Old City, however, was not inhabited until 1000 BC. Later, when Rome conquered Geneva, it was defended by a small Celtic tribe. In 58 BC, Julius Caesar drove off an attack by the Helvetii. At the height of the Roman Empire, around 400 AD, Geneva was granted its own bishop. == Municipalities == Geneva does not have any districts.
