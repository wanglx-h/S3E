File:VH1_logonew.svg '''VH1 (Video Hits One)''' is an United States television network. It is a sister station to the groundbreaking MTV. VH1 shows reality shows and reruns of sitcoms, whereas MTV's focus is on the current hits and also reality shows. VH1 also shows ''You're Cut Off!'' on Mondays for 3 weeks and Wednesdays for 5 weeks.
