Subject: Sunset Boulevard (movie)
'''''Sunset Boulevard''''' is a 1950 American black comedy film noir movie about a screenwriter (William Holden) accompanying a retirement Hollywood silent movie actress (Gloria Swanson) who wants to return to acting in movies. It was nominated for eleven Academy Awards and won three.
