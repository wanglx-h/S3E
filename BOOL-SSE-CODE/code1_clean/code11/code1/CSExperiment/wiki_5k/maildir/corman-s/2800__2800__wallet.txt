File:Czech Wallet.jpg File:Rannelompakko.jpg A '''wallet''' is a small flat wikt:container, mostly of leather or fabric, that a person uses to hold cash, credit cards, identification cards, etc. Most men usually keep their wallets in their pockets, while women usually keep them in larger bags called wikt:purses. Wallets, particularly in Europe, where larger coins are prevalent, contain also a coin purse compartments. Some wallets have built-in clasps or bands to keep them closed. As European banknotes, such as Euros and Pound sterling, are typically larger than American banknotes in size, they do not fit in some smaller American wallets. A cryptocurrency wallet is a device, program, or service that stores public and/or private keys for cryptocurrency transactions.
