Subject: Serres
Image:Serres overview.jpg '''Serres''' is a Greek city. It is also one of 51 prefectures of Greece. There are 56,400 people in Serres. Serres is built on the large plain. It is in the north of Greece, in the region of Macedonia (Greece).
