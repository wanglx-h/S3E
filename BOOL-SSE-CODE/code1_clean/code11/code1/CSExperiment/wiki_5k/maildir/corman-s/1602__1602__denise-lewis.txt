Subject: Denise Lewis
File:Denise Lewis - Soccer Aid for Unicef 2025.jpg '''Denise Lewis''' (born 27 August 1972) is a British people retired Track and field athletics from West Bromwich. She won the bronze medal in the heptathlon at the 1996 Summer Olympics. She won the gold medal in the heptathlon at the 2000 Summer Olympics.
