Unlike a knife, scissors have two sharp edges. Some types of scissors, like children's scissors, are not very sharp. Children's scissors are often protected with plastic. These are called "Safety Scissors". Scissors can be made for left-handed or right-handed people. Using scissors made for the wrong hand is very hard for most people.
