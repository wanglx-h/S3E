Subject: Black Forest
Image:Schwarzwald - Deutsche Mittelgebirge, Serie A-de.png The '''Black Forest''' (German Language: Schwarzwald) is a world famous forest in Baden-Württemberg, Germany. Description, Map, & Facts|url=https://www.britannica.com/place/Black-Forest-mountain-region-Germany|access-date=2020-12-04|website=Encyclopedia Britannica|language=en}} Important towns are (from north to south): Pforzheim, Calw, Baden-Baden, Offenburg, Freudenstadt, Horb, Villingen-Schwenningen, Titisee-Neustadt and Waldshut-Tiengen. The highest mountain is the Feldberg (1492m). The Black Forest is quite popular for hiking, skiing and Nordic walking. In Triberg, at the lake '''Titisee''' and in other places you can buy the famous cuckoo clocks. A good way to get known to the Black Forest is to travel on the ''Deutsche Uhrenstraße,'' a "National Scenic Byway." ==References== ==Other websites== * [http://www.schwarzwald-tourismus.info Official website] * [http://www.badische-seiten.de/schwarzwald/bilder.php Black Forest image galleries] * [http://www.schwarzwald.de Information about Black Forest, Hotels, Wellness and more] Category:Forests of Germany Category:Geography of Baden-Württemberg
