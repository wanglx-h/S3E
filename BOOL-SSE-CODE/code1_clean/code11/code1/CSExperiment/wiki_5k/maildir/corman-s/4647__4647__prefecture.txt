Subject: Prefecture
File:Prefectures of Greece.png.]] A '''prefecture''' is a word used in Greece and Japan (called ''νομός'', ''nomós'' in Greece and called '''県''' Prefectures of Japan) for a part of a country with its own government. It is a kind of local government. It is like an American U.S. state or a British county. In France, it is the head of the Departments of France (''see'' Prefectures in France ''for more detail'').
