Subject: Hoe (tool)
File:Peasant in the vegetable garden.JPG A '''hoe''' is a tool in agriculture and gardening. It has a blade, usually metal, attached to a long handle, usually wood. There are many types of blade and several uses, probably the most common of which is weeding. Other uses include shaping the soil and harvesting root crops such as potatoes. Along with the spade and Garden fork, the hoe is considered a basic, essential hand farming implement. Category:Gardening tools
