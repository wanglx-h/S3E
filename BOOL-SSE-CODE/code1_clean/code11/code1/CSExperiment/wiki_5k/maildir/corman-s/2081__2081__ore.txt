Subject: Ore
An '''ore''' is a mineral which has a valuable metal inside it. Ores are usually extracted by being mining. They can sometimes be found on the Earth's surface. Most ores are chemical compounds such as oxides or sulfides. There are different ways to get the metal out of the ore, which is called smelting. Iron ore, for example, is smelted in a blast furnace.
