Subject: Wendy Carlos
File:Wendy Carlos HS Project.jpeg '''Wendy Carlos''' is an United States composer and musician. She was born in Pawtucket, Rhode Island, Rhode Island. She is known as one of the first people to use Electricity Musical instrument to make music. In 1968, she released her music album "Switched on Bach". This album used many new electric instruments including the Moog Synthesizer. It won three Grammy Awards. She also did the music for movies. These include ''A Clockwork Orange (movie)'', ''The Shining (movie)'', and ''Tron''.
