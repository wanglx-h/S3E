Subject: Drink
File:USMC-01617.jpg drinking water. ]] A '''drink''' or '''beverage''' is a liquid that an organism can take into their body, by using their mouth. Typical drinks for humans include water, tea, milk, coffee, juice, soft drinks and alcohol drinks. All drinks are mainly water. All living things need water to live. If we don't get enough water, we will Dehydration, and likely Death. Plants take in water through their roots, which are underground. Most Terrestrial animal get water by drinking. == Places that have no water == Desert are places that have very little water. The only area in the desert that has water is an oasis.
