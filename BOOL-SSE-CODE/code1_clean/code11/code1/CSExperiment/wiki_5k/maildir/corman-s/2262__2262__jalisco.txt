Subject: Jalisco
File:Flag of Jalisco.svg File:Coat of arms of Jalisco.svg '''Jalisco''' is a state located in west Mexico. The Capital (city) of Jalisco is Guadalajara. It is next to the Pacific Ocean and the states of Nayarit, Zacatecas, Aguascalientes, San Luis Potosí, Guanajuato, and Michoacán. It also has half of the Chapala Lake. Category:Jalisco
