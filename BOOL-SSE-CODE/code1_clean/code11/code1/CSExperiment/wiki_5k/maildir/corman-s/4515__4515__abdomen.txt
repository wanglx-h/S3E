Subject: Abdomen
File:Anatomy Abdomen Tiesworks.jpg The '''abdomen''' is the part of the body that is between the chest and the thigh. It contains (has in it) the rectus abdominis muscle and many Organ (anatomy). Some of the organs in the abdomen are: * Gallbladder * Liver * Rectum * Stomach * Anus * Small intestine and large intestine * Ureters * Bladder * Urethra * Reproductive system except breast * Appendix Category:Torso
