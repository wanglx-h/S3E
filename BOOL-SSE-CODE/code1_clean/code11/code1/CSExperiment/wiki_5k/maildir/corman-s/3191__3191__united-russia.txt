Subject: United Russia
File:Election russia 2007 002.jpg '''United Russia''' () is a Russian political party, which usually labels itself centrist and conservationist. Former President and Prime Minister Dmitry Medvedev is leader of this party. Incumbent President Vladimir Putin, though not a member of the party, is its de-facto Leader. The party has most of the seats in the State Duma and in the Composition of the Russian Regional Parliaments.
