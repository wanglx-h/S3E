Subject: Gary Burghoff
File:GaryBurghoff03.jpg '''Gary Burghoff''' (born May 24, 1943) is an Americans actor, artist, and musician. He is probably best known for his role as Walter "Radar" O'Reilly, a company clerk that had extraordinary hearing, in the 1970 movie ''M*A*S*H'' and on the television programme of the same name.
