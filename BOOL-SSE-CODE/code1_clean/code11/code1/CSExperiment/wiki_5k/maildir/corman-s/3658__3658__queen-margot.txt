Subject: Queen Margot
'''''Queen Margot''''' is a book written in 1845 by Alexandre Dumas, père. It is a story about Margaret of Valois and Henry IV of France. The same writer wrote many other books including ''The Man in the Iron Mask'', ''The Count of Monte Cristo'' and ''The Three Musketeers''.
