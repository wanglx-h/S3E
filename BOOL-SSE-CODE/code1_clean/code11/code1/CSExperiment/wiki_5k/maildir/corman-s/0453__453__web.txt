'''Web''' can mean several things * Spider webs are weaving traps usually used by spiders to catch insects * Web is a common short name for the World Wide Web * Web is a word for the Tissue between fingers and toes, such as the webbed feet of ducks and other waterfowl, or material with a similar purpose, such as between the thumb and finger of a baseball glove
