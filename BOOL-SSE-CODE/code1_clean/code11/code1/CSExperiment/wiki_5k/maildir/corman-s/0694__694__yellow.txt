Inkjet printers use yellow ink as one of their three primary colors (basic colors used to make the other colors), along with cyan ink and magenta ink. '''Yellow''' is the color of: * The color Amber (color). * The outer skin of a mango. * The flower of the dandelion. * The flower of the sunflower. * The flower of the marigold. * The flower of the mustard. * The fruit of the lemon. * The peel of a banana. * The base of the pineapple (mostly). * The middle light of a stoplight. * The colour of the school bus (mostly). == Meaning of yellow == * Yellow can represent light and the sun's rays as well as lightning. * The phrase "yellow" used to be used as a sign of cowardice. * In China, yellow is the color of royalty.
