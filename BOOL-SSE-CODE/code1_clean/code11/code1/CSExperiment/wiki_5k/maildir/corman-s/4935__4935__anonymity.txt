Subject: Anonymity
File:Anonymous emblem.svg emblem ]] '''Anonymity''' means "namelessness" and comes from Greek language words meaning "without name". It is when a person's identity or name is unknown. If a person is called '''anonymous''', nobody knows who he or she is, or the anonymous person does not want to be found out. File:Anonymous Scientology 1 by David Shankbone.JPG calling itself 'Anonymous' against the practices and tax status of the Scientology.]] The concept of anonymity has some important uses. It is vital for a democracy that votes cast in an election are anonymous. In that way, no-one can find out how a person voted in an election. Category:Names Category:Privacy
