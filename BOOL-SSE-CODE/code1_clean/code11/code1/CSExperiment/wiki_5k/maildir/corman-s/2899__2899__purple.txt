The first written use of ''purple'' as a color name in English language was in 975.Maerz and Paul ''A Dictionary of Color'' New York:1930 McGraw-Hill Page 202 == Meaning of purple == * Purple is associated with wisdom, dignity, independence, creativity, wikt:mystery and witchcraft. Purple is a very rare color in nature, though the lavender flower and catmint are two examples. * Tyrian Purple is used to represent Royal family. It is a sign of Influence, nobility, luxury, and ambition. It shows wealth and Eccentricity. It is thought as a royal color because in Ancient Rome times, there was only one way to make the color purple, which the Romans called ''purpura''. They used one or two species of the gastropod ''Murex'', which produced the dye in a gland. These tiny shellfish lived in the Mediterranean Sea.Interscience. Purple dyes made from shellfish in antiquity Each snail produced only tiny amounts, so it was very expensive. Therefore, purple was only worn by the rich and famous. Naturally, the richest and most famous people in the country were the Roman Empire, so they were able to wear the most purple.Marginal Revolution, Royal Purple Roman sumptuary laws forbade plebeians to wear it. * Light purple is a good choice for a female design. It creates feelings of Love. Bright purple can be used when advertisement children's things. * Dark purple may create sad feelings.
