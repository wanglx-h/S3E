Subject: Bull
A '''bull''' is the adult male member of the species Cattle. It is in the Bovid family (Cattle, Domestic goat, Domestic sheep). They are Muscle and Aggression like a cow, who is the adult female member of the species ''Bos taurus''. This animal is also classified as a Pet and a Livestock. A bull is Halal. File:Taureau charolais au pré.jpg The bull has long been an important symbol in many Culture, and plays a large role in beef Ranch, dairy farming. They are also important in other cultural activities, including bullfighting and bull riding. The management of cattle in hot and cold climates is a popular thing. The physiological and behavioral changes induced in these animals compromise their welfare, Breed, health, zootechnical performance, resulting in final products (Gamete, milk, meat) with unsatisfactory characteristics. As with other Animal, some bulls have been kept as pet animals. ==Life== The bull semen market is very lucrative. Animal agriculture forcefully breeds animals to have what it considers the best traits for food production. In 2011, the U.S. exported $124.7 million worth of bull semen to other nations, representing 32 percent of the global market. In February 2019, an Angus bull named SAV America 8018 sold for the record-breaking price of $1.5 million due to “superior genetics.” A bull can live up to 18 - 22 years. ==References== Category:Cattle
