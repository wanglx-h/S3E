Subject: Mars (mythology)
File:Statue of God Mars York.jpg '''Mars''' is the god of war in Roman mythology. Mars is the son of Juno (mythology) and Jupiter. Mars is the father of Romulus and Remus, and also Cupid. The planet Mars (planet) and the month March are named after him. He was the god of war, murder, and bloodshed. His Greek name is Ares. He tried many times to start wars between the gods. ==Related pages== * Ares - Greek mythology version of Mars * Tyr - Norse mythology version of Mars Category:Roman gods and goddesses
