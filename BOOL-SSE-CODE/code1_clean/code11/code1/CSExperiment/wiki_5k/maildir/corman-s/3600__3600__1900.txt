'''1900''' (Roman numerals) was an Century common year Common year starting on Monday in the Gregorian calendar and a leap year starting on Saturday in the Julian calendar. It was last year of the 19th century. The Gregorian calendar was 12 days ahead of the Julian calendar as of the start of the year. The Julian calendar observed a leap day this year; this made the Gregorian calendar 13 days ahead.
