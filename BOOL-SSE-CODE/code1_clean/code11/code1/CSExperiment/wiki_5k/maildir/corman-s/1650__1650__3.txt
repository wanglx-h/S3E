The year '''3''' ('''Roman numerals''') was a common year which started on a common year starting on Monday. according to the Julian calendar. According to the Gregorian calendar, it started on a common year starting on Wednesday. At the time, it was known as the ''Year of the Roman consul of Lamia and Servilius''. The year is called 3 since the early medieval period, when the Anno Domini calendar era became the usual method in Europe for naming years.
