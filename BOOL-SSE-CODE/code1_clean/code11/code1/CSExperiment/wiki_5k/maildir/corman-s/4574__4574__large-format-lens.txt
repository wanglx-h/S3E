Subject: Large format lens
A '''large format lens''' is a Lens (optics) that can be used on a large format camera. These cameras usually cover an area of 4x5 inches or more. The picture taken is then recorded on photographic film, or as a digital image. == Other websites == * http://www.schneideroptics.com/info/vintage_lens_data/large_format_lenses/ * http://www.kenrockwell.com/tech/4x5.htm Category:Camera lenses
