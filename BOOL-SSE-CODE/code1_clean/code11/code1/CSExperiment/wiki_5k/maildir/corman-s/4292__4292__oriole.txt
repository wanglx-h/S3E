Subject: Oriole
The '''oriole''' ( or Old World oriole) is a bird in the Family (biology) '''Oriolidae'''. They make up the genus '''''Oriolus'''''. Old World orioles are found areas of Africa, Asia and Europe. They are most often found in Tropics areas but one species lives in cooler areas. The golden oriole is the only species of Old World oriole which is not a tropical bird. Many species have bright coloured feathers. The orioles evolved in Australasia, and later spread to Eurasia. They are not related to the New World oriole.
