File:Stargard_Collage.jpg Image:POL Stargard Szczeciński flag.svg Image:POL Stargard COA.svg '''Stargard Szczeciński''' () is a town in Pomerania, northwestern Poland. It has 71,400 inhabitants (2003). It is on the Ina river. It is the capital of Stargard County in West Pomeranian Voivodship. ==Other websites== Category:Towns in Pomeranian Voivodeship Category:Hanseatic League Category:Pomerania
