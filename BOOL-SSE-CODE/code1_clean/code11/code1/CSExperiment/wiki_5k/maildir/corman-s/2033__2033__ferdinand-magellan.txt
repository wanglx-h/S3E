Records disagree about whether he was born in Sabrosa or Porto in Portugal.Daniel Rogers, Magellan and da gama Magellan's parents were members of the Portuguese nobility. As a result, after they died, Magellan was able to get a job in the royal court. At age 10, he became a Page boy for the Queen of Portugal. In the royal court, he learned many important skills, including Cartography, navigation and astronomy. == Accomplishments == In 1505 Magellan joined the Portuguese navy. He traveled to many parts of the world, such as India, the Spice Islands, East Africa, and Morocco. He was wounded in Morocco in 1513, and walked with a limp for the rest of his life. After he lost his job for the King of Portugal, Magellan went to work for the Monarchy of Spain, Charles V of SpainCharles V gave Magellan five ships for his voyage around the world. Only one ship, the ''Victoria'', completed the voyage. In 1520, during his circumnavigation voyage, Magellan became the first European to sail through the Strait of Magellan (which was then named for him). == Death == Magellan was killed in Mactan, Philippines on April 27, 1521, aged 40-41. He was accused of stealing from the Filipinos, which led to a battle.
