Subject: Direction
File:Cute young afro american boy child.jpg '''Direction''' follows an imagination line connecting two points. Something following the line is moving in that ''direction''. People show directions by pointing. The word ''direction'' is made from the root ''direct'' meaning to guide. These are words used to talk about a '''direction''': * Up * Down * Backward * Left * Position * Forward * Northeast * South * Southeast * East * Northeast * West ==Related pages== * Compass * Navigation Category:Basic English 850 words
