'''''Gilligan's Island''''' is a 1964 United States television series. The series is about seven people (two crew members and five tourism) who take a 3-hour sightseeing tour from a harbor in Hawaii. This occurs on the boat, S.S. ''Minnow''. An unexpected storm sends the boat to an uncharted island. Now, the passengers of the boat have to live on the island and try to find a way home.
