Subject: The Blues Image
'''The Blues Image''' was a late 1960s rock and roll band formed in Tampa, Florida in 1966, and their only hit was the song "Ride, Captain, Ride". Members are singer and guitarist Michael Pinera, drummer Manuel Bertematti, and drummer Joe Lala.
