Subject: Huntz Hall
'''Henry "Huntz" Hall''' (August 15, 1919 - January 30, 1999) was a well-known United States actor. He was best known as Dippy in the Dead End Kids series. He made 78 movies. == Trivia == *He is one of the famous people who appeared on the cover of The Beatles, Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band album.
