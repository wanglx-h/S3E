Subject: Shepton Mallet
'''Shepton Mallet''' is a town in the county of Somerset, England. It is in the Mendip district. From 1185 to April 2023 Shepton Mallet was where Mendip District Council was based for the Mendip district. In April 2023, it was abolished and replaced by Somerset Council. It is a pleasant place to go, and has a beautiful countryside. Category:Towns in Somerset Category:Mendip
