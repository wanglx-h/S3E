Subject: Isabelle of Hainaut
File:IsabelladeHainault.jpg '''Isabelle of Hainaut''' (5 April, 1170 - 15 March, 1190) was a Queen of France. She was born in Lille on 5 April 1170. Her father was Baldwin V of Hainaut and her mother was Margaret I of Flanders. She married Philip II of France in 1180.
