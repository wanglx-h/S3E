The population was about 23,000 in 2023. All 24 German submarines are stationed in Eckernförde. Eckernförde is a well-known tourism centre in Northern Germany. == History == * In 1302 Eckernförde is first mentioned as a city. * The Count Saint-Germain is supposed to be buried in Eckernförde near the St.-Nicolai-Church (building).
