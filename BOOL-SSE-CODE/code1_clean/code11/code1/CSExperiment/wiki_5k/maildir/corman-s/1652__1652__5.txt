'''5''' (Roman numerals) is a common year of the Julian calendar which started on a common year starting on Thursday. According to the Gregorian calendar, it started on a common year starting on Saturday. It was known as the ''Year of the Roman consul of Messalla and Cinna''. It was the 5th year of the 1st century. It is one of only seven years to use just one Roman numeral.
