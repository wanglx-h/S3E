File:Logo-notruf-112-europaweit.svg File:Centrum Powiadamiania Ratunkowego w Krakowie 4.JPG The telephone number '''1-1-2''' (or '''112''') is the standard European Union (EU) emergency telephone number. It works in every country of the EU, for land lines and mobile phones. It is also used in some other countries as an emergency telephone number for both mobile and fixed-line telephones. 112 is also the international emergency telephone number for all Global System for Mobile Communications mobile phone networks.
