|synonyms = }} |synonyms_ref = }} File:Hordeum vulgare MHNT.BOT.2015.2.39.jpg '''Barley''' is a member of the grass family of plants. Its seeds are a cereal grain. It is a staple food of humans and other animals, and it is one of mankind's oldest crops. It can be made into flour or beer. Its Latin language name is ''Hordeum vulgare''.
