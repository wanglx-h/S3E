Subject: Arachnology
'''Arachnology''' is a science that studies arachnids. Examples of arachnids are spiders, scorpions, Pseudoscorpionida, and Opiliones. Ticks and mites are also arachnids but they sometimes are not included in arachnology. They are studied in acarology. == Related pages == *Entomology Category:Branches of zoology
