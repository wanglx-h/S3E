It was created in 1939 by Bill Hewlett and Dave Packard, in a garage in California. It is based in Palo Alto, California. They are best known for making computers and computer hardware such as inkjet computer printer, laser printers, all-in-one printers, Image scanners and digital cameras. In 2002, HP bought Compaq and re-branded together as one company that is stylized as ''hp''.
