Subject: Houseplant
A '''houseplant''' is any kind of decorative plant. People grow them indoors, sometimes in a house, but very often in an office or a shop. Houses or public premises have conditions not as favorable for plants as in greenhouses. For example, the direction of light, low light, dry air, dust, drafts, uneven temperature. So, special techniques are used to get a healthy state and proper development of plants.Indoor Plants Kentucky Master Gardener Manual Chapter 14. [http://www2.ca.uky.edu/agcomm/pubs/ho/ho103/ho103.pdf] Conditions in public and office premises are usually even less favorable than in homes, so the most hardy indoor plants are grown there. == Benefits of houseplants == === Feel and observe === One obvious advantage of keeping houseplants is the improvement in aesthetic quality that they provide. Some large Palm (unit) varieties, can serve as a focus point for guests. === Condition of the air === Houseplant may enhance Indoors air quality. That is a key perk. NASA conducted a study to identify which plants are most effective at filtering out harmful Chemical substance. === Health === Taking care of plants, like taking care of people or animals, can have a positive effect on one's mental and Emotional intelligence health. === Therapeutic, preventative, and cosmetic === Plants and herbs with medical characteristics, such as Aloe vera, are grown for human consumption. == Well-known houseplants == Image:Cyclamen sp1.jpg *Saintpaulia *Aloe *Bromeliad *Butterfly palm *Cactus *Cyclamen *Elephant leaf plant *Golden pothos *Mother-in-law's tongue *Snake plant *Spider plant *Poinsettia *Prayer plant *Umbrella tree *Weeping fig *Yucca == References == Category:Plants
