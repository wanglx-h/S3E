A '''symbol''' is a Painting, shape, or an object that Representation an idea, object, or amount of something. ==Overview== The most common symbols are Letter, which are symbols of Word and Sound. A symbol can be an actual object (such as the cross, a symbol of Christianity or a scepter, a symbol of royalty and Influence), or a certain color or pattern. ==Purposes== Symbols are used often in poetry and other types of literature, sometimes as Metaphor or Smile. A national emblem is a symbol for a certain country. ==List of common symbols== There are thousands of symbols that are recognized by most people all over the world, and many more that are limited to certain regions, Religion and Science.
