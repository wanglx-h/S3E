File:Flag of Scotland.svg has a blue background]] File:The Earth seen from Apollo 17.jpg of Earth, taken by NASA's Apollo program 17]] File:Trees rising out of Cheow Lan Lake, blue sky, eternal summer in Surat Thani.jpg]] '''Blue''' is one of the colors of the rainbow that people can see. It is one of the primary colors (colors that can be mixed with other colors) of light, along with red and green. Apart from indigo and violet, it has the shortest wavelength of colors in the rainbow (about 470 nanometers). Blue paint can be made by mixing magenta and cyan paint. Blue is the color of the Earth's sky and sea. Earth looks blue when seen from outer space by astronauts. It is the color of cold, Extraversion and introversion and openness, by opposition to Red, which is the color of warm, Extraversion and introversion and the closure. In alchemy, blue is also the color of the Extraversion and introversion (blue fire), by opposition to Red, which is the color of the Extraversion and introversion (red fire). Blue is usually associated with masculinity, harmony, conservatism, liberalism, the cold, sadness, calmness and royalty. == Meaning of blue == The color blue is sometimes associated with sadness, which may be where the name of the music style Blues comes from. Blue is a color of the Jewish religion. In Western cultures since the 1940s, blue is associated with men/boys, while pink is associated with women/girls. Before the 1940s, it was the other way around in some countries, so blue was for women/girls and pink was for men/boys.
