Subject: Jamie Farr
File:JamieFarrHWOFSept2012.jpg '''Jamie Farr''' (born July 1, 1934) is an Americans actor of Lebanon descent. He is best known for his role as Corp./later Sgt. Maxwell Q. Klinger on the 1970s TV show, ''M*A*S*H''. His real name is '''Jameel Joseph Farha''' and he was born in Toledo, Ohio. Farr also appeared in movies like ''The Blackboard Jungle'' (1955) and ''The Cannonball Run'' (1980), was a regular "celebrity judge" on ''The Gong Show'' and has a golf tournament named after him. ==References== Category:1934 births Category:Living people Category:Actors from Toledo, Ohio Category:American movie actors Category:American television actors
