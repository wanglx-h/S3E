File:M cursiva.gif '''M''' is the 13 (number) letter#Letters of the alphabet in the English language alphabet. == Meanings for M == * In calendars, M sometimes means Monday or means one of the months "March" or "May". * In currency, M means million, such as $25M (25 (number) million dollars). * In the International System of Units, m is the symbol for the metre. * In Roman numerals, M means 1000 (number). * '''''M''''' is also the name of a 1930 Germany movie made by Fritz Lang and its 1951 remake. ==Other websites==
