Subject: Jurassic Park III
'''''Jurassic Park III''''' is a 2001 United States movie and the third ''Jurassic Park'' movie. It follows ''The Lost World: Jurassic Park''. Paul Kirby, who says he is a rich business owner, convinces Coop Burtonburger and his assistant, Mister Brennigan, to take him and his wife to Isla Sorna as a vacation and serve as a guide. Actually, Kirby and his wife want to search for their son, who was stranded on Isla Sorna while on a parasailing trip. After crashing on the island, they are attacked by a ''Spinosaurus'', who kills several members of their party. After escaping, the remaining people start hiking for the coast, fighting many dinosaurs, including the ''Spinosaurus'', along the way.
