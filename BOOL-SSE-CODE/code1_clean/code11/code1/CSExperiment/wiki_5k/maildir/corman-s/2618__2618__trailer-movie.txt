Mankiewicz.]] A '''movie trailer''' (also called a '''preview''' or '''coming attraction''') is a short showing of a future (not yet released) movie. They are shown in a theater before the current movie starts. They are often later shown in advertisements for DVD releases, and broadcasts of the movie on television. Category:Movie terminology
