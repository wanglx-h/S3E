J. Heinz Company. He was a member of the United States House of Representatives from 1971 to 1977 and was a US United States Senate from 1977 to 1991. He was killed in a plane crash in 1991. Heinz married Teresa Heinz on February 5, 1966 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. The couple had three sons together: H. John Heinz IV, André, and Christopher.
