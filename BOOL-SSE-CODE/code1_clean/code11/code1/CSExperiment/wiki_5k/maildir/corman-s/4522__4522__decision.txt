Subject: Decision
The word '''Decision''' can refer to different concepts: == General == * When there are several options on what to do, Choice is called 'making a decision'. Humans and other animals make decisions almost all the time. * A field of mathematics, and economics, that looks at this process is called decision theory. == Law == * A decision is also the result of a legal case. ''The decision in Brown v. Board of Education was to end segregation.'' == Sports == * A decision is also the result of a sports contest. ''The team lost three decisions in a row''. * A decision in Boxing is when a referee (official) decides who wins when the one boxer does not win by knockout (hitting the other until he loses consciousness.)
