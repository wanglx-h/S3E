Subject: Palo Alto, California
Image:Flag of Palo Alto, California.svg Image:Seal of Palo Alto, California.svg '''Palo Alto''' is a city in the United States U.S. state of California in Santa Clara County, California that has a population of about 65,000 people. It is the birthplace of the region called Silicon Valley and includes Stanford University. It is the home of many innovators and entrepreneurs. "Palo Alto" means "tall stick" in Spanish language after a famous redwood, El Palo Alto, along the banks of San Francisquito Creek. The tree was struck by lightning in 1955 but much of it remains standing. The tree is over 1,080 years old. A diverse population thrives in a city known for its schools, parks, libraries, restaurants and mild climate. Palo Alto is where Apple CEO, Steve Jobs died on October 5, 2011.
