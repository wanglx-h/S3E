| birth_place = Baltimore, Maryland, United States | website = }} '''Philip Morris Glass''' (born January 31, 1937) is an Americans composer of modernism music. He is one of the most influential music makers of the late 20th century.Naxos Classical Music Spotlight podcast: Philip Glass Heroes Symphony Glass has created operas, music for quartets, dance, and much more. He is known for writing minimalism music. Glass has described himself instead as a composer of "music with repetitive structures".
