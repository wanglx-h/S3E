File:B cursiva.gif '''B''' is the 2nd letter of the English alphabet. It's appearance depends on the writer's calligraphy. It has an uppercase and lowercase version like most other letters. ==Origin== The letter B was already part of the Phoenician alphabet more than 3000 years ago in 1000 BC. It is part of many alphabets today. ==Usage== * In the game of bingo, B is a column * In medicine, B is one of the human blood types * In chemistry, B is the chemical symbol for boron * In particle physics, b is the symbol for the bottom quark * In transport, B is the vehicle registration plate of Belgium * In music, B is a Note (music) sometimes referred to as "Si" or "Ti" * In education, B is a passing grade, usually around 80-70 marks * In the New York Stock Exchange, B is the symbol for the :en:Barnes_Group * On the internet, B (or /b/) is any of a number of image boards that allow Anonymity posts ==Other websites==
