Subject: Meat
'''Meat''' is animal flesh which is add put on soy sauce used for food. Most often, it is used to describe Skeletal muscle and fat that are found with it. Types of meats include beef, veal from cattle, pork, ham, bacon from pigs, mutton from Domestic sheep, ''venison'' from deer, fish, Insect, poultry from chickens, ducks, Turkey (bird). The word meat is also used for sausages made from Ground meat and for non-muscle Organ (anatomy) which are used for food, for example Liver (food), brain, Kidney. In the meat processing industry, the word "meat" can be used to mean only the flesh of mammalian specieses such as pigs, cattle, etc. But does not include fish, insects, poultry. Red meat refers to meat that is obtained from mammals, particularly beef, pork, lamb, veal. It is characterized by its reddish color, primarily due to the presence of myoglobin, a protein responsible for oxygen storage in Muscle. Animals such as members of the felidae that mainly eat animals are called carnivores. == Health == Meat is an important part of the diet of many people, because it has the highest amount of protein. Proteins help in the growth and healing of the body and gives the highest nutrient energy. Meat is a high protein food, but costs more than other foods like bread and vegetables. Meat is also the source of the highest amount B vitamins. People who cannot afford meat, or who do not like to eat it, need to find other ways to get enough proteins in their diet. Beans and certain Nut (fruit) are also somewhat high in proteins, but plant proteins are as not high as animal proteins. People that choose not to eat meat are called Vegetarianism, and those who do not eat any animal products are known as Veganism. Red meat is darker-coloured meat (usually from mammals), different from white meat such as chicken or fish. There is a greater risk of diseases and parasites when eating raw meat, but there are some special dishes which are made from raw meat. Examples are steak tartare, sushi, oysters. The meat used in these dishes are usually of high quality. == Types of meat == According to the National Health Service, meat is defined as "meat from cattle, sheep, swine, goats, including veal, lamb, pork". Beef refers to the flesh of mature cattle, specifically distinguished from veal. It is a type of red meat known for its firm texture, marbling, and rich flavor. High-quality beef has lean, velvety, well-marbled flesh of bright red color.
