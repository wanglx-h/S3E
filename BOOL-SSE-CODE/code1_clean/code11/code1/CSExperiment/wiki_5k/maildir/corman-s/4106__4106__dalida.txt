Subject: Dalida
File:Dalida 1960s.jpg '''Dalida''' (real name: '''Iolanda Cristina Gigliotti''') (January 17, 1933 - May 3, 1987) was an Egyptian-born Italians/French people biggest international singing icon and actor. She was born in Cairo to an Italian family. She had Italian citizenship all her life. From 1961, she also had French citizenship. She died in 1987 by suicide.
