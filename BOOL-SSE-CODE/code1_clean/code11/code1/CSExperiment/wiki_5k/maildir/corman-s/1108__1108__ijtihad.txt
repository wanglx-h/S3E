Subject: Ijtihad
File:Grand Ayatollahs Qom فتوکلاژ، آیت الله های ایران-قم 02.jpg '''Ijtihad''' () is the process of reaching a legal decision on the basis of one's own interpretation of Islamic law. The word is related to the better-known ''jihad''. Ijtihad appeared among the Muhammad Sahaba. Category:Islamic law
