Subject: Derivation
A '''derivative''' is something created from a primary source, which can mean different things. * Derivative (finance), or derivative security * Derivative (mathematics), a central idea of calculus *Derivative works made from an existing work of art * Derivative, a word that is made from a more basic word * Derivative, a substance that has similar properties to the original. However, one or more components of the original are replaced
