File:Z cursiva.gif '''Z''' is the twenty-sixth (26 (number)) and last letter in the English language alphabet. The small letter, '''z''' or '''ᴣ''', is used as a lowercase consonant. Z is not used much. It is the most rarely used letter in the English language. The same letter of the Greek alphabet is named zeta (letter). == Pronunciation == In British English, the letter is Pronunciation '''zed''' . This comes from the Greek alphabet ''zeta (letter)''.
