File:Teresa Heinz Kerry 2013.jpg '''Teresa Heinz Kerry''' (born October 5, 1938) is a Portugal-Americans businesswoman and philanthropist. She was born in Maputo, Mozambique to Portuguese parents. She is the wife of United States United States Senate John Kerry. She was previously married to former U.S. senator H. John Heinz III, until his death. She is a naturalized American citizen.
