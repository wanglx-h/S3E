Subject: Poznań
File:Ratusz Poznan od Wielkiej.jpg Image:POL_Poznań_flag.svg]] Image:POL Poznań COA.svg]] '''Poznań''' (German name: ''Posen'') is the biggest city and capital of Greater Poland Voivodeship in Poland, one of the oldest and the biggest cities in Poland. Its population is about 560,000 and its area is 262 square kilometers. Poznań was one of the 4 Polish cities where the UEFA Euro 2012 matches were played in 2012. Poland co-hosted the championship together with Ukraine. Category:Poznań
