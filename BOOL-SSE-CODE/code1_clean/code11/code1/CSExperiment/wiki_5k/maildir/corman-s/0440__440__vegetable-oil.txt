This meal is mostly prepared during festive seasons like new yam festival or marriage ceremony.jpg File:Italian olive oil 2007.jpg is a common vegetable oil, used for Mediterranean cuisine.]] '''Vegetable oils''' are triglyceride oils made from plants. They are used in food and for cooking. In past centuries they were much used as fuel in oil lamps. Some kinds of plant oils that people use are palm oil, maize, Olive (fruit), peanut, rapeseed, soy bean, and sunflower.
