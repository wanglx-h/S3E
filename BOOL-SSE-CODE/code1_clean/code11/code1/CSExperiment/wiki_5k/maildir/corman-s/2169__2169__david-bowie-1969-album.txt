Subject: David Bowie (1969 album)
'''''David Bowie''''' (also known as ''Space Oddity'') is the second Album by David Bowie. Philips Records released the album on 14 November 1969. RCA Records released it again when Bowie became popular in 1972. Bowie released "Space Oddity" as a single in July 1969.
