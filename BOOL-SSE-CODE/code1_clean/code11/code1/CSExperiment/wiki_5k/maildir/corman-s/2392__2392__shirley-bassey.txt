File:Shirley Bassey à Munster ( Allemagne ) début des années 70.jpeg '''Dame Shirley Veronica Bassey''', Order of the British Empire (born 8 January 1937) is a Welsh people singer, born in Cardiff to an English people mother and Nigerian father. She is best known for singing the theme songs for the James Bond films, ''Goldfinger'', ''Diamonds are Forever'', and ''Moonraker''.[ Allmusic bio] She was "one of the most popular female vocalists in Britain during the last half of the 20th century".
