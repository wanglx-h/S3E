She won two Academy Awards for her role in the movie ''Cold Mountain (movie)'' and as Judy Garland in ''Judy (movie)''. She has acted in many movies, including ''Jerry Maguire'', ''Bridget Jones's Diary (movie)'', ''Chicago (2002 movie)'' and ''Cinderella Man''. ==Early life== Zellweger was born in Katy, Texas. Her father, Emil Erich Zellweger, is from a small town in the cantons of Switzerland of Canton of St. Gallen, Switzerland, and is a mechanical and electrical engineer who worked in the oil refining business.[http://www.filmreference.com/film/1/Renee-Zellweger.html "Renee Zellweger Biography (1969–)."] Film Reference.com.
