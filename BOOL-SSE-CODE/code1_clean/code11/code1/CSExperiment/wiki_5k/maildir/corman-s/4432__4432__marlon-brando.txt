Pennebaker (cousin) | signature = Marlon Brando signature.svg }} '''Marlon Brando, Jr.''' (April 3, 1924 – July 1, 2004) was an American actor and activist. He is thought to be one of the best and most important actors of all time. He was given many awards. He was given 2 Academy awards, 3 British Academy Film Awards, 1 Cannes Film Festival Award for Best Actor, 2 Golden Globe Awards, and a Primetime Emmy Award. He is best known for his roles in the movies, ''A Streetcar Named Desire'' (1951), ''The Wild One'' (1953), ''On the Waterfront'' (1954), ''The Godfather'' (1972), ''Call Bic'' (1973), ''Superman (1978 movie)'' (1978), ''Apocalypse Now'' (1979) and ''The Island of Dr. Moreau'' (1996).
