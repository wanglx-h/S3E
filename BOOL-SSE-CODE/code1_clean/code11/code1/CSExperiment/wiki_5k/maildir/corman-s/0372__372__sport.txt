In this image, a striker is shooting the ball to score a goal.]] Image:4 sports photo.jpg Image:Young child football.jpg Image:Sport football 3.jpg and the African football]] '''Sport''' usually means Athletics (sport) game that involve a degree of competition, such as netball, basketball or cross-country Olympic skiing. Some games and many kinds of racing are called sports. A professional at a sport is called an athlete. Many people do sports with their friends. Sportsperson need Coach (sport) to teach or train teams or individuals how to do better. Sports can be played indoors or Position and by individuals or team. Sports are helpful for health and can help reduce diseases such as heart attacks, many types of cancer, depression and anxiety, and dementia. Different types of sports help our body in different ways. For children, sports play an extremely important part in their lives by providing all round development of the child, physically, mentally and emotionally. Some people like to watch other people play sports. Those who watch others playing sports are called ''fanatics''. While some fans watch sports on television, others actually go to stadiums or other places where people pay to watch them in person. These fans are called ''spectators''.
