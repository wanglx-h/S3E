File:IGN_Entertainment_Logo.svg '''IGN''' is a game website that was first launched in 2000. It has FAQ's, guides, and walkthroughs about many of the games on GameCube, personal computer, PlayStation 2, Xbox, Wii, PlayStation 3, Xbox 360, Xbox One, and the PlayStation 4, as well as older systems. IGN's main competitor is GameSpot, another gaming website. IGN also features a popular message board, with a lot of members. == Other websites == * [http://www.ign.com Official website] Category:Gaming websites
