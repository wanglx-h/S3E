File:O cursiva.gif '''O''' is the fifteenth (15 (number)) letter in the English language alphabet and it has a shape of a circle. It appears after “N” and before “P”. == Meanings for Letter O == * On calendars, O sometimes means the month October. * In chemistry, O represents the symbol "oxygen". * In medicine, O is one of the human blood types. * Sometimes when saying decimals, O is another word for 0. ==Other websites== Category:Vowel letters
