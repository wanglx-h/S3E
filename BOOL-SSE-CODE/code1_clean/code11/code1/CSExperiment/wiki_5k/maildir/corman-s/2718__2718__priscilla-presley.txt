She is the former wife of Elvis Presley and is the mother of Lisa Marie Presley. Presley is probably most famous for her roles in the television primetime soap opera ''Dallas (TV series)'', and ''The Naked Gun'' movies with Leslie Nielson. She is also a member of Church of Scientology. ==Early life== File:Meeting priscilla presley (cropped).jpg Presley was born at Brooklyn Naval Hospital in Brooklyn, New York City.
