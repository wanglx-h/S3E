Subject: Sri Yukteswar Giri
Image:Sriyukteswar.jpg '''Sri Yukteswar Giri''' (May 10, 1855 - March 9, 1936) was an Indian guru and swami. His real name was '''Priya Nath Karar''' and was born in Serampore, West-Bengal, India. He is one of the famous people who appears on the cover of the Beatles' Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band. He became known through "Autobiography of a Yogi" as the guru of the author Paramahansa Yogananda.
