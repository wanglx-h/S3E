'''''Joey''''' was a 2004-2006 United States television programme. It was a situation comedy on the NBC television network. The series was a spin-off of the series ''Friends''. It starred Matt LeBlanc as Joey Tribbiani, his Fictional character from ''Friends''. It consists of Joey, his sister, and his nephew. NBC canceled the series due to low ratings in May 2006. == Cast == * Matt LeBlanc as Joey Tribbiani * Drea de Matteo as Gina Tribbiani (Joey's sister) * Paulo Costanzo as Michael Tribbiani (Joey's nephew; Gina's son) * Jennifer Coolidge as Bobbie Morganstern * Andrea Anders as Alex Garrett ''Joey'' stopped broadcasting after the episode "Joey and the Snowball fight" because fewer than 4.1 million viewers tuned in, compared to 18 million who tuned into see the pilot episode.
