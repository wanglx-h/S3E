Image:Thomm Foto.jpg Image:DEU Thomm COA.svg '''Thomm''' is a village near Trier (at the Moselle River) in Rhineland-Palatinate in Germany. Thomm is famous for an old Celtic stone, called ''Hinkelstein'' near the village and a big Celtic grave. In further times there was a slate minor industry near Thomm. Thomm is also famous for its cider, an apple-wine.
