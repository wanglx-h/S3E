| iso_code = US-TN | ElectoralVotes = 11 | website = www.tn.gov }} '''Tennessee''' is a U.S. state in the United States. Its capital (city) is Nashville, Tennessee, which is the country music center of America. It is also the home of the Great Smoky Mountains National Park which are a famous tourist attraction. Other well known cities and towns are Memphis, Tennessee, Knoxville, Tennessee, Chattanooga, Tennessee, Oak Ridge, Tennessee, Lynchburg, Tennessee, Carthage, Tennessee, Lawrenceburg, Tennessee, Clarksville, Tennessee, Lebanon, Tennessee, Pigeon Forge, Tennessee, Murfreesboro, Tennessee, and Gatlinburg, Tennessee. Tennessee touches eight states: Kentucky and Virginia to the north; North Carolina to the east; Georgia (U.S. state), Alabama, and Mississippi on the south; Arkansas and Missouri (by the Mississippi River) to the west. Tennessee ties Missouri as the state bordering the most other states. Tennessee was the 16th state to join the nation, on June 1, 1796. About 6.9 million people lived there in 2020. Several professional sports teams play there, including the Tennessee Titans of the NFL, the Memphis Grizzlies of the NBA, and the Nashville Predators of the NHL. Cherokee and other Native American tribes lived in Tennessee before the arrival of Europeans. African American slaves worked on plantations in the state. Tennessee is known for its country music and Cuisine of the Southern United States.
