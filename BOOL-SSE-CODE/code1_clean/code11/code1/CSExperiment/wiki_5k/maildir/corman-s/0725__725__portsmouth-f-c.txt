Subject: Portsmouth F.C.
'''Portsmouth F.C.''' is an England Association football club. They play in EFL League One. Their home stadium is called Fratton Park. Portsmouth's manager is John Mousinho. The most famous manager was probably Harry Redknapp who took the team to an FA Cup victory in 2008 which subsequently got the team into the UEFA Europa League (then called the UEFA Cup) for the first time. The club has since been hit with financial problems and see themselves in the fourth tier of English football. They have a big rivalry with a team called Southampton F.C.
