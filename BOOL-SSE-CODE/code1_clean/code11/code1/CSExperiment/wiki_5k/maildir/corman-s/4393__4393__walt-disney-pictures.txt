A few Disney movies were rated PG-13: ''Pirates of the Caribbean'' and its sequels and ''John Carter (movie)''. Walt Disney Pictures started as a subsidiary of The Walt Disney Studios (division) in 1985. Before that, Disney movies were released under the '''Walt Disney Productions''' name. Part of Distribution Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures, the studio also has Walt Disney Animation Studios and DisneyToon Studios.
