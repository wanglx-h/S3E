File:Flag of Germany (1935–1945).svgFile:Flag of Japan (1870-1999).svgFile:Flag of Italy (1861-1946) crowned.svg '''Axis countries''' and '''Axis Powers''' was a military coalition of country that fought together against the Allies of World War II during World War II. Benito Mussolini, Prime Minister of the Fascist Italy, announced that an October 1936 agreement had created an Italo-German "axis" around which future European events would revolve. Nazi Germany made a separate agreement with the Empire of Japan at the same time. The three major powers were joined by several minor countries. The war ended in 1945 with the Axis powers losing and their alliances broken. == Berlin-Rome-Tokyo Axis Powers == The most important Axis countries organized the '''Berlin-Rome-Tokyo Axis Powers''' which they formalized with the Pact of Steel and Tripartite Pact.
