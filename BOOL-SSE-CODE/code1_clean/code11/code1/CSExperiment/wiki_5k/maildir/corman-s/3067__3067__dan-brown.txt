File:Dan Brown bookjacket cropped.jpg '''Dan Brown''' (born June 22, 1964) is an Americans writer. His best-known book is ''The Da Vinci Code''. Dan Brown lives in the United States. His father was a mathematics teacher and his mother was a church organ (music)ist. Before Dan Brown wrote books, he was an English language teacher. Dan Brown's wife, Blythe, is an artist. Sometimes she is known to help him with his books. Dan Brown also likes to write about cryptography and secrets. Sometimes he writes about real secrets, but he mostly writes fiction. Hollywood, California movie director Ron Howard made a movie about one of Brown's books. Tom Hanks is the movie's star actor. It was released in movie theater in May 2006. Brown's next book was supposed to be called ''The Solomon Key'', but then the name was changed to ''The Lost Symbol''. When the book ''The Da Vinci Code'' came out in the United States, there were some game hidden in the art of the book cover. Brown said that the answers to those puzzles give hints about what would be in his next book. Two of the puzzles are about a famous sculpture called ''Kryptos'', which is in the middle of the Central Intelligence Agency Headquarters building in Washington D.C.
