Subject: Sadness
File:01 Tristeza.jpg '''Sadness''' is an emotion. It is the opposite of happy. People feel sad when something bad has happened, for example, if their mother or father has died. In capital letters, '''SAD''', or '''S.A.D.''' is short for "Seasonal affective disorder". SAD is a sickness that some people have in the winter. They feel Major depressive disorder as the nights become longer and there is less daylight. When people are very sad, and for a long time, the person could have a serious mental illness called Major depressive disorder or Bipolar disorder. == Notes == It is normally thought of as the opposite of happiness, but you can feel both happy and sad at the same time. ==Related pages== * Happiness * Depression (mental illness) Category:Basic English 850 words Category:Emotions
