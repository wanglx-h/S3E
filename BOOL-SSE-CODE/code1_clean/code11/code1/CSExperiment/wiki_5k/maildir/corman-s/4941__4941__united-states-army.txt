Subject: United States Army
File:Logo of the United States Army 2023.svg (since 2023)]] The '''United States Army''' is a branch (or section) of the United States Armed Forces that deals mainly with land-based missions. Oher parts of the military deal with air and sea missions. the Army's current Commander-in-Chief is the U.S. President Donald Trump. The United States Secretary of the Army, a civilian, leads the army with the help of the Army Chief of Staff, a General officer. Members of an army are called Soldier. When people join the army, they must serve for a certain amount of time, called a "tour of duty." A new soldier, or recruit, first goes through basic raining at a training camp to become. There, soldiers are trained in Weapon and equipment that they will operate to carry out missions. ==Mission== The U.S. Army serves as the land-based branch of the U.S. military.
