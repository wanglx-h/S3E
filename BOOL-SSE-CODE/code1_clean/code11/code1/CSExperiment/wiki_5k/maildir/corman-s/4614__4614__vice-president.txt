Subject: Vice president
A '''vice president''' is someone who helps a president conduct his or her duties. A vice president also takes over if a president is not wikt:available. In business and in government a vice president (VP) is below a president in rank. Vice is comes from Latin meaning 'in place of'.[http://www.etymonline.com/index.php?term=vice- "vice"], etymologyonlive.com In British English a vice president in business is called a Board of directors. In some country, a vice president is called a ''deputy president''.''Constitutional Law of 10 EU Member States: The 2004 Enlargement'', eds.
