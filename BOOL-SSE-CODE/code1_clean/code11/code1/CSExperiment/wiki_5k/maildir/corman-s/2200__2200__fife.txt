Subject: Fife
File:Fife Brit Isles Sect 2.svg '''Fife''' is a county on the north coast of the Firth of Forth, Scotland. The county town is Glenrothes. For local government, the county is a Council area (Scotland) too. The towns in Fife are: * Buckhaven * Kirkcaldy * St Andrews * Dunfermline * High Vallyfield * Dalgety Bay * Kelty * Hillend, Fife * Dairsie * Methil * Leven, Fife * Auchtertool * Auchtermuchty * Falkland, Fife * Cowdenbeath * Cardenden * Cupar Category:Fife
