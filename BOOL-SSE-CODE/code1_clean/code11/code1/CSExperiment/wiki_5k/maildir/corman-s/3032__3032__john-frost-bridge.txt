Subject: John Frost Bridge
Image:John Frost Brug (Arnhem) 01.jpg The '''John Frost Bridge''' is a bridge in Arnhem. It was bombed after Operation Market Garden in September 1944. It was later rebuilt. Some parts of the bridge are still the same as they were in 1944.
