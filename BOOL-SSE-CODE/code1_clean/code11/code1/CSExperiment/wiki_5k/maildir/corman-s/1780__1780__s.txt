File:S cursiva.gif '''S''' is the nineteenth (19 (number)) letter in the English language alphabet. == Meanings for S == * On calendars, S is most times the abbreviation for Saturday or Sunday, or the month September. * In chemistry, S is the symbol for sulphur. * In Wheel of Fortune (US game show), S is one of the six letters given to any contestant participating in the "Bonus Round." * S is the letter Onomatopoeia used to characterize the hissing noise known to be made by snakes, e.g. "sssssss..." * Hebrew people also consider “S” to be sacred because it is the initial of Sameh, God. * S (programming language) is used for statistics. ==Other websites==
