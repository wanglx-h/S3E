Accessed 28 June 2008 Historians use written sources to understand past events and societies. ==Education== Professional historians often get a Master's degree or Doctor of Philosophy. A Master's degree student spends most of their time in the classroom. A PhD student needs to write a long research paper known as a thesis to graduate. Classes focus on learning about history, not teaching history. However, most historians do need to teach history. ==As a job== Professional historians are often Professor of history and teach it at colleges and universities. They share their ideas about history by writing books and articles. Other historians work in public history.
