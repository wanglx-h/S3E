Subject: Still Standing
'''''Still Standing''''' is a CBS sitcom starring Mark Addy. It is about a working-class couple who live in Chicago, Illinois. Addy's Fictional character is a father of three with a wife named Judy (Jami Gertz). Judy and Addy's character, Bill Miller, deal with having an annoying relative, Aunt Linda (Jennifer Irwin), and with having children of different ages. It was first shown in September 2002. Lifetime had the rights to have the show in 2005.
