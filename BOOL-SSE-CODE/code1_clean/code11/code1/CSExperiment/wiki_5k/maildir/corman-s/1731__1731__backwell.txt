|unitary_england= North Somerset |lieutenancy_england=Somerset |region= South West England |constituency_westminster= North Somerset (UK Parliament constituency) |post_town= BRISTOL |postcode_district= BS48 |postcode_area= BS |dial_code= 01275 |os_grid_reference= ST485685 }} '''Backwell''' is a village in the county of Avon (county), in North Somerset. 5,455 people live in Backwell. It is 7 miles from Bristol. Nailsea, Flax Bourton, Yatton, Brockley and Barrow Gurney are nearby. There is a railway station in the village called ''Nailsea and Backwell''. Bristol International Airport is less than three miles away. ==References== Category:Villages in Somerset
