File:Vandalized KÄ—dainiai cemetery 2.jpg, Lithuania.]] File:G-20_-_Hamburg_Schulterblatt_01.jpg and bicycle in Hamburg, Germany.]] '''Vandalism''' is a crime that is done by wikt:destroy or wikt:damage the property of someone else. This can include graffiti and website damage. A person that does this is called a '''vandal'''. The name comes from the Vandals, a tribe that attacked and damaged the city of Rome in 455 CE. ==Examples== File:Graffiti_in_Bucharest,_July_2007.jpg".]] Examples of physical vandalism include salting lawns, cutting trees without permission, egg throwing, breaking windows, arson, spraying paint on others' properties, and tagging. ===Internet=== Many Website, such as Wiki, such as Wikipedia, Internet forum, and Blog, can be changed by anyone. Because of this, they can be "vandalized" by adding Commentary or replacing everything on the Webpage with spam, nonsense or other wikt:silly content. It is a kind of Troll (internet), and these websites have special users, called administrators, whose job is to remove the vandalism and to wikt:block users who do not stop vandalising.
