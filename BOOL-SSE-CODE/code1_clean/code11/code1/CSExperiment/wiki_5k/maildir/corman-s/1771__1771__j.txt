File:J cursiva.gif '''J''' is the tenth (10 (number)) letter in the English language alphabet. It comes before the letter K and after the letter I. The letter was not used in the Roman civilization until the 16th Century. == Meanings for J == * In calendars, J can sometimes mean these months: January, June, or July. * In a deck of playing cards, J is used to mark each of the jack (playing card). * Notation of cult figure John Income * It can also be used as a initial for people with first or middle names such as Jack, James, John, Jesus, or Jason. ==Other websites==
