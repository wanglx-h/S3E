Subject: Fifth Avenue
File:Fifth Avenue.jpg '''Fifth Avenue''' is a large avenue in New York City (U.S.). Running from Greenwich Village in southern Manhattan to Harlem in the north, the avenue marks the line dividing Manhattan into two parts, called the East Side and the West Side. The nearest avenues, on either side, are Madison Avenue (east) and Sixth Avenue (west), also known as the Avenue of the Americas. There are many famous sights, such as the New York Public Library, St. Patrick's Cathedral (New York), Rockefeller Center and Central Park (which runs along the west side, between 59th and 110th Street).
