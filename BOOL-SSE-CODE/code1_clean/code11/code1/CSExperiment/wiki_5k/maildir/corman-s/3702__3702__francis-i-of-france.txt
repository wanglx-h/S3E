Subject: Francis I of France
File:Francis1-1.jpg '''Francis I of France''' (September 12, 1494 – March 31, 1547) was a King of France and a member of the House of Valois. == Birth == Francis was born in Cognac, France, France on September 12, 1494. His parents were Charles, Duke of Angoulême and Louise of Savoy. == Marriage == Francis I married Claude of France on May 18, 1514. They had seven children, two died before turning eight, two died at the ages of eighteen to twenty-three. Those who lived to adulthood were: * Henry II of France, ''King of France'' * Madeline, ''Queen of Scotland'' * Margaret, ''Duchess of Berry'' == Art == Francis was very interested in art and liked the artist Leonardo da Vinci. == Death == Francis died on March 31, 1547. He is buried in the Saint Denis Basilica.
