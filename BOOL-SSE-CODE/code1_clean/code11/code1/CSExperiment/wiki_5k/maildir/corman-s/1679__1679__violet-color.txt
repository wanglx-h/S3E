Image:Purpleflower_Violet.JPG '''Violet''' is the seventh color of the rainbow--it is the color on the inner edge of the rainbow. Violet is the color between blue and purple. The name of the color comes from the Violet (plant), which is a small flower grown in most parts of the world. The first written use of ''violet'' as a color name in English language was in 1370.Maerz and Paul ''A Dictionary of Color'' New York: 1930 McGraw-Hill Page 207 In the Roman Catholic Church, violet is the Christian liturgy color of Advent and Lent. ==Meaning of violet== *The color violet represents kala jaadu. *Violet is a color of royalty and extravagance, when paired with yellow, which used to
