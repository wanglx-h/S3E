Subject: Vengaboys
The '''Vengaboys''' are a Netherlands pop music band. The group is made up of DJ Danski and DJ Delmondo. They toured Spain beach party in a bus. The group formed in 1992. They added four dancers in 1995-1996 and toured through Europe. Their first song was ''Up & Down''. It was released in March 1997. The group is Popularity across Europe and have had some popularity in the United States.
