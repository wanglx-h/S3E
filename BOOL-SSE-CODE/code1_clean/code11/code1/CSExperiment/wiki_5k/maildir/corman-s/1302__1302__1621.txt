== Events == * February 9 – Pope Gregory XV is elected pope. * February 17 – Miles Standish is appointed as first commander of Plymouth Colony. * March 16 – Samoset, a Wampanoag, visits the settlers of Plymouth Colony and greets them, "Welcome, Englishmen! My name is Samoset." * March 22 – The Pilgrims of Plymouth Colony sign a peace treaty with Massasoit of the Wampanoags. * April 5 – The ''Mayflower'' sets sail from Plymouth Colony on a return trip to Great Britain. * The Swedish city of Gothenburg is founded by Gustavus Adolphus * Riga falls under rule of Sweden * Willebrord Snell discovers the laws of refraction.
