(loan) | caps8 = 1 | goals8 = 0 | totalcaps = 365 | totalgoals = 0 | club-update = }} '''Luke McCormick''' (born 15 August 1983) is a former English people association football player. He was born in Coventry, England. He is a goalkeeper. He plays for the England football team Swindon Town F.C.. In the 2003–04 season, McCormick played when Romain Larrieu could not. Larrieu injured his knee. McCormick played instead. Plymouth Argyle beat Stockport County on 24 January 2004. The score was 2-0. In this match McCormick set a new club record of seven ''clean sheets'' in a row. A clean sheet is a game where the other side gets no goals. McCormick was voted the most promising player of the 2003–04 season by Plymouth Argyle fans. On 7 June 2008. McCormick was arrested on suspicion for killing two people by dangerous driving. Subsequently, he was released by Plymouth Arygle and was jailed for seven years on 6 October 2008.
