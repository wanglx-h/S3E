Subject: Science Olympiad
The '''Science Olympiad''' is a nationwide event in the United States that tests students' knowledge in science and technology. It started in 1982, and takes place every year. Science Olympiad consists of twenty three events testing students in middle and high school on different science topics, such as astronomy and chemistry.
