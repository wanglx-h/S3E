Subject: Cannes Film Festival
The '''Cannes Film Festival''' is an event held every year in Cannes, France. The festival previews films from around the world. Only those who are invited can attend the festival. The top film wins the Palme d'Or. The second most prestigious trophy is the Grand Prix (Cannes Film Festival). A jury selects which film earns the prizes. The jury is led by an internationally recognized personality of cinema.
