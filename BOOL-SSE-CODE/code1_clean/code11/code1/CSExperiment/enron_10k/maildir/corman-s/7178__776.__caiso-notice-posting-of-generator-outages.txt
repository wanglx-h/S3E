Subject: CAISO Notice: Posting of Generator Outages
Market Participants and Scheduling Coordinators: As of Monday, March 12, the ISO will revise the format and contents of the generator outage information that is posted daily in accordance with Public Utilities Code section 352.5. The revised content will be planned or unplanned, resource name, owner, location, MW curtailed, Unit Maximum. Further revisions may be implemented in the future. Any questions related to this announcement should be relayed to Jeanne M. Sol, at jsole@caiso.com <mailto:jsole@caiso.com> by noon on Friday March 9.
