PRESENTATIONS THAT WORK August 29 & 30 in EB560 Does your job include preparing and delivering presentations? . . . . . . . Then this course is for you! Participants will receive personalized, confidential feedback from the instructor and will develop a self-improvement action plan. Customized exercises give participants first-hand experience in one-on-one, small groups, impromptu, and sit-down settings.
