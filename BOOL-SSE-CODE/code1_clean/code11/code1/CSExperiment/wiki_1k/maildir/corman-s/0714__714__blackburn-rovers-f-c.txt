Subject: Blackburn Rovers F.C.
'''Blackburn Rovers F.C.'''FC is an acronym. FC stands for "'''F'''ootball '''C'''lub". is an England Association football club. They play at Ewood Park, which is in Blackburn, Lancashire. The manager of the club is Jon Dahl Tomasson. They are famously nicknamed [https://www.lancashiretelegraph.co.uk/sport/12938635.20-years-day-blackburn-rovers-proved-bottle-win-premiership/ 'The Bottlers'] due to how many times they have failed to get promotion to the Premier League while being so close. ==History== Blackburn Rovers won the FA Premier League once in the 1994/1995 season. However, in 1999, they were relegated.
