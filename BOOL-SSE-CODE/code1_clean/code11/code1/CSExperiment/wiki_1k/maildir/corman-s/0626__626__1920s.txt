The '''1920s''' was the decade that began on January 1, 1920 and ended on December 31, 1929. It is distinct from the decade known as the '''193st decade''' which began on January 1, 1921 and ended on December 31, 1930. This decade was known as the Roaring Twenties or the Jazz Age. == The most important events == * 1920 – The League of Nations was founded. * 1920 – Prohibition began in the United States. Criminal activity associated with alcohol. * 1922 – The Soviet Union was founded as a dictatorship. * 1922 – Howard Carter discovered the tomb of the Egyptian King Tutankhamun. * 1922 – Republic of Ireland gained independence from the United Kingdom. * 1929 – Black Tuesday, which lead to the Great Depression. **Role of the depression in stimulating the career of Adolf Hitler in Germany == Important people == === World leaders === * Prime Minister Stanley Bruce (Australia) * Prime Minister William Lyon Mackenzie King (Canada) * President Sun Yat-sen (Republic of China) * President Chiang Kai-shek (Republic of China) * President Friedrich Ebert (Germany) * President Paul von Hindenburg (Germany), later replaced by Adolf Hitler. * Ahmad Shah Qajar of Qajar dynasty (Persia/Iran) * Reza Shah Pahlavi of Pahlavi Dynasty (Iran) * King Victor Emmanuel III of Italy (Italy) * Prime Minister Benito Mussolini (Italy) * President W.T.
