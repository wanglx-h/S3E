File:National Park Service 9-11 Statue of Liberty and WTC fire.jpg, terrorist attack on the New York World Trade Center (1973â€“2001).]] '''Terrorism''' is the use of fear and violence for Politics, Religion or Ideology reasons. Many different types of social or political organizations might use terrorism to try to achieve their goals. People who carry out terrorism are called ''terrorists''. The foundation of modern terrorism is the work of Sergey Nechayev, a Russia wikt:radical who developed strategies for carrying out terrorism, which also includes politics. ==Overview== File:BombenanschlagUS-BotschaftBeirut.jpg Embassy in Beirut after a terror attack.]] It is difficult to explain terrorism. Terrorism has no official criminal law definition at the International law. Common definitions of terrorism refer to violent acts which are intended to create fear. The acts may be done for a Religion, political or Ideology Motivation and use illegal violence. Some definitions now include acts of unlawful violence and war crimes. The use of similar tactics by criminal gangs is not usually called terrorism. The same actions may be called terrorism when they are done by a politically-motivated group. There are over 10,000 definitions of "terrorism". In some cases, the same group may be described as "Freedom fighter" by its supporters and "terrorists" by its opponents. The term is often used by states to criticize political opponents. The major effect of terrorism comes from the fear that is generated. ==Counter-terrorism== File:ROC Military Police Special Force in Counter-Terrorism Exercise.jpg Military Police Special Force in Counter-Terrorism Exercise.]] Counter-terrorism is broad in scope. Types of counter-terrorism include but not limited: * Preemptive Humanitarianism activities * Preemptive war or reactive military action * Fortification and other technical means * More permissive interrogation and detention Policy for the Suspect terrorists * Increased military intelligence and surveillance activities. Often facilitated and rubber stamped with legislation like the United States Patriot Act ==Context== The more modern governments restrict weapons to their military, the more ordinary people are helpless against terrorists.
