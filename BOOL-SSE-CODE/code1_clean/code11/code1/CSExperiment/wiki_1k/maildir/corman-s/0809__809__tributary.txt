Subject: Tributary
File:Icon river tributary L.svg A '''tributary''' of a river is another river that Flux into it. If one river flows into a second river, then the first river is a '''tributary''' of the second river. A tributary is a body of water that flows into another body of water. The opposite of a tributary is a distributary. A distributary is a river that branches off from the main flow of water, for example in a river delta. Category:Rivers
