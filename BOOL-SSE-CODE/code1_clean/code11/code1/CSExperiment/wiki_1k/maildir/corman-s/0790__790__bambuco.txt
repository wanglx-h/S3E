Subject: Bambuco
'''Bambuco''' is a type of music from Colombia. The beat (music) of this music is like the European waltz (music) or polska. Bambuco is often folk music. There is a dance that goes with Bambuco music. It is a group dance and has a 6/8 or 3/4 metre (music). Sometimes Bambuco is called '''"el bambuco"'''. People think Bambuco started in Africa. It is popular in the Andean region of Colombia and throughout Latin America. Category:Folk music category:Colombian culture
