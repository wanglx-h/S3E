Subject: Sheffield F.C.
'''Sheffield Football Club''' is a association football club in England. Sheffield F.C. is the oldest football club in the world. It was set up in 1857 in the city of Sheffield. Unlike Sheffield United F.C. and Sheffield Wednesday F.C., it does not play in a national league.
