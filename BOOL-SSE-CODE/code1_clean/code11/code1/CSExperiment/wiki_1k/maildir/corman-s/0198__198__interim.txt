An '''interim''' is a period of temporary pause or change in a sequence of events, or a temporary state, and is often applied to transitional political entities. '''Interim''' may also refer to: ==Temporary organizational arrangements (general concept)== *Provisional government, emergency government during the creation, collapse, or crisis of a state; also called interim government *Caretaker government, temporary rule between governments in a parliamentary democracy; also called interim government *Acting president, interim head of a state *Acting (law), designation of a person temporarily exercising the authority of any position *Interim management, in business ==Specific temporary political arrangements== ===Provisional and interim
