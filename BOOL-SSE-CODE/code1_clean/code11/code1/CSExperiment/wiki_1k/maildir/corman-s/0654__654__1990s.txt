Many important world events happened in the 1990s. One was the fall of the Soviet Union, which ended the Cold War. Two Genocide, in Rwandan genocide and Bosnian genocide, happened in the '90s. In South Africa, Apartheid ended. The European Union was established, and the Gulf War was fought in the Middle East. There were major developments in Information technology. The World Wide Web (invented in 1989) became extremely popular and connected people around the world. Microsoft and Apple Inc. grew significantly. In sports, West Germany, Brazil, and France each won a FIFA World Cup. In Major League Baseball, World Series winners included the Cincinnati Reds, Minnesota Twins, Toronto Blue Jays, Atlanta Braves, New York Yankees, and Miami Marlins. == Events == These are just a few of the major world events that occurred in the 1990s: * 1990 - 1991 – Gulf War * 1990 – German reunification * 1991 – The end of the Cold War. * 1991 – Breakup of Yugoslavia, followed by the Yugoslav wars. * 1991 – Dissolution of the Soviet Union * 1991 – Rosuvastatin was Patent.
