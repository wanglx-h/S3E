File:Candylol.jpg]] File:Buesum molenfeuer lol rofl.JPG '''LOL,''' or '''lol''', is an internet slang term that means "laugh(ing) out loud". It is commonly mistaken for "lots of love." LOL is often used in wikt:message meaning "That is really Humour." It also means mute in Persian language and is a native Dutch language word (not an acronym) which means "fun" ("wikt:lollig" means "funny"). In Welsh language, lol means nonsense - e.g., if a person wanted to say "utter nonsense" in Welsh, they would say "rwtsh lol". LOL is usually used on the internet or when text messaging. ==Words similar to "LOL"== * '''lolz''',''' lulz''',''' lul, lawls''': Sometimes used instead of '''LOL''', but can have other meanings. * '''LOOOOOL''': For more wikt:emphasis. Any amount of "OL"s can be added to the end of LOL. * '''LMAO''': Stands for "Laugh my Buttocks off". Another way to say it is '''LMFAO''' ("Laugh my fucking ass off"). * '''ROFL''': For "wikt:roll on the floor laughing". It is often combined with LMAO to make '''ROFLMAO''' ("Rolling on the floor laughing my ass off"). Another way to write this is '''ROTFL'''. *'''ROTFLOL''': Rolling on the floor laughing out loud; used by Weird Al Yankovic in the song "White and Nerdy". *'''LEL:''' Means the same thing as LOL, but with an E. *'''kek:''' The Japan version of saying lol. ==Related page== * Lolcat ==References== Category:Internet slang
