It sells Hamburger, french fries, and soft drink. It was created in 1954 in Miami, Florida, by James McLamore and David Edgerton. Edgerton bought a restaurant named Insta Burger King and changed it to Burger King. More than 34,000 people work for the company. There are more than 18,000 Burger King restaurants world-wide in 100 countries, and almost all of them are Franchising. But in Australia, since the name "Burger King" was already Trademark there, BK franchises there are called '''Hungry Jack's'''. Burger King is owned mostly by 3G Capital, a Brazilian investment company. But it is also owned by shareholders and used to Public company on the New York Stock Exchange under the Ticker symbol '''BKW'''. In 2014, the chain's operations was merged with that of Tim Hortons from Canada, under the name Restaurant Brands International (). Burger King is an American global chain of hamburger fast food restaurants.
