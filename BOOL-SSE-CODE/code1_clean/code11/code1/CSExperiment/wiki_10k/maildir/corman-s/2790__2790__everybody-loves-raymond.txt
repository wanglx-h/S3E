Subject: Everybody Loves Raymond
'''''Everybody Loves Raymond''''' is an United States television programme. It stars Ray Romano. The series was a popular sitcom on the CBS television network. ==Overview== Ray Romano plays Ray Barone, a sports writer in Lynbrook, New York. Patricia Heaton plays his wife, Debra. Ray and Deborah have a fun set of neighbors: Ray's parents. Robert, Ray's "loser" brother, is a police officer, and is very unlucky in love. By the end of season seven, however, he marries a woman named Amy.
