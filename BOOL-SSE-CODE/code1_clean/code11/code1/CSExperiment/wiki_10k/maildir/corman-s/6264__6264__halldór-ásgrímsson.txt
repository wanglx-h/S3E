Subject: Halldór Ásgrímsson
File:Halldor Asgrimsson generalsekreterare Nordiska ministerradet (2).jpg '''Halldór Ásgrímsson''' (8 September 1947 18 May 2015) was the Prime Minister of Iceland. He became the leader of the Progressive Party (Iceland) in 1994. On September 15, 2004, he took over as Prime Minister from the Independence Party (Iceland) leader, Davíð Oddsson. He resigned in 2009.
