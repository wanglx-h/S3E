Subject: Eurasian people
A '''Eurasian person''' is a person of mixed European and Asian descent or mixed parentage (someone with one Caucasian race parent and one Asian parent). In Hawaii, the term ''hapa'' is used. Eurasian people can also mean people who live in Europe or Asia, or by the Ural Mountains Category:Ethnic groups in Asia category:Ethnic groups in Europe
