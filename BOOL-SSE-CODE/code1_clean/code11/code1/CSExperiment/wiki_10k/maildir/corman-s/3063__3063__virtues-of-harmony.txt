Subject: Virtues of Harmony
'''''Virtues of Harmony''''' was a sitcom that ran in Hong Kong from 2001 until February 2005. ''Virtues of Harmony One'' was set in ancient China during the Ming Dynasty. It follows the lives of the Kam family. The allure of the series was the comedy storylines and how the Good (philosophy) always wins. The star of the show was Nancy Sit, a kind hearted matriarch with the best intentions. Following the success of ''Virtues of Harmony One'', ''Virtues of Harmony Two'' was set in modern Hong Kong with a similar cast.
