Subject: Wasilla, Alaska
Image:Cityofwasilla.JPG '''Wasilla''' is a small city in the U.S. state of Alaska, about 43 miles from Anchorage, Alaska. It is one of the ten largest cities in Alaska. Sarah Palin was once mayor and owns a house with her family there. The city's population was 9,054 at the 2020 United States Census. ==References== ==Other websites== *[http://www.cityofwasilla.com/ Official City of Wasilla website] Category:Cities in Alaska
