| Past_members = Felix CavaliereEddie BrigatiGene CornishDino Danelli }} '''The Rascals''' (formerly known as '''The Young Rascals''') were an United States pop music-soul music band Popularity in the 1960s. They are known for their songs: Good Lovin', Groovin', A Beautiful Morning, and People Got To Be Free. Their members were: Felix Cavaliere (Singer and Keyboard instrument), Eddie Brigati (vocals), Gene Cornish (guitar) and Dino Danelli (Drum). Dino Danelli died on December the 15, 2022, at the age of 78 years old.
