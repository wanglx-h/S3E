It is in the center of Anatolia. Ankara is the second largest city after Istanbul. The city has a population of 4,319,167 (2005) (Province 5,153,000), and an elevation of 938 meters (3080 feet).[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/TU/68/Ankara.html Ankara, Turkey: Latitude, Longitude and Altitude] It was formerly known as Engürü. The city is the capital of Ankara Province Ankara is a very modern city. Ankara was made the capital of Turkey in 1923. Ankara is known for its performing arts, home to the State Opera and Ballet, the Presidential Symphony Orchestra and several national theater companies. In Ankara there's a place called Anitkabir, which is a big hilltop mausoleum that was built to celebrate Kemal Atatürk who was the first president. == History == Ankara is a city that has a lot of history. Ankara was invaded many times. In 546 BCE, Achaemenid Empire Persians was in control of Ankara (approximated date). Ankara was then invaded by the Roman Empire in 25 BCE. At the time, Ankara was controlled by Augustus. Ankara castle was attacked by Crusades in 1101. Ankara castle was captured by the Seljuk Empire in 1227. Ankara was controlled again by the Ottoman Empire in 1403.
