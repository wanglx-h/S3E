Subject: Zhuge Liang
File:Zhuge_Kongming_Sancai_Tuhui.jpg '''Zhuge Liang''' (;181-234) was a China politician and general of the ancient Shu Han Dynasty. He helped his lord Liu Bei conquer the country ''Ba Shu'' from 211 to 214, along with Pang Tong. After Liu Bei's death in 223, Zhuge Liang was a regent for the new emperor Liu Shan. Zhuge Liang attempted to conquer the Southern Wei Dynasty territories five times but had no success. He died in 234, and Jiang Wan succeed his power.
