Subject: Zoroaster
'''Zoroaster''' was an ancient prophet who lived in Persia. He founded a religion named Zoroastrianism. This religion has a long history. It was the national religion of Sassanid Empire of ancient Persia. In Persian language, the name takes the form of ''Zartosht''. |image=Zartosht 30salegee.jpg |birth_date = 6th century BCE |death_date = }} Most scholars agree that Zoroaster was a real person who lived long ago. No one is certain about the time when he lived.
