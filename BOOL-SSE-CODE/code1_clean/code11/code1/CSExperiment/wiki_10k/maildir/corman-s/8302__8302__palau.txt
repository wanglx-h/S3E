It is in the Pacific Ocean. Palau is 300 miles (500kilometre) east of the Philippines. It got independence from United Nations trusteeship administration (administered by the United States) in 1994. It is one of the world's youngest and smallest nations. It is sometimes referred to in English language under its native name '''Belau'''. The capitol of Palau used to be Koror.
