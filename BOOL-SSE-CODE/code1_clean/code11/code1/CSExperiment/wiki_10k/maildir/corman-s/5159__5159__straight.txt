Subject: Straight
The word '''straight''' means something that goes in one direction without wikt:turns, wikt:bends, or curves. Lines can bend in a curve. If lines do not bend, they are '''straight'''. For example, the edge of a circle is not straight. The edge of a circle bends in a curve. Light bends in a curve in air and water, but travels in a straight line away from the earth or other bodies in outer space where there is no air.
