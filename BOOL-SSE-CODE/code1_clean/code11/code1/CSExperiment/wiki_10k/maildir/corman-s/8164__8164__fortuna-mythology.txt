Subject: Fortuna (mythology)
File:Fortuna, rielaborazione romana da originale greco del IV secolo ac. con testa non pertinente, da tor bovicciana (ostia), inv. 2244.JPG '''Fortuna''' was the Deity of fortune, luck and fate in Roman mythology. Fortuna could bestow good or bad luck onto people. She was sometimes represented veiled and blind. She was a daughter of Jupiter (mythology) and like him, she could also be bountiful and generous to everybody. ==Related pages== * Tyche (mythology) - Greek mythology version of Fortuna * The Wheel of Fortune ==Other websites== Category:Roman gods and goddesses
