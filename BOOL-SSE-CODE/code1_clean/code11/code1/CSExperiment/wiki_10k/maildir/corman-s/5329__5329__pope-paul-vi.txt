'''Pope Paul VI''' (; ), born '''Giovanni Battista Enrico Antonio Maria Montini''', was an Italian priest of the Roman Catholic Church and the List of popes Pope from 1963 until his death in 1978.[http://www.newadvent.org/cathen/12272b.htm "List of Popes,"] ''Catholic Encyclopedia'' (2009); retrieved 2011-11-02. Paul was a spiritual leader and the head of the church bureaucracy.Briggs, Kenneth A. August 7, 1978; retrieved 2011-10-30. Pope Benedict XVI declared in 2012 that Paul had lived “a life of heroic virtue.” Two years later he was beatified by Pope Francis. He was canonized by the same pope in October 2018.
