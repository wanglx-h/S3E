Subject: Khadijah bint Khuwaylid
'''Khadijah bint Khuwaylid''' () (555 - 619) was the first wife of Muhammad, an Prophets of Islam. Her nickname was Khadijah al-Kubra. She was known by the special names Thahira and Ummu Hinth. She was the daughter of Khuwaylid ibn Asad and Fatimah bint Za'idah. Khadijah is widely known as the first person to convert to Islam.
