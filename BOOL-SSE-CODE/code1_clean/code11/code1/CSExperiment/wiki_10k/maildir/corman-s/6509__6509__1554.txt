Subject: 1554
== Events == * January 5 – Great fire in Eindhoven, Netherlands. * February – Thomas Wyatt the younger surrenders to government forces in London. * February 12 – After claiming the throne of England the previous year, Lady Jane Grey is beheaded for treason alongside her husband. * March 17 – Princess Elizabeth I of England imprisoned in Tower of London. * April 12 – Mary of Guise becomes Regent of Scotland. * July 23 – July 25 – Wedding of Queen Mary I of England and King Philip II of Spain, only son of Emperor Charles V in Winchester, England. * August 12 – Battle of Renty. French forces led by Francis, Duke of Guise turn back an invasion of Picardy by Charles V. * The French forces in Siena surrender to the Imperialists after a long siege. * Mikael Agricola becomes the bishop of Turku. * Jesuits establish São Paulo in Brazil.
