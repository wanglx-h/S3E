Subject: Sunday
File:2012-10-13_15-29-35-sun.jpg]] '''Sunday''' is one of the seven days of the week. It is part of the weekend, along with Saturday. Sunday is the shabbat or day of Christian worship for most Christians (for example, Easter is celebrated on Sunday). Sunday is the beginning of the week in the United States, but is the end of the week in the United Kingdom. Category:Days of the week
