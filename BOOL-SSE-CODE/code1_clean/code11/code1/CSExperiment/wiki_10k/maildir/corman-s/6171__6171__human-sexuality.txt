File:Gustave Courbet - Le Sommeil (1866), Paris, Petit Palais.jpg: Sleep (1866).]] '''Human sexuality''' is the ability of people to have sexual experiences and feelings. This involves biological, erotic, physical, emotional, social, or Spirituality feelings and behaviors. The most common form of human sexuality is heterosexuality, but homosexuality, bisexuality and asexuality exist too. An abnormal form of sexuality is called a paraphilia, while a normal one is called a normophilia. ==Related pages== *Human sexual activity *LGBT *Pornography ==References== Category:Human sexuality
