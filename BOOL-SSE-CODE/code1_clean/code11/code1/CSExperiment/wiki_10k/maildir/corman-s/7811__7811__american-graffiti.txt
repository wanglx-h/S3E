Subject: American Graffiti
'''''American Graffiti''''' is a 1973 United States teen movie comedy movie-drama movie directed by George Lucas. This was Lucas' second movie and made him very well known. It is about what happens to a group of teenagers one night in August 1962 in Modesto, California, while they are driving around town and listening to radio DJ Wolfman Jack.
