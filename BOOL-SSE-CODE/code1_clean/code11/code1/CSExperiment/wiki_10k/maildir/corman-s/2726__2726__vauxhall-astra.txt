Subject: Vauxhall Astra
'''Vauxhall Astra''' is a car produced by Vauxhall Motors since 1980. It was first just branded as a '''Vauxhall Motors''', since 1991 it has also been sold as a '''Opel''', a '''Holden''' and a '''Chevrolet'''. From 2007 to 2009 it was sold as a Saturn (Automobile).
