Subject: 1707
== Events == * January 1 – John V is crowned King of Portugal * May 1 – The Acts of Union 1707 become law, making the separate kingdoms of Kingdom of England and Kingdom of Scotland into one country, the United Kingdom of Great Britain. * April 25 – Allied army is defeated by Bourbonic army at Almansa (Spain) in the War of the Spanish Succession. * September 29 – Four British Royal Navy ships run aground near Scilly Isles because of faulty navigation – Admiral Sir Cloddisley Shovel and thousands of sailors drown * October 23 – First Parliament of Great Britain. * A fortress is founded on the future site of Ust-Abakanskoye (modern Abakan). * Mount Fuji in Japan erupts. * The Lao empire of Lan Xang officially ends and splits into the kingdoms of Vientiane, Luang Prabang, and Champasak. == Births == * February 1 - Frederick, Prince of Wales (d. 1751) * April 15 - Leonhard Euler, Switzerland mathematician and physicist (d. 1783) * April 22 - Henry Fielding, United Kingdom writer (d. 1754) * May 23 - Carolus Linnaeus, Sweden Botany (d. 1778) * September 7 - Georges-Louis Leclerc, Comte de Buffon, French people scientist (d. 1788) ==Deaths== * May 9 - Dieterich Buxtehude, Germany composer (b. c. 1637) Category:1707
