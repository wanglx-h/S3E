Subject: Tampa Bay Mutiny
'''Tampa Bay Mutiny''' was an United States football (soccer) team that played in Major League Soccer from 1996 to 2001. They played in Tampa, Florida. Their stadium was the Raymond James Stadium. The Mutiny went out of business after the 2001 season, after having low attendance and finishing in last place.
