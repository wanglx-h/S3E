File:Cingular_SIM.pngUMTSSIM Card|thumb|173x173px|Cingular 3G Universal Mobile Telecommunications Service SIM card.]] '''Cingular Wireless''' was a mobile phone company from the United States. Cingular Wireless was created in 2000 resulting from a joint venture of SBC Communications Inc. and the BellSouth Corporation. AT&T Mobility LLC bought Cingular in 2006, subsequently causing Cingular to adopt the AT&T Wireless name. ==References== Category:American telephone companies Category:Mobile phones Category:Companies based in Atlanta Category:2001 establishments in the United States
