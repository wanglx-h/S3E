| season = | position = | website = | }} '''Aberystwyth Town Football Club''' are a Wales football (soccer) team. The club was started in 1884. They play at the Park Avenue stadium in Aberystwyth. They play in the Cymru Premier. They came third in the Welsh league in 1992 but have only had success at a regional level.
