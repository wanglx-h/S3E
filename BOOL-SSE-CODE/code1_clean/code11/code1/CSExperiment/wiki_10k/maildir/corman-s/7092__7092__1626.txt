== Events == * September 30 – Nurhaci, chieftain of the Jurchens and founder of the Qing Dynasty dies and is succeeded by his son Huang Taiji. * November 18 – The new St Peter's Basilica in the Vatican City is consecrated, the anniversary of that of the previous church in 326. * Spain establish a trading colony on Taiwan. * Peter Minuit, director of the New Netherland colony, begins a policy of "purchasing" Manhattan from the Lenape. * Work begins on building the sailing ship Vasa (ship). * The Duchy of Urbino is incorporated into the Papal States. * The English Parliament impeach George Villiers, 1st Duke of Buckingham, favorite of Charles I of England. * Charles I of England dissolves Parliament. * The The Netherlands settle Manhattan, founding the town of New Amsterdam.
