'''Stairway to Heaven''' was a 1971 song by Led Zeppelin. It was on the album Led Zeppelin IV. == Hidden Satanic message == Some listeners claim that by reversing the song, a hidden Satanic message is revealed. The message is: Oh, here's to my sweet Satan The one whose little path would make me sad whose power is Satan He'll give, he'll give you 666 (number) There was a little toolshed where he made us suffer, sad Satan It's unlikely such a message was intentionally hidden in the song; rather, it is just a bizarre coincidence. == Other websites == * [http://www.stairway-to-heaven.net/ Stairway to Heaven songfacts, tabs, videos and audio] , at the Stairway to Heaven website.
