Subject: Comedian
File:Borat in Cologne.jpg, a British comedian and actor portraying Borat Sagdiyev from Kazakhstan.]] A '''comedian''' is a person who tells jokes, or acts in comedy. Some people use the French language loanword ''comedienne'' to refer to female comedians. A comedian can also be called a comic or a comic actor.
