Subject: Electronic Entertainment Expo
Image:E3 Logo.svg The '''Electronic Entertainment Expo''' (Exposition) or '''E3''', first held in 1995, was one of the biggest conferences for video games. Every year in late May or early June, game developers and other professional people would gather to presentation new video games, computer hardware, and features. It was only open to people who had been invited and people that were visiting had to be over eighteen years old. In 2019, Secrecy of more than 6000 people were leaked. As a result, people were harassment.
