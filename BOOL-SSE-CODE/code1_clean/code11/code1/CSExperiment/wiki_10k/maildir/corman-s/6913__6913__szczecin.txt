File:1109 Szczecin Panorama.jpg File:0910 Bazylika archikatedralna św Jakuba Szczecin.jpg of St. Jacobs Cathedral in Szczecin]] File:1109 Ratusz Staromiejski Szczecin.jpg File:Szczecin gmach urzedu celnego 2005-07.jpg Image:POL Szczecin flag.svg Image:POL Szczecin COA.svg '''Szczecin''' () is a large city in Poland in West Pomeranian Voivodeship. As of 2005, 411,119 people live there. The city is on the river Oder River (), near the border to Germany. It is one of the largest sea ports on the Baltic Sea. It is the historical capital of the German province of Pomerania. Szczecin has got one of the biggest harbours on the Baltic Sea.
