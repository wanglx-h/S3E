Subject: Sisimiut
File:Sisimiut.jpg Image:Qeqqata-coat-of-arms.svg '''Sisimiut''' (also known by its Danish_language name 'Holsteinsborg') is a town in southwestern Greenland. With 5,247 people (as of 2005), it is the second biggest town in Greenland. It is 75 kilometers north of the Arctic Circle, and is Greenland's most northern year-round port which has no ice. == Other websites == * [http://www.sisimiut.gl/eng/main_en.htm Municipality of Sisimiut] Category:Settlements in Greenland
