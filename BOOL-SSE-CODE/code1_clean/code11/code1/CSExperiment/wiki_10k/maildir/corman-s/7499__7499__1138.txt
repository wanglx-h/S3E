== Events == * Robert Warelwast becomes Bishop of Exeter. * March 13 – Cardinal Gregory is elected anti-pope as Antipope Victor IV (1138), succeeding Antipope Anacletus II. * August 9 – Deadly earthquake in Aleppo killing about 230,000 people. * August 22 – Battle of the Standard between David I of Scotland and the English people. The Scottish people were defeated. * Bavaria was taken away from the Welfen Duke Henry X, Duke of Bavaria and given to the Austrian Margrave Leopold IV of Austria (Babenberg). Category:1138
