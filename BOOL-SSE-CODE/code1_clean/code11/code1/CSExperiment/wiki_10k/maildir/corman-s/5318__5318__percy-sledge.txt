File:Percy Sledge Alabama Music Hall of Fame (cropped).jpg '''Percy Sledge''' (November 25, 1940 April 14, 2015) was an Americans R&B singer. He was born in Leighton, Alabama. He was best known for his song "When a Man Loves a Woman".
