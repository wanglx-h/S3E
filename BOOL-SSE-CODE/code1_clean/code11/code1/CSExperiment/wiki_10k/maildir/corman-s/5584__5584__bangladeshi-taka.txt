Bangladesh | subunit_ratio_1 = 1/100 | subunit_name_1 = poisha | symbol = ৳ | plural = Taka | plural_subunit_1 = | frequently_used_coins = ৳1, ৳2, ৳5 | frequently_used_banknotes = ৳2, ৳5, ৳10, ৳20, ৳50, ৳100, ৳500 & ৳1000 }} The '''Taka''' is the currency of Bangladesh. The most commonly used symbol for the Taka is Tk and ৳. This is used on receipts while purchasing goods and services. One taka is divided into 100 paisa. In Bengali language, the word ''taka'' is also used to mean any money, currency, or notes. Bangladesh Bank, the central bank of the country, prints the currency, except for one taka and two taka notes, which are made by the Ministry of Finance. == Exchange rates == The taka is fixed to be the same as 0.008182 USD. == Other websites == * [http://www.bis-ans-ende-der-welt.net/Bangladesch-B-En.htm Banknotes of Bangladesh] == References == category:Economy of Bangladesh Category:Currency of Asia
