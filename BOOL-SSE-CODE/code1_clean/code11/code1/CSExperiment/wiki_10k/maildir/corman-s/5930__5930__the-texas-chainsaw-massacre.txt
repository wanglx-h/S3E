| runtime = 84 minutes | country = United States | budget = <$300,000 | gross = $30,859,000 | language = English }} File:Chain Saw Massacre House3.jpg '''''The Texas Chainsaw Massacre''''' is a 1974 United States horror movie. It was based loosely on real events, the crimes of Ed Gein, although much of its plot is fictional. It was movie director by Tobe Hooper and stars Marilyn Burns. It is the first movie of The Texas Chainsaw Massacre (franchise).
