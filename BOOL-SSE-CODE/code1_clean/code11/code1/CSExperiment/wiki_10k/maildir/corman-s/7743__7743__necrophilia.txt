Subject: Necrophilia
File:Pietro Pajetta - Der Hass - 1896.jpg showing a man molesting a dead woman]] '''Necrophilia''' is a paraphilia of being sexually attracted to dead bodies. Some necrophiliacs have sexual intercourse with dead bodies. It is usually considered Morality wrong. In addition sexual contact with dead bodies carries the risk of disease if the body has started to decay. It is illegal in many parts of the world, but not all.
