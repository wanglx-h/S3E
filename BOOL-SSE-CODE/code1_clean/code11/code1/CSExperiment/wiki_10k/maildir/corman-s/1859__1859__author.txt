Subject: Author
An '''author''' is a person who makes an original piece of work. Most people use author to mean writer. While a writer is a type of author, authors often do more than write. Anyone who creates something original is an author. These could be written items such as books, stories, or poems. This could also be other types of art, such as sculpture or music. Any type of work can be made by an author. == ''Writer'' and ''author'' == The term author is commonly used to refer to a person who writes as a profession. It is also believed by many that the term is used for writers who are wikt:distinguished and who have had their works Publishing. These explanations are common with people who work with those who write professionally. An author does not always have to be a writer. If a person wikt:dictation a letter to a secretary, then the secretary is a writer, but the person who dictated the letter is the author. == Examples == Other examples of someone being called an ''author'': * In copyright law, the author is the first owner of copyright. These laws also apply to other works of art, such as pictures or music. * In biology, the first person to write an article that gives a name to and describes a type of living thing is the author of that name. * Someone who writes music may be called the author of that piece of music. Another name for an author of music is a composer. == French usage == When talking about movies, the French language word for author, ''auteur'', is often used about someone who has been both the writer and Film director of a movie. == Related pages == * Writer * Journalist * Genre of literature == References == == Other websites == * [http://www.garfield.library.upenn.edu/papers/pricenetworks1965.pdf Networks of scientific papers] Category:Writers Category:Writing occupations
