It is landlocked, meaning that it is not next to the ocean. It is bordered to the North and East by Brazil, the West by Bolivia, and the South and Southeast by Argentina. The main languages are Spanish language and Guaraní language. Paraguay's population is about 6 million. The ethnic makeup of the population is the following: Mestizo (mixed European and Amerindian) 80%, European 20%, unmixed Amerindian 1-3%, Asian 1-4%. Over (20%) of the population lives below the poverty line. Paraguay has two Official language, Spanish language and Guaraní language. Guaraní is recognized as a national language. The capital city is Asuncion. Asuncion was founded in 1537 by a Spaniards named Juan de Salazar. Paraguay was a colony of Spain, but became independent in 1811. From 1865-1870 Paraguay lost land and most of its male population in the War of the Triple Alliance against Brazil, Argentina, and Uruguay. It gained land in the Chaco War against Bolivia in the 1930s. Paraguay's main religion is Roman Catholicism. The weather is humid in the east and dry in the west. ==Government and politics== ===Administrative subdivisions=== Paraguay has 17 Departments of Paraguays and one Capital city district (''Distrito Capital''). The departments are formed by districts.
