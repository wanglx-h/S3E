Including Equatoguinean Spanish (''Español ecuatoguineano''). }} The '''Republic of Equatorial Guinea''' is a nation in west central Africa, and one of the smallest countries in Africa. It borders Cameroon on the north, Gabon on the south and east, and the Gulf of Guinea on the west. The country's territory is both on the continent and on islands. The continental part is known as Río Muni. On one island, Bioko, is the capital, Malabo. Equatorial Guinea and the peoples living there were Colonization by Spain.
