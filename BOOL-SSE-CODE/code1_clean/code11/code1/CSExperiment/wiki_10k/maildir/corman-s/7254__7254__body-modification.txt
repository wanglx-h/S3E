Subject: Body modification
'''Body modification''' (or '''body alteration''') is when a person changes their body in a way that lasts forever or for a long time. There are many different kinds of body modification, like piercing, circumcision, or tattoos. Almost all cultures do some kind of body modification. Religions might tell people that they should change their bodies to look a certain way. Other people with body modifications might just like the way the body modifications look, or how they feel good during sexual intercourse. ==Kinds of body modification== ===Common body modification=== Image:Pode parecer uma semana narcisista.jpg Image:Euler's identity scarification, 3PiCon, Springfield, MA.jpg * ''Body piercing'' is when a hole is made through the skin, and a piece of jewelry is put into the hole. Whole body tatooing and tattooing of the lips and eyes would be thought extreme by some. * ''Scarification'' is when part of the skin is removed to create a scar. * ''Branding'' is when the skin is burned to create a scar. ==="Extreme" body modification=== * ''Tongue splitting'' is when a person's tongue is split in two like a snake's. * ''Subdermal implant'' is when a small object is placed right underneath a person's skin. * ''Transdermal implant'' is when a small object is placed underneath a person's skin, but part of it pokes out of the skin and is visible. * ''Extra ocular implant'' (eye jewellery) is when a tiny chip of metal is placed in someone's eye. ===Other body modification=== * ''Amputation'' is when a body part is cut off. == References == Category:Body art Category:Surgery Category:Skin
