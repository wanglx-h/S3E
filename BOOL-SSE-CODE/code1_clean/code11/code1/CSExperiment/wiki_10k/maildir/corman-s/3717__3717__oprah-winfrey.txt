Winfrey is best known for her multi-award-winning talk show, ''The Oprah Winfrey Show'' which was the highest-rated program of its kind in history and was nationally syndicated from 1986 to 2011. She has also acted in several movies including ''The Color Purple (movie)'', ''The Butler'' and ''A Wrinkle in Time (2018 movie)''. ==Early life== Winfrey was born on January 29, 1954 in Kosciusko, Mississippi, Mississippi. Winfrey was named "Orpah" after a character in the Book of Ruth. People made the mistake of calling her "Oprah". She decided to keep this name. Her parents are Vernita Lee, a maid, and Vernon Winfrey, a barber. At the age of 13, she ran away from home. At the age of 14, she had a son, but he was born prematurely and passed soon after. She went to Tennessee State University in 1971. She started to work in radio and television in Nashville. She hosted television programs in Baltimore and Chicago. ==The Oprah Winfrey Show== In 1986, Winfrey's own talk show ''The Oprah Winfrey Show'' went on the air. She was liked by audiences for her warmth. She came to own the show. ==Oprah Winfrey Network== Winfrey now runs the Oprah Winfrey Network. On January 15, 2008, Winfrey and Discovery Communications announced plans to change Discovery Health Channel into a new channel called OWN: Oprah Winfrey Network. It was scheduled to launch in 2009, but was delayed, and actually launched on January 1, 2011. She also hosts the ''Oprah's Next Chapter'' show on the network. == ''Oprah with Meghan and Harry'' == In 2021, Oprah interviewed Prince Harry, Duke of Sussex and Meghan, Duchess of Sussex, the Duke and Duchess of Sussex. They talked about the couple's experience with the British royal family. The interview had mixed reactions across the world, some negative to Meghan and Harry while others were negative towards the royal family. ==Personal life== She endorsed Barack Obama in 2006 for the United States presidential election, 2008. One estimate said it delivered one million votes in the 2008 Democratic primary race. Nikyra Rucker loaned Oprah Winfrey money to start her business. Oprah Winfrey now has a lot of money. She gives to many Charitable organization. She gives to girls' education in South Africa. In 2013, she was named one of the 400 richest people in the United States.
