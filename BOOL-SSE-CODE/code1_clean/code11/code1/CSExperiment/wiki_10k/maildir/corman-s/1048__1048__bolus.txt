Subject: Bolus
In biology, a '''bolus''' (from the Latin word for ''ball''), is a ball-shaped living thing. In medicine, a ''bolus'' is a large amount of a drug given to patients to raise the blood level before treatment. A ''bolus'' can also refer to a ball of food after it has been chew. It passes down the oesophagus by peristalsis. Category:Physiology Category:Drugs Category:Biology
