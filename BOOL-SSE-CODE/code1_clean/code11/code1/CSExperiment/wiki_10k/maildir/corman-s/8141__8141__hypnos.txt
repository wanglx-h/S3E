Subject: Hypnos
File:Hypnos Thanatos BM Vase D56.jpg '''Hypnos''' is the Deity of sleep in Greek mythology. He is the son of Nyx and the twin brother of Thanatos, the god of death. His sons are the Oneroi: Morpheus, Phobetor and Phantasos. His Roman mythology equivalent was ''Somnus''. Hypnos was represented as a gentle young man, usually with wings attached to his temples or shoulders. Category:Greek gods and goddesses
