Image:White Gumba and Blue Sky.jpg Image:Delphinapterus leucas 2.jpg '''White''' is the brightest color. White light can be made by putting all the other colors of light on the spectrum together. These other colors are red, Orange (color), yellow, green, blue, indigo, and Violet (color). ==Meaning of white== White is linked with light, goodness, innocence, purity, cleanliness and virginity. It is sometimes thought to be the color of perfection. The opposite of black, white usually has a positive connotation. White can stand for a successful beginning. In heraldry, white depicts wikt:faith and purity. In advertising, white is linked with coolness and cleanliness because it is the color of snow. You can use white to show simplicity in high-tech Product (consumer goods). White is an appropriate color for Charitable organization organizations; angels are usually imagined wearing white clothes. White is associated with hospitals, Medical doctor, and cleanliness, so you can use white to show safety when promoting medicine products. White is often linked with low weight, low-fat food, and dairy products. ==Tones of white color comparison chart== White (Hex: FFFFFF) (RGB: 255, 255, 255) Baby Powder (Hex: FEFEFA) (RGB: 254, 254, 250) Snow (Hex: FFFAFA) (RGB: 255, 250, 250) Ivory (Pale Yellow) (Hex: FFFFF0) (RGB: 255, 255, 240) White Smoke (Hex: F5F5F5) (RGB 245, 245, 245) Ghost White (Hex: F8F8FF) (RGB 245, 245, 245) Cosmic Latte (Hex: E1F8E7) (RGB: 225, 248, 231) Gainsboro (Hex: DCDCDC) (RGB: 220, 220, 220) Quartz (Hex: D9D9F3) (RGB: 217, 217, 220) ==Related pages== * List of colors Category:Basic English 850 words
