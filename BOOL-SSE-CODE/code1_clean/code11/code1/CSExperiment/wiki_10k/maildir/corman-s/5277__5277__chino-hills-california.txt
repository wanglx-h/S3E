Subject: Chino Hills, California
'''Chino Hills''' is a city in the U.S. state of California. It is in San Bernardino County, California. In 2020, 78,411 people lived in Chino Hills. It is named for the hills that cover most of the city. ==References== Category:Cities in California Category:Settlements in San Bernardino County, California
