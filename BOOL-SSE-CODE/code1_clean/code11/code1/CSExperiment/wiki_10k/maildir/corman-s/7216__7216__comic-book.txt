Woodcut engraving was a cheaper type of illustration widely used in the 19th century. These are important because they created an understanding that illustrations are sometimes just as important as words. Routledge, p.xv Comic books are published in three different forms. * Single issues that are usually 32 pages long are called comic books. These are printed and bound like a magazine, with the pages folded and stapled together through the centerfold. * Several comics may be put together in a Trade paperback (comics). These have spines like a book and are glued together. * A bigger comic book that is bound like a trade paperback but is all new, instead of collecting older comics, is called a graphic novel. == American comics == American comics are usually about superheroes.
