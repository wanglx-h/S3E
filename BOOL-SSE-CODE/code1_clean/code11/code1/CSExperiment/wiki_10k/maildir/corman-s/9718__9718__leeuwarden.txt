It has about 110,000 inhabitants. It is the capital city of a part of the Netherlands where a lot of people speak Frisian. The name of that province is Friesland. Leeuwarden is called ''Ljouwert'' in Frisian. On 1 January 2014, Boarnsterhim is a part of the municipality of Leeuwarden.
