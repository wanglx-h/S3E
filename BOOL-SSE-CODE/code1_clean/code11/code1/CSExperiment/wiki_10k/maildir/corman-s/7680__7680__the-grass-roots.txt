| Notable_instruments = | Current_members = Mark DawsonJoe DoughertyDusty HanveyLarry Nelson | Past_members = See #Band members }} '''The Grass Roots''' are an United States rock music band. Their peak in popularity was in the 1960s and 1970s. They formed in Los Angeles, California in 1966. Their biggest hit was the song "Midnight Confessions" which charted in the US at #5 in 1968.
