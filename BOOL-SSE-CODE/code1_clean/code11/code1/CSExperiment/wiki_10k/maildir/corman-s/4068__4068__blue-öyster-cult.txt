Subject: Blue Öyster Cult
'''Blue Öyster Cult''' is a psychedelic rock band that formed in Long Island, New York (state) in 1971. They have many well-known songs like ''(Don't Fear) The Reaper'', and ''Burnin' For You''. The group's best known songs are probably: The 1976 single "(Don't Fear) The Reaper" from the album Agents of Fortune (featured in the movie Halloween, and in the TV movie The Stand based on Stephen King's novel of the same name, and in the credits of Peter Jackson's 1996 movie The Frighteners) and the 1981 single "Burnin' for You" from the album Fire of Unknown Origin and "Godzilla" (1977). == Lineup == ===Current members=== The current members of the band are: *Eric Bloom (1971-present) -
