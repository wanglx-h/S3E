File:Winter olympics all cities.svg '''Winter Olympic Games''' were originally considered to be linked with the nation hosting the Summer Olympic Games. The country holding the summer games was preferred as the host for the winter games.Organizing Committee of the XIIth Olympiad. (1940). [http://www.la84foundation.org/6oic/OfficialReports/1940/OR1940.pdf ''Report of the Organizing Committee on Its Work for the XIIth Olympic Games of 1940 in Tokyo Until the Relinquishment,'' p. 127 [151 of 207 PDF]]; retrieved 2012-7-25.
