Subject: Anarcho-punk
File:Anarchy-symbol.svg '''Anarcho-punk''' is a subculture that combines punk music and anarchism politics. Some important anarcho-punk bands include Crass, Conflict (band), Chumbawamba, and Subhumans. Common ideas that many anarcho-punks support are anti-war, animal rights, feminism, environmentalism, Social equality, anti-capitalism and other common anarchist causes.
