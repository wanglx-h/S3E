Subject: Kurów
::''For the place of similar name in New Zealand see: Kurow'' Image:Saint_Michael_Archangel_church_in_Kurów,_Poland.jpg Image:POL Kurów COA.svg '''Kurów''' is a town in south-eastern Poland, near Puławy and Lublin, on the Kurówka River. It is the main village of a gmina within the Lublin Voivodship and has 2826 people living there (as of 2010). The village was built before 1185 and made bigger between 1431 and 1442 with city rights based on the Magdeburg Law. The former President of Poland, Wojciech Jaruzelski was born in Kurów in 1923.
