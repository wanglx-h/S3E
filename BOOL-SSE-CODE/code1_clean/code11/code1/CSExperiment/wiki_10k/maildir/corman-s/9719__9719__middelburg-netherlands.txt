Subject: Middelburg, Netherlands
File:NL - locator map municipality code GM0687 (2016).png Image:Middelburg vlag.svg Image:Coat of arms of Middelburg.svg '''Middelburg''' is a town and municipality on the (former) island of Walcheren, Province of Zeeland, the Netherlands. It is the capital of Zeeland. About 48,000 people were living in the municipality in 2017. About 40,000 people were living in the town in 2011.
