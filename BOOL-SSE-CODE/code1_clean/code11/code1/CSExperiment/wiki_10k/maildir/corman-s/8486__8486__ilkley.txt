Subject: Ilkley
'''Ilkley''' is a tourist town in the City of Bradford in West Yorkshire, England. The biggest attraction there is the wonderful and big ''Ilkley moors''. It is very green and grassy and provides walks for any type of people. One of these walks includes a big mineral and a little rock called the Cow and Calf Rocks. This is one of the bigger wonders of the Yorkshire dales. By these rocks there is a public house called ''The Cow and Calf''. The town is has one railway station where trains run to Leeds and Bradford. Category:Towns in West Yorkshire
