Subject: Half-life
'''Half-life''' can mean different things: *Half-life (physics), a property of radioactive elements *Half-Life (video game), a 1998 video game made by Valve Software *Half-Life (series), the video game series made by Valve Software * Biological half-life, amount of time it takes for half of a drug or chemical to be used up in a living thing
