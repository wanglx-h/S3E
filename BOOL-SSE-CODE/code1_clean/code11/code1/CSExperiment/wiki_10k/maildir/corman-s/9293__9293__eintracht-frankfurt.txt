'''Eintracht Frankfurt''' is a Germany sports club from Frankfurt, best known for the Association football section. The club plays in the German Bundesliga. They have won the German Championship once and German Cup (DFB-Pokal) 5 times (last time in 2018) and the UEFA Europa League twice. They were founded in 1899. The team colours are red, black and white. They play at Deutsche Bank Park (former Waldstadion). The current captain of the side is goalkeeper Kevin Trapp from Germany. The coach of Eintracht Frankfurt is Dino Toppmöller.
