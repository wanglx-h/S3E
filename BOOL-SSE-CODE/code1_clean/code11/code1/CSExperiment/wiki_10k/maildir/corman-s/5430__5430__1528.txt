Subject: 1528
== Events == *June 19 – Battle of Landriano – A French army in Italy under Marshal St. Pol is decisively defeated. *November 6 – Shipwrecked Spanish conquistador Álvar Núñez Cabeza de Vaca becomes the first known European to set foot on Texas. *Andrea Doria revolts against the French and establishes the independence of Genoa. *Montenegro gains autonomy under Turkey power. *Maya civilization drive Spain Conquistadors out of Yucatán. *Spain takes direct control of Acapulco. *Bubonic plague breaks out in England. *Fourth major outbreak of the sweating sickness in England. This time the disease also spreads to northern Europe *St. George's Chapel, Windsor in Windsor Castle completed. *Chateau Fontainebleau in France begun. *Thomas Wolsey founds a college at Ipswich, Suffolk, England. *Michelangelo (Michelangelo Buonarroti) begins work on fortifications of Florence. *Baldassare Castiglione publishes The Book of the Courtier.
