== Events == * February to August – Explorer Abel Tasman's second expedition for the Dutch East India Company maps the north coast of Australia. * April – A popular China Uprising led by Li Zicheng enter and conquer Beijing, prompting Chongzhen Emperor of China, the last emperor of the Ming Dynasty, to commit suicide. * June 6 – The invading Manchu army, with the help of Ming Dynasty general Wu Sangui, captures Beijing, China. This marks the beginning of the Qing Dynasty (also known as the Manchu Dynasty), the last Dynasties in Chinese history. * July 2 – Battle of Marston Moor, a crushing victory for the Roundhead side of the English Civil War, ends Charles I of England's hold on the north of England. * September 1 – Battle of Tippermuir, James Graham, 1st Marquess of Montrose defeats Earl of Wemyss's Covenanters, reviving Royalist cause in Scotland * September 2 – Second Battle of Lostwithiel, the last major victory for Charles I and the Cavaliers side in the English Civil War. * September 15 – Pope Innocent X is elected to the Papacy, in Rome. * November 23 – ''Areopagitica'' by John Milton is published. * December – Black Death breaks out in Edinburgh.
