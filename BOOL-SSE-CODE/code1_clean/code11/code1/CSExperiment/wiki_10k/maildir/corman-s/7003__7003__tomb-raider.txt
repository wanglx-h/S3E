'''''Tomb Raider''''' is a video game series. Originally the games were made by Core Design. Now they are being made by Crystal Dynamics. The first game was sold in 1996 for PC, PlayStation and Sega Saturn. The main character of the game is a woman called ''Lara Croft''. She is an archeologist and explores ancient places, searching old relics, often fighting a great danger to the world. Since 2001, Paramount Pictures has made two movies like the game, starring Angelina Jolie. And 2018 movies, Angelina Jolie has been switch into Alicia Vikander. ==Games in the series== The first six games in the series was made by Core Design.
