Subject: Jordanhill railway station
'''Jordanhill railway station''' is in a suburb of Glasgow, Scotland. It is a station where passenger trains stop on the First ScotRail railway line. The station opened on August 1, 1887 as part of a different railway line. On March 2, 2006, a new article on the Jordanhill railway station became Wikipedia's million article.
