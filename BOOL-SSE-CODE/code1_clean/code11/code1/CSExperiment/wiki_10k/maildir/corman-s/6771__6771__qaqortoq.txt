With a population of 3,100, it is South Greenland's biggest town. It is the fourth biggest List of towns in Greenland. The name Qaqortoq is Western Greenlandic language, and it means "white". Qaqortoq also is the seat of the municipality of Qaqortoq which apart from the town itself includes three small settlements. Qaqortoq was founded by Norway Anders Olsen in 1775.
