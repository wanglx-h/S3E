The '''1720s''' was a decade that began on 1 January 1720 and ended on 31 December 1729. It is distinct from the decade known as the '''''173th decade''''' which began on January 1, 1721 and ended on December 31, 1730. == Events and trends == * The oldest pianos still in existence today are from the 1720s. == World leaders == * Louis XV of France, King of France (king from 1715 to 1774) * Shahs of the Safavid dynasty in Persia: # Husayn, 1694–1722 # Tahmasp II, 1723–1732 * George I of Great Britain, List of British monarchs (king from 1714 to 1727) * George II of Great Britain, List of British monarchs (king from 1727 to 1760) * Philip V of Spain, King of Spain (king from 1700 to 1746) ==References== Category:1720s
