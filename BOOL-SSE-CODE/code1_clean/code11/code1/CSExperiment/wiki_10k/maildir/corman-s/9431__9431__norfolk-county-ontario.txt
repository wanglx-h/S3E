Subject: Norfolk County, Ontario
'''Norfolk County''' is a city in Ontario, Canada that is largely agriculture. It has many primary schools and high schools and the majority of citizens are Christian. Its communities include: * Simcoe, Ontario * Delhi, Ontario * Port Dover, Ontario * Pine Grove * Gilbertville, Ontario * Waterford The current mayor is Amy Martin who took office in 2022.
