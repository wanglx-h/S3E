Image:Chocolate Sour-Cream Icing on cake.JPG icing]] '''Icing''' is a paste (food) that is usually made from sugar. It is also called "frosting". It is put on cakes to add flavour. Many kinds of cake have icing, especially cakes made for special occasions like birthday cakes, wedding cakes and Christmas cake. Category:Food ingredients
