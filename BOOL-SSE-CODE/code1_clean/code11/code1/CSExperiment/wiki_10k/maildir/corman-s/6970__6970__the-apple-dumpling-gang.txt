| runtime = 100 minutes | country = United States | language = English | gross = $36,853,000 }} '''''The Apple Dumpling Gang''''' is a Walt Disney Pictures movie from 1975, in which three orphan children find gold during California's Gold Rush. Tim Conway and Don Knotts also star in it as two gangsters.
