Subject: Stage name
A '''stage name''' is a name an entertainer (such as a movie star or a musician) uses instead of their real name. Famous examples include Actor Marilyn Monroe, singer Lady Gaga and Hip hop Nicki Minaj. takes in the place of their real or birth name, they might like this name better than their real one or it's easier for people to remember. Professional wrestlers use stage names also, in their case it is called a "ring name". Actors and actresses choose stage names for a variety of Reason.
