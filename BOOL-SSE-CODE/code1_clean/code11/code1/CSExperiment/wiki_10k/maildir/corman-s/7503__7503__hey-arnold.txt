It was created by Craig Bartlett. The series was first Broadcasting on Nickelodeon from August 7, 1996, to June 8, 2004. It was about a boy with a football-shaped head named Arnold, his "gang" of friends and their lives in the fictional universe of Hillwood. Each episode of ''Hey Arnold!'' ran for about 15 minute. In 2002, Nickelodeon made a movie, Hey Arnold! (movie) based on the series. Arnold's best friends in his gang are Gerald, Sid, Stinky, Eugene, Rhonda, Phoebe, Lila, and Curly.
