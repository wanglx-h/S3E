The agency is also tasked with protecting U.S. communications networks and information systems. NSA was started in 1952, and its main office is in Maryland. Its stated wikt:goal is to protect the US people by *Worldwide "eavesdropping," secretly listen to what people are saying in other country. It monitors, collects, codebreaking, translation and analysis information and data.
