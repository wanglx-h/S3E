Subject: United Nations General Assembly
The '''United Nations General Assembly''' is made up of all United Nations Nation. The Assembly meets once a year, which usually begins on the third Tuesday in September and ends in mid-December. The first meeting was held on 10 January 1946. File:Mahathir Mohamad addressing the United Nations General Assembly (September 25 2003).jpg Mahathir Mohamad spoke at the United Nations General Assembly on 25 September 2003.]] Vote in the General Assembly on important questions, for example suggestions on world peace, human rights and wikt:security, is by two-thirds of those present and voting. Other questions are decided by popular vote. Each member country has one vote. At present, the Holy See (Vatican City) and Palestine are the only two observer states at the United Nations.
