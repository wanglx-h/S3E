File:Kristy swanson.jpg '''Renee Kristen "Kristy" Swanson''' (born December 19, 1969) is an Americans actor. She is best known for playing the main fictional character in the movie ''Buffy the Vampire Slayer (movie)''. She has also starred in the movie ''Flowers in the Attic'' as well as many movies and television programs.
