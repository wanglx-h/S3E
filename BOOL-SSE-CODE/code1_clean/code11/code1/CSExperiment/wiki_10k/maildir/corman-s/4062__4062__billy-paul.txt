Image:Billy Paul.jpg '''Billy Paul''' ('''Paul Williams'''; December 1, 1934 April 24, 2016) was an Americans R&B singer. His biggest hit was "Me and Mrs. Jones". He was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
