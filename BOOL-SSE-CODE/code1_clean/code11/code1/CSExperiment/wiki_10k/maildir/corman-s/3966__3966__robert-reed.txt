He is best known for his role as Mike Brady on ''The Brady Bunch''. He also played major roles in ''The Defenders (1961 TV series)'' and ''Rich Man, Poor Man (TV miniseries)''. He was nominated for three Primetime Emmy Awards during the 1970s. Reed was born John Robert Rietz in Highland Park, Illinois. He also grew up in Muskogee, Oklahoma and Navasota, Texas. Reed was gay but kept this Closeted. He died on May 12, 1992, in Pasadena, California.
