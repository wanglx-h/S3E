Subject: Pulitzer Prize
File:Gen pulitzer.jpg The '''Pulitzer Prize''' is an United States prize given to journalism and literature. The prize is considered a very high prize. It is also given for musical composition of very high standard. Originally started by Joseph Pulitzer, Columbia University in New York City now awards the prize. Prizes are given in twenty-one category. The winner gets $10,000 cash award and a certificate. One of the twenty-one prizes is the Pulitzer Prize for Fiction. Category:Pulitzer Prizes
