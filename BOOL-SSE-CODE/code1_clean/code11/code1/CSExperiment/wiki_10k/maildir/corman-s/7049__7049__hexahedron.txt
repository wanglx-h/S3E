Subject: Hexahedron
A '''hexahedron''' (plural: hexahedra) is any polyhedron with six Face (geometry). A cube, for example, is a Regular polyhedron hexahedron with all its faces Square (geometry), and three squares around each Vertex (geometry). There are seven topologically distinct ''convex'' hexahedra,[http://www.numericana.com/data/polycount.htm Counting polyhedra] one of which exists in two mirror image forms.
