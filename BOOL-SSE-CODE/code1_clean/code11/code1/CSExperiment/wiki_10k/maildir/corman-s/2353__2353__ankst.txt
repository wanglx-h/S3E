Subject: Ankst
'''Ankst''' is a Wales independent record label formed in 1988 at University of Wales Aberystwyth. Ankst has been the launch-pad for several popular Welsh artists, including Super Furry Animals and Gorky's Zygotic Mynci. The label is now based in Pentraeth on Anglesey. ==Other websites== *[http://www.ankst.co.uk/ Official website] Category:British record labels Category:1988 establishments in the United Kingdom Category:20th-century establishments in Wales
