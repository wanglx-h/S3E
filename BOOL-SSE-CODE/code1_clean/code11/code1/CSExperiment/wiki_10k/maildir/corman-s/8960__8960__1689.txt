== Events == * Louis XIV of France passed the "''Code Noir''," allowing the full use of slaves in the French colonies. * January 11 – The Parliament of England declares King James II of England deposed. * February 13 – William III of England and Mary II of England are proclaimed co-rulers of Kingdom of England, Scotland and Ireland. Scotland and Ireland do not yet recognize them. * April 11 – The Estates of Scotland declare King James VII of Scotland deposed. * April 11 – Crowning of co-rulers King William III of England and Mary II of England as King and Queen of England, Scotland and Ireland. Ireland does not recognize them yet. * May 12 – King William's War: William III of England joins the League of Augsburg starting the war. * May 24 – The Act of Toleration passes the English Parliament protecting Protestantism (Roman Catholics are intentionally excluded). * May 25 – Last collection of the Hearth#Taxation n England and Wales. It was abolished by William III of England * May 31 – Leisler's Rebellion – Calvinist Jacob Leisler deposes lieutenant governor Francis Nicholson and assumes control of New York (state) colony * July 27 – Glorious Revolution: Battle of Killiecrankie ends * August 5 – 1,500 Iroquois attack village of Lachine, Quebec, in New France. * August 27 – China and Russia signed the Treaty of Nerchinsk. * December 16 – The official declaration of the English Bill of Rights Category:1689
