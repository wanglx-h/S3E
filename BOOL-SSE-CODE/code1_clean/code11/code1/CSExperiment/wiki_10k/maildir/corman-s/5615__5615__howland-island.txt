File:Howland Island-CIA WFB Map.png '''Howland Island''' is a small island in the north Pacific Ocean, just north of the Equator. It is owned by the United States.[https://www.cia.gov/library/publications/the-world-factbook/geos/um.html "Howland Island" at ''CIA World Factbook''] ; excerpt, "no indigenous inhabitants"; retrieved 2013-4-19. == Weather == Howland island is a very hot and sunny island with wind blowing almost all the time.
