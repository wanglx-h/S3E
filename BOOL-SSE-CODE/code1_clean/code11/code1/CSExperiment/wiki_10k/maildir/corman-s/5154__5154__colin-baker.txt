Subject: Colin Baker
File:Colin Carrot cropped.JPG '''Colin Baker''' (born 8 June 1943) is an England actor. He is most famous as the Sixth Doctor on ''Doctor Who'' from 1984 to 1986. He has no relation to Tom Baker, who played the fourth incarnation of the Doctor.
