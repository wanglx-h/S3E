Subject: Antipatrid dynasty
The '''Antipatrid dynasty''' was a Macedonian dynasty. It was created by Cassander in 302 BC, who was the son of Antipater. However it was not a long dynasty. It ended in 294 BC when it was taken over by the Antigonid dynasty. ==Kings== * Antipater, regent, not king *Cassander (302-297 BC) *Alexander V of Macedon (297-296 BC) *Philip IV of Macedon (296-294 BC) *Antipater II of Macedon (296-294, 279 BC) *Sosthenes of Macedon (279-277 BC) ==Other websites== * [http://www.macedonia.com/english/kings.html List of the Kings of Macedonia] == References == Category:Ancient Greek dynasties
