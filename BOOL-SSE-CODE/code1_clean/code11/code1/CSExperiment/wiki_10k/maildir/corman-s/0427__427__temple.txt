File:Roma-tempio di antonio-faustina.jpg, Italy.]] A '''temple''' is a building where people go to practice their religion. In a temple people may perform religious rituals, ceremony, and pray. Thus, a temple is a general term for a house of worship. Christianity usually call their religious buildings Church (building).
