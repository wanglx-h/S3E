File:Location of Savitaipale in Finland.png Image:Savitaipale.vaakuna.svg '''Savitaipale''' is a Finland municipality in the South Karelia region. As of January 2014, more than 3,700 people lived in Savitaipale. Savitaipale has an area of 690.78km². 151.63km² of it is water. The municipality is on the coast of the Lake Kuolimo. Helsinki is 220km away. Neighbouring municipalities are Kouvola, Lemi, Luumäki, Mikkeli, Mäntyharju, Puumala and Taipalsaari.
