Subject: Unforgiven
'''''Unforgiven''''' is a 1992 United States Western (genre) movie movie director by Clint Eastwood. It stars Eastwood, Gene Hackman, Morgan Freeman and Richard Harris. It ranks #98 on AFI's 100 Years... 100 Movies and won the Academy Award for Best Picture.
