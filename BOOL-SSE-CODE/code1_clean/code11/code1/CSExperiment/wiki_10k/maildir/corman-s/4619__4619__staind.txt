The band was started in 1994. Its first album, ''Tormented'' was released in 1996. Staind's newest album, ''The Illusion of Progress'' was released on August 19, 2008. Some of its biggest hits have been ''Outside'', ''It's Been Awhile'', and ''So Far Away''.
