Subject: Bolton Wanderers F.C.
'''Bolton Wanderers F.C.''' is an England Association football club. It is based 15 miles north of Manchester. The club plays in the English football league 1, the third tier league in England. Their home is the University of Bolton Stadium. It holds around 28,723 people. Their manager is Ian Evett. At home they play in a white shirt, navy blue shorts & white socks.
