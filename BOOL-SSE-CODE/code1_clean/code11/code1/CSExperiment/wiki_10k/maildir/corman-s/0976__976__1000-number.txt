Subject: 1000 (number)
'''1000''' ('''1,000''', '''one thousand''' or '''thousand''' for short) is the natural number after 999 and before 1001 (number). One thousand thousands is known as a million. In Roman numeral, 1000 is written as M. ==Examples== * The number of Year in a millennium * The number of Gram in a kilogram * The number of Millimetre in a Metre Category:Integers
