== Events == * January 24 – King Charles II of England disbands Parliament * June 22 – the Duke of Monmouth subdues an insurrection of Scottish Covenanters at Birthwell Bridge * August 7 – The brigantine ''Le Griffon'', which was commissioned by René Robert Cavelier, Sieur de La Salle, is towed to the southern end of the Niagara River, to become the first ship to sail the upper Great Lakes (North America). * December 24 – Domenico Sarro, a composer from Italy, was born. * Battle of Bothwell Bridge * Habeas Corpus Act 1679 passed in England. * European explorers discover Niagara Falls.
