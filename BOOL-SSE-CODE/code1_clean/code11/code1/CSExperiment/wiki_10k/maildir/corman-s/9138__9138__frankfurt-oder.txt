'Frankfurt at the Oder') is a town in Germany. It lies on the Oder river, which marks the current border between Germany and Poland (The so-called Oder-Neisse line). It's in the east of the States of Germany of Brandenburg. In January 1999, the town added a prefix ''Kleiststadt'' to refer to Heinrich von Kleist, who was born there in 1777.
