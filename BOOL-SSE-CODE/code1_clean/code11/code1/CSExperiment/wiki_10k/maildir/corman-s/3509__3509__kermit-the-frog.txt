Subject: Kermit the Frog
'''Kermit the Frog''' is a Muppet character created by Jim Henson. Kermit was Henson's trademark Muppet character. Kermit was originally performed by Henson himself. After Henson's death, Kermit was played by Steve Whitmire. In 2017, Whitmire stopped working with the Muppets. Matt Vogel replaced him and played Kermit until 2022. Kermit has appeared on many TV shows, including ''Sesame Street'', ''The Muppet Show'', Sam and Friends, The Ed Sullivan Show, and ''Muppet Babies''.
