Subject: Segura
Image:Murcia - Puente Viejo.jpg city]] '''Segura''' is a Spain river that is 325 kilometers (200 miles) long. It flows through the provinces of Jaen province, Albacete province, Murcia (autonomous community), and Alicante province, and the cities of Cieza, Molina de Segura, Murcia, Orihuela, Rojales, and Guardamar. It then flows into the Mediterranean Sea. Category:Rivers of Spain
