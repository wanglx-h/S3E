Subject: Audience
File:USMC-07786.jpg An '''audience''' is a group of wikt:people that see, hear or otherwise experience the same thing. It may be many different kinds of thing, such as a Play (theatre), a movie, a book, or a broadcast. Audience members participate in different ways in different kinds of art; some live performance events invite the audience to be part of the performance, while others allow only applause and criticism. Mass media seldom provide for audience participation. An audience at a sporting event are called ''spectators'' or a ''live audience'' Category:Performing arts Category:Entertainment Category:Observation
