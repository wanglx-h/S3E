The city has a famous snow festival in winter, it has a growing population of about 1,800,000 wikt:people. Its area is . There are a lot of fish markets in Sapporo. A beer brand named "Sapporo" was named after this city. Ramen is a very popular food in Sapporo. The city has a subway system. Some famous landmarks are the Sapporo TV Tower, and the Sapporo Clock Tower that is in the center of the city. There are several universities here, including Hokkaido University.
