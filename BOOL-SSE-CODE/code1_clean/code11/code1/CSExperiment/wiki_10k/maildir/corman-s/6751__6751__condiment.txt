Subject: Condiment
File:ImageMoutarde1.jpg Image:Mustard French condiment.jpg A '''condiment''' is a prepared food that is put to other food, to make the food better or taste different. It usually is in the form of a sauce or a powder. Salt and Black pepper are examples of well-known condiments. Ketchup, mustard, relish, mayonnaise, horseradish, soy sauce and Worcestershire sauce are also well-known condiments. Category:Condiments
