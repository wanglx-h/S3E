Subject: Nezahualcoyotl
'''Nezahualcoyotl''' (1402 - 1472) was king of Texcoco, a part of the Aztecs empire. He was also a famous poet. The name Nezahualcoyotl is Nahuatl language, it means "hungry coyote".
