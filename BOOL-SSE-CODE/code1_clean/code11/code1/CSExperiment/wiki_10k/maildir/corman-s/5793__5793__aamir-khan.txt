Khan first appeared in ''Yaadon Ki Baaraat'' (1973) as a child actor. His first big starring role was in ''Qayamat Se Qayamat Tak'' (1988). Khan was born to a Muslim family. Another of his best-known adult roles was starring in ''Lagaan: Once Upon a Time in India'' (2001), which was nominated for an Academy Award. Khan was credited as movie producer for the movie, with his first wife (1986-2002), Reena Datta, executive producer. Khan met his future second wife (2005-), Kiran Rao, on the set, where she was an assistant director. Their first movie released in 2006 is ''Rang De Basanti'' after his divorce with Reena Dutta.
