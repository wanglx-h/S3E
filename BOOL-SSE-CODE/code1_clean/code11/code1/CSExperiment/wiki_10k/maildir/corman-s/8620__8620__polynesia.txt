'''Polynesia''' is a group of over 1,000 islands in the Pacific Ocean. == Triangle == File:Pacific Culture Areas.png Polynesia is the islands in a triangle in the Pacific Ocean. The top of the triangle is Hawai'i, the bottom left is New Zealand and the bottom right is Easter Island.
