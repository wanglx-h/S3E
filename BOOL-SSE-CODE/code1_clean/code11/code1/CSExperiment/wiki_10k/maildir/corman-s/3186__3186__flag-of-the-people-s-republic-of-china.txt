Subject: Flag of the People's Republic of China
File:Flag of the People's Republic of China.svg The '''Flag of China''' has one big Star (shape) and four small yellow stars on a red background. The big star stands for the communist government, and the four small stars stand for the workers, peasants, middle-class citizens and soldiers. The red background stands for the blood lost while the history of China was made. == Sources == == Other websites == * [https://www.chinese-flag.org/ Chinese-flag.org] Category:National flags Category:National symbols of the People's Republic of China
