|birth_place = Białogard, People's Republic of Poland |party = Independent (politician) (1995–present) | profession = |spouse = Jolanta Kwaśniewska |children = Aleksandra Kwaśniewska |otherparty = Polish United Workers' Party (1977–1990)Social Democracy of the Republic of Poland (1990–1995) |alma_mater = University of Gdańsk |signature = Aleksander Kwaśniewski Signature.svg }} '''Aleksander Kwaśniewski''' (; born 15 November 1954) is a Poland politician. He served as President for two terms (1995-2000 and 2000-2005). He was born in Białogard in Northwest Poland. Before became a politician, he was a journalist. Before became president, he was minister and deputy to the Sejm. In the late 1980s, he participated in the Polish Round Table Agreement.
