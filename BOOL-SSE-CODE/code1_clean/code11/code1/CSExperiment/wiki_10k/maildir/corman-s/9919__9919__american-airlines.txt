(AA) is the largest airline of the United States. It is based in Fort Worth, Texas It was founded in 1930 with the name ''American Airways''. 82 small airlines were combined in 1930 to form American. In 1934, the name was officially changed to ''American Airlines''. American was the first airline to have computerized reservations, a frequent-flyer program and an airline lounge.[https://web.archive.org/web/20091128133653/https://www.aa.com/i18n/amrcorp/corporateInformation/facts/admiralshistory.jsp https://www.aa.com/i18n/amrcorp/corporateInformation/facts/admiralshistory.jsp] In 1999, American became a founding member of Oneworld alongside British Airways, Canadian Airlines (until 2000), Cathay Pacific and Qantas. In 2001, American bought TWA. In 2011, American filed for bankruptcy. American was combined with US Airways in 2013. In 2016, American will introduce a new no-frills fare.
