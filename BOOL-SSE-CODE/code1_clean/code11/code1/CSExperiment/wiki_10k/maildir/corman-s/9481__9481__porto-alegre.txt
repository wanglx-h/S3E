Subject: Porto Alegre
File:Lago do parque da redenção.JPG Image:Bandeira de Porto Alegre.svg Image:Brasão de Porto Alegre.svg '''Porto Alegre''' is a Brazilian city, capital of the state of Rio Grande do Sul. It has about 1.4 million inhabitants and an area of 496.8 km². Its main famous park is the 'Parque Dos Expedicionários' also known as 'Parque da Redenção' (Redemption Park, in English). Porto Alegre became known all over the world by The Social World Forum. Also known worldwide by the hottest city in the world at February, 2009.
