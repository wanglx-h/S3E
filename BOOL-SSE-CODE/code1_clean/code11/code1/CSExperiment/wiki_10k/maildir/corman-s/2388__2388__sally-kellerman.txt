File:Sally Kellerman at The Rose premier 1979 cropped.jpg '''Sally Clare Kellerman''' (June 2, 1937 February 24, 2022) was an Americans movie Actor. She was born in Long Beach, California. Her acting performances include the pilot for the original ''Star Trek'' television programme, and the movies ''MASH'' (1970) and ''Back to School'' (1986). For her role in ''MASH'', she was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress.
