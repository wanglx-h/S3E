Subject: Race
'''Race''' has many meanings in English language. It can mean: *To go very fast – ''he raced to the store'' *A competition between people - ''the race for the US presidency'' *Racing, a competition about speed – ''the Tour de France is an important bicycle race'' *Race (biology), a group smaller than a species. Members of different races can have sex and make babies, but they are separate (usually because they live in different places) - ''a large race of birds lives on this island'' *Race (sociology), wikt:subdivision of the human species based on wikt:distinguishing physical characteristics that are passed on to wikt:succeeding wikt:generations. *Race (anthropology) groups humans are often divided into based on physical traits often common among shared ancestry. *The ''human race'' means all people - ''the human race is destroying the environment''
