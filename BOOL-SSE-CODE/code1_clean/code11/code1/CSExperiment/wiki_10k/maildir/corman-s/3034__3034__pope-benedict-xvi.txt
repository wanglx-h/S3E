| birth_place = Marktl, Weimar Republic | death_date = | death_place = Mater Ecclesiae, Vatican City | parents = Joseph Ratzinger, Sr. and Maria Ratzinger (née Peintner) | residence = Mater Ecclesiae | nationality = German (with Vatican citizenship) | previous_post = Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Munich and Freising Cardinal-Priest of Santa Maria Consolatrice al Tiburtino President of the International Theological Commission Prefect of the Congregation for the Doctrine of the Faith President of the Pontifical Biblical Commission Cardinal-Bishop of Roman Catholic Suburbicarian Diocese of Velletri-Segni Dean of the College of Cardinals Bishop of Ostia | motto = ''Cooperatores veritatis'' (cooperators of the truth) | signature = Pope Benedict XVI Signature.svg | coat_of_arms = Coat of arms of Benedictus XVI.svg | other = Benedict | buried = St. Peter's Basilica, Vatican City }} '''Pope Benedict XVI''' (; ; , born '''Joseph Aloisius Ratzinger'''; 16 April 1927 31 December 2022) was a Pope of the Catholic Church. He served as the 265th Pope from 2005 to 2013. In that position, he was both the leader of the Catholic Church and ruler of the Vatican City State. Benedict was elected on 19 April 2005 in a papal conclave, 2005 after the death of Pope John Paul II. He celebrated his papal inauguration Mass (liturgy) on 24 April 2005. He took possession of his cathedral, the Archbasilica of St. John Lateran, on 7 May 2005. On 11 February 2013, Benedict announced his resignation in a speech in Latin before the Cardinal (Catholicism). His reason was a "lack of strength of mind and body" because of his old age. Resignation of Pope Benedict XVI became effective on 28 February 2013. He was the first pope to resign since Pope Gregory XII in 1415. He was the first to resign by choice since Pope Celestine V in 1294. As Pope Emeritus, Benedict retained the Style (form of address) of ''His Holiness'', and the title of ''Pope''. He continued to dress in the papal colour of white up until his death in 2022. He was succeeded by Pope Francis on 13 March 2013. He moved into the newly renovated ''Mater Ecclesiae'' monastery for his retirement on 2 May 2013.
