Bad Aibling is a health resort (peat pulp bath and mineral water). == History == File:Merian aybling 1644.png 1644)]] In 804, Bad Aibling was mentioned for the first time. After the World War II Bad Aibling has evolved to a major centre for intelligence organizations and secret services. In 2004 the US American Bad Aibling Echelon station closed after decades of operation. In 2016, Bad Aibling rail accident. == Number of people == In the town live about 18,000 people.
