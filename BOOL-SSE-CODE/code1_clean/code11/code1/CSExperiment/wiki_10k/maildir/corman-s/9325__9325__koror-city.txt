Subject: Koror City
'''Koror''' is the former capital city of Palau. Palau is divided into sixteen states, and Koror is one of the states in Palau. The state of Koror has about 90% of the population of the country. Koror is also the largest town, with a population of 14,000 (2004). Category:Capital cities in Oceania Category:Settlements in Palau
