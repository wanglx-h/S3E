Subject: Royal family
File:Royal Family of Bhutan (cropped).jpg]] A '''royal family''' is the family of a monarch (king or queen of a country). Or, it is someone who is related to a queen, king or a monarch. A royal family may be called "House of" something, as in "House of Habsburg". Some royal families such as the British Royal Family participate in various ceremonies and royal duties.
