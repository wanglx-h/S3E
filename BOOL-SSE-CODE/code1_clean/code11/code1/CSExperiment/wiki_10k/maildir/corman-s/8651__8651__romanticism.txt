File:Caspar David Friedrich 032.jpg, ''Wanderer above the Sea of Fog,'' 38.58 Ã— 29.13 inches, 1818, Oil on canvas, ''Kunsthalle Hamburg''.]] '''Romanticism''', or the '''Romantic movement''', was a movement, or style of art, literature and music in the late 18th and the early 19th century in Europe. The movement believed that feelings, imagination, nature, human life, freedom of expression, individualism and old folklore, such as legends and fairy tales, were important. It was a reaction to the aristocracy social and political ideas of the Age of Enlightenment and of the Industrial Revolution. It was also a reaction against turning nature into Natural sciences. The movement showed most strongly in arts like music and literature.
