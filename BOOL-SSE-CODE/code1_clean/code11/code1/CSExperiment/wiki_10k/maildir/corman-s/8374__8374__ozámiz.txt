Subject: Ozámiz
Image:Ozamiz-City.png '''Ozámiz''' is a city in the Philippines, in the province of Misamis Occidental. There were 141,828 people living there according to the 2015 census. Ozámiz is named after Senator José Ozámiz, a hero of the Philippine resistance against the Japanese during World War 2. == Other websites == * [http://www.ozamizcity.com/ Official website of the city] Category:Cities in the Philippines Category:Misamis Occidental
