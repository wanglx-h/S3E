He was born in Dublin, Ireland. Becket wrote Novel, Play (theatre) and poetry. He also Translation other famous works of literature. He was given the Nobel Prize for Literature in 1969. His best-known play is Waiting for Godot It has often been acted on stage and on TV. Beckett was stabbed in Paris in 1938. He Death of breathing problems in Paris in 1989. Many writers of plays (playwrights) and others think he is one of the most important writers of the 20th century. There have been many books written about him.
