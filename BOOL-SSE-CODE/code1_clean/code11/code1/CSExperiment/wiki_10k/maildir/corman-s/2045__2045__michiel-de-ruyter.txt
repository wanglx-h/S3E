File:Hendrick Berckman - Portrait of Michiel de Ruyter (1607-1676).jpg '''Michiel Adriaenszoon de Ruyter''' (1607 â€“ 1676) was a famous Netherlands admiral. De Ruyter was born in Vlissingen. He fought the England in the three Anglo-Dutch Wars and scored several huge victories. He won amongst others the Four Days Battle and the Raid on Medway, close to London, the English Capital (city). He also fought battles against Sweden and France. He died because of injuries he got on a battle against France, he is buried in Amsterdam.
