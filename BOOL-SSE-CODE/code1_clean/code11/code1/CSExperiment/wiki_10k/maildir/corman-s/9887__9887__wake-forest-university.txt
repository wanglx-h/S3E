It was started in 1834 in the eastern North Carolina town of Wake Forest. The university was moved to Winston-Salem in 1956. A private school for both men and women, it has been a university since 1967. The wikt:component of Wake Forest University are the Undergraduate College and the Wayne Calloway School of Business and Accounting, the School of Law, Wake Forest School of Medicine, the Babcock Graduate School of Management, the Graduate School of Arts and Sciences, and the Religion School. == Sports == Wake Forest's athletics teams are called the ''Deamon Deacons'' and they have sixteen varsity teams.
