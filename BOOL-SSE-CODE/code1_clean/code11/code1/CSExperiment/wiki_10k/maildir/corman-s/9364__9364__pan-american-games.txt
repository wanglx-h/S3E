Subject: Pan American Games
The '''Pan American Games''' are a multi-sport event. They are held every four years between competitors from all nations of the Americas. == History == The Pan American Games were going to be started in 1932, but World War II caused the first Pan American Games to happen on 1951. From that time, the Games are held every four years. More than 5000 athletes from more than 40 countries have attended the recent Games.
