Subject: 1478
== Events == * February 18 – George, Duke of Clarence, convicted of treason against his older brother Edward IV of England, is privately executed in the Tower of London. * April 26 – The Pazzi attack Lorenzo de' Medici and kill his brother Giuliano di Piero de' Medici during High Mass in the Santa Maria del Fiore. * December 28 – Battle of Giornico – Swiss troops defeat the Milanese. * Lorenzo de' Medici becomes sole ruler of Florence, Italy * Grand Prince Ivan III of Moscow conquers Novgorod * Vladislav II of Bohemia makes peace with Hungary * Eskender succeeds his father Baeda Maryam as Emperor of Ethiopia at the age of six.
