File:Blick über Linz von der Franz-Josefs-Warte.jpg File:Flag of Linz.gif File:AUT Linz COA.svg '''Linz''' is a city in Austria with nearly 200,000 inhabitants. It has a humid continental climate (Dfb in the Köppen climate classification). It is the third largest city in Austria, after Vienna and Graz. Linz is the capital city and a district of Upper Austria.
