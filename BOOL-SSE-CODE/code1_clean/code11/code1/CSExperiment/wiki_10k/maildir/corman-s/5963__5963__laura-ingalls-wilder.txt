Subject: Laura Ingalls Wilder
'''Laura Ingalls Wilder''' (February 7, 1867 - February 10, 1957) was an Americans writer. She was born in Wisconsin and spent her childhood in Missouri and Kansas. She became a teacher in South Dakota at age 16. Wilder is best known as the author of the ''Little House on the Prairie'' series of books. A television series, ''Little House On the Prairie'', was inspired by the story of her life as told in her books.
