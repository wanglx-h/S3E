Subject: 50 (number)
'''50''' ('''fifty''') is the integer that is after '''49 (number)''' and before '''51 (number)'''. The prime factors of fifty are 2 and 5. (2 * 5 * 5 = 50) The factors of 50 are 1 (number), 2 (number), 5 (number), 10 (number), 25 (number), and 50. 5*10=50. 50 is the smallest number that can be written as the sum of two squares in two distinct ways: 50=12+72=52+52. Also, 50 is one-half of 100 or half of a century (a century is 100 years). Category:Integers
