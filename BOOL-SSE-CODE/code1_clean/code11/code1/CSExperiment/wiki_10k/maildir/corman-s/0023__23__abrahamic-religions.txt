Subject: Abrahamic religions
The '''Abrahamic religions''', are a group of religious communities of faith that claim descent from the Judaism of the ancient Israelites and the worship of the Yahweh of Abraham. The Abrahamic religions are Monotheism. The term derives from patriarch Abraham, a major biblical figure from The Hebrew Bible.
