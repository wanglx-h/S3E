Subject: Marmalade
Image:Marmalade spread on bread.jpg '''Marmalade''' is a topping, like a jam or jelly, that is usually made with Orange (fruit). Marmalade can also be made from lemons, limes, grapefruits, or a combination of citrus fruits. The word Marmalade comes from the Galician language word "marmelada" meaning ''quince jam''. == Related pages == * Marmalade (Candy) == Other websites == Category:Spreads
