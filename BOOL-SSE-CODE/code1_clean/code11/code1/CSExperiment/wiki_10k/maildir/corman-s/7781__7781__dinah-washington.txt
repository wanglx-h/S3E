Subject: Dinah Washington
File:Dinah Washington 1962.jpg '''Dinah Washington''' (born '''Ruth Lee Jones'''; August 24, 1924 - December 14, 1963) was an Americans R&B and jazz music singer. She was an African American who was born in Tuscaloosa, Alabama and raised in Chicago.
