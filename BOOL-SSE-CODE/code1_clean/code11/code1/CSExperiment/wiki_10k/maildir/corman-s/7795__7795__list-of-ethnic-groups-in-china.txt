Subject: List of ethnic groups in China
File:Ethnolinguistic map of China 1983.png map of China]] There are many China population. They all have many different cultures, history and religion. The Government of China officially says there are 56 small groups of people. They are called ''zú'' or ''mínzú'' in China. Of these, almost all of them (about 90%) are Han people (&#27721;族 : Hàn Zú).
