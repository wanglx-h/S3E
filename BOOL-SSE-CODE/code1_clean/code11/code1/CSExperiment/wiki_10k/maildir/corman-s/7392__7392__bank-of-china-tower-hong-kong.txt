Subject: Bank of China Tower (Hong Kong)
The '''Bank of China Tower''' (BOC Tower, 中銀大廈) is one of the most well-known skyscrapers in the central parts of Hong Kong. In the tower, the Bank of China has their offices. The tower was designed by the architect I. M. Pei and is 315 meters high.
