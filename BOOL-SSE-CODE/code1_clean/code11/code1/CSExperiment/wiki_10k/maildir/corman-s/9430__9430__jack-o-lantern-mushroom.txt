Subject: Jack o'lantern mushroom
The '''jack o'lantern mushroom''' is an Orange (color)- to yellow gilled mushroom. It looks like the chanterelle and bioluminescence. Unlike the chanterelle, the jack o'lantern mushroom is very poisonous. While eating this mushroom will not death you, it may cause cramps, vomiting, and diarrhea. This mushroom smells and looks very appealing, so there are reports of repeat poisonings from individuals who were tempted to try them a second time. == Description == The Jack-o-Lantern mushroom's fruiting body (its stem and cap) is an orange color. It grows from late summer into November, in large clumps on or near tree stumps. However, it can also be found growing on the ground (along buried roots) nearby. Underneath the cap is its well-known glowing color, which glows in a lime green color.
