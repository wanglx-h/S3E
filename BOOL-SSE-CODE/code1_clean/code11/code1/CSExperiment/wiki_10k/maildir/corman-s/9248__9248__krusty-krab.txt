Subject: Krusty Krab
File:The Krusty Krab.png The '''Krusty Krab''' is a fictional fast food restaurant in the television programme, ''"SpongeBob SquarePants"''. It is in the city of Bikini Bottom. The restaurant is run by a crab named Eugene H. Krabs. The other two workers at the restaurant are SpongeBob SquarePants (character) (fry cook), and Squidward Tentacles (cashier). The fast food sold includes the popular Krabby Patties (which are similar to hamburgers but made of krill), and others such as French fried potatoes, and drinks.
