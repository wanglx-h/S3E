Subject: Grevena
'''Grevena''' is a Greek city and it is one of 51 prefectures of Greece. Grevena is a small city which is in the west of Macedonia (Greece). Its population is 12,037 inhabitants according to 2001 census. It is built at a height of 500 meter approximately. West of Grevena is the mountain range of Pindos with many forests and with a skiing center which it is named Vasilitsa. This ski center has a lot of snow throughout the winter. == Geography == Greneva has a total area of 1,860 square kilometers. Category:Prefectures of Greece
