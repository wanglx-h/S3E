Subject: La Jolla, San Diego
Image:090207-LaJollaCove.jpg '''La Jolla''' is a neighborhood in the city of San Diego, California, in the state of California, in the United States. It is pronounced "La Hoya". The University of California, San Diego is in La Jolla. Another famous place in La Jolla is the Salk Institute. It is the birthplace of the Hollywood actor, Gregory Peck.
