Subject: Full Metal Jacket
'''''Full Metal Jacket''''' is a 1987 United Kingdom-United States war drama movie movie director by Stanley Kubrick. It is based on the Gustav Hasford book ''The Short-Timers''. The name of the movie comes from the full-metal jacketed bullets used in the military. The movie follows soldiers who are drafted to fight in the Vietnam War.
