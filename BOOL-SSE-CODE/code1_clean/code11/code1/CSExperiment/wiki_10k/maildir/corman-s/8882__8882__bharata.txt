Subject: Bharata
'''Bharata''' has several meanings. Some of the meanings are given below: * A king of Ancient India named Bharat. Ancient name of India was '''Bharatavarsh''', the country of Bharata. * Bharata was the name of one of the brothers of Rama, a god of the Hindus. * A writer named Bharata wrote a book long back. The book is named ''Natyashastra'', that is, the book of dramas. * Bharata is another name for Republic of India. This has been noted in the constitution of India.
