Born in Los Angeles, she is the daughter of Bruce Dern and Diane Ladd. She received an Academy Award and Golden Globe Award for her performance as Rose in ''Rambling Rose''. For the HBO film ''Afterburn'', she received an Emmy Award nomination and a Golden Globe Award. She has also won Independent Spirit Awards for ''Blue Velvet'' and ''Smooth Talk''. She is perhaps most famous for playing Dr. Ellie Sattler in ''Jurassic Park''. She used to date Jeff Goldblum. In 2020, she won another Oscar for her role in ''Marriage Story''.
