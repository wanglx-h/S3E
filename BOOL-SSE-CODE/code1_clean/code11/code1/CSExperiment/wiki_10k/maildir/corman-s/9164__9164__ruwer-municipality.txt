Subject: Ruwer (municipality)
Image:Lage der Verbandsgemeinde Ruwer in Deutschland.png]] Image:DEU Verbandsgemeinde Ruwer COA.svg '''Ruwer''' is a municipality (a convention community called ''Verbandsgemeinde'' Ruwer) on the River Ruwer River.
