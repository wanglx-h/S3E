Subject: Frasier
'''''Frasier''''' is an United States sitcom starring Kelsey Grammer. The series is set in Seattle, Washington (state). It aired on NBC from 1993 to 2004. It is a spin-off from ''Cheers''. It is based upon the psychiatrist and his life. It revolves around his personal life and interpersonal interactions with his father Martin Crane, a retired police officer, who was shot in the hip by a robber, who as a result has a limp and walks using a cane, who lives with him. Other characters include his eccentric brother Niles, who is also a psychiatrist, Martin's pretty English people healthcare worker Daphne Moon and a host of folks he regularly runs into / works with at the radio station (including his radio producer Roz Doyle), where he hosts a community psychiatric call-in show.
