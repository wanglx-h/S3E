Subject: Newsround
'''''Newsround''''' is a television television programme for children. It talks about the news and gives information about things that are happening at the moment. It is shown on the channels BBC One, CBBC channel and the BBC World News. == The past == ''Newsround'' used to be called ''John Craven's Newsround'' before the presenter called John Craven left in 1989.
