Subject: Centaur
File:Young Centaur Musei Capitolini MC656.jpg A '''centaur''' is a creature in Greek mythology. It has the upper body of a human, but below the waist it has the body of a horse. There are also female centaurs. Centaurs lived in far away places, including Thessaly and Cyprus. They were wild, like wild horses. File:GiorcesBardo55.jpg (Mosaic from Roman Tunisia, 2nd century AD)]] Famous centaurs are Chiron and Nessus. ==Other websites== Category:Greek legendary creatures
