Subject: Brine
'''Brine''' is water that has more than 5% table salt in it. It is used for food preservation food. For this it is much like vinegar or sugar. Brine is also used to transport heat. Adding salt to water lowers the freezing point of the water. The solubility of salt in water depends on temperature. At 15.5°Celsius, a saturated solution of brine can hold 26.4 % salt by weight. At 0°C, it can hold 23.3% salt. Brines also have some pharmacy properties, which can be used to treat people, especially those with diseases of the skin, allergy and common colds. ==Related pages== *Salinity *Salt water ==References== category:Food preservation Category:Drugs
