Subject: Aaron
'''Aaron''' is a person described in the Bible and the Qu'ran. He was the older brother of Moses. He helped Moses lead the Israelite out of Egypt. In the Bible, he appeared very much in Exodus. == Moses' helper == Aaron spoke for Moses, when he went to tell Pharaoh the King of Egypt everything God wanted Moses to say. The Lord said to Moses (Exodus 7:1 to 3), "See, I have made you like God to Pharaoh, and your brother Aaron will be your prophet. You are to say everything I command you, and your brother Aaron is to tell Pharaoh to let the Israelites go out of his country...". ==Related pages== *Aaron (disambiguation) Category:Old Testament people Category:Ancient Israeli people Category:15th-century BC deaths
