Subject: Flipper
File:Humpback flipper.jpg]] File:SwimFins 02.jpgmer's flippers]] A '''flipper''' is a special kind of limb that is flattened like a paddle for moving through water. Many animals that live in water, such as whales and pinniped have flippers. The flipper of a fish is called a fin. It can also be an item worn on the foot to help people swim more easily.
