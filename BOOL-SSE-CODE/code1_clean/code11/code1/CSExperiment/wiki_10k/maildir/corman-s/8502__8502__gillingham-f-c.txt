Paul Scally |manager= Steve Evans (footballer, born 1962) |captain= |league= Football League One |season= The Football League 2019-20 |position=Football League One, 10th }} '''Gillingham Football Club''' is an England association football club. They are based in the town of Gillingham, Kent, Kent. They are currently in Football League One. They play home games at Priestfield Stadium. Gillingham are manager by Steve Lovell and the chairman is Paul Scally. The club captain, Gabriel Zakuani, plays in defence. They finished their last season (2017-18) in 17th.
