File:Abu Bakr Caliph Calligraphy.png Calligraphy]]'''Sunni Islam''' () is the largest branch of Islam and came through the Rashidun Empire, which started with Abu Bakr and ended by Ali. Sunni beliefs are based on the Qur'an and the Kutub al-Sittah. Sunnis make up around 90% of the world's Muslim population.[http://www.galesburg.com/newsnow/x917961022/Sue-Hulett-U-S-should-focus-on-sanctions-against-Iran Sue Hellett; U.S. should focus on sanctions against Iran] retrieved 13 December 2012[https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-middle-east-16047709 Quick guide] retrieved 13 December 2012 With approximately 1.8 billion followers, it is the largest religious denomination of any religion in the world.Religious Diversity and Children's Literature: Strategies and Resources, Sandra Brenneman Oldendorf - 2011, p 156 Catholicism is the second-largest religion. There are four sub-groups (Madh'hab) of Sunni Islam; Maliki, Hanafi, Hanbali and Shafi`i. Adherents of Sunni Islam are '''Sunnis''' or '''Sunnites'''. The word ''Sunni'' comes from the word sunnah (), which means the tradition of the prophet of Islam, Muhammad.
