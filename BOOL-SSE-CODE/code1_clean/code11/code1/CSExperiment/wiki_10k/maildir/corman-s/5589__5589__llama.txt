A '''Llama''' (''Lama glama'') is a mammal of the ''Camelidae'' family. It is from South America, the Andes of Peru and Ecuador. Sometimes the name ''llama'' is used for all four South American camelids. == Llama and people == The llama was Domestication about 5000 years ago. It was and is used for carrying things, and for wool. The llama was very important for the Incan people. Even today, llamas are used for similar reasons, but will more commonly be used as domesticated pets. == Related animals == The llama's close relative is the alpaca, who is a more demure, petite animal in comparison to the llama.
