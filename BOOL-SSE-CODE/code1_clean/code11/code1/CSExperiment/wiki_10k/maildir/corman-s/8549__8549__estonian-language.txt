It is mainly spoken in Estonia. The Estonian language is similar to Finnish language and is one of the few national languages of Europe not to be an Indo-European language. The Estonian alphabet uses the Latin alphabet. It has many vowels, including Ö, Ä, Õ and Ü. It has been influenced by and adopted many words from German language and Swedish language.
