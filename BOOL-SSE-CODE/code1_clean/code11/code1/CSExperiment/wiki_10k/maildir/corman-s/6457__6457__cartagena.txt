File:Situacion Cartagena CARM.png File:Ciudad de Cartagena.png Image:Bandera Cartagena.svg Image:Coat of Arms of Cartagena (Spain).svg '''Cartagena''' is a city in the Murcia (autonomous community), in Spain. In the south of the region, at the Mediterranean Sea. The population of the city is 205,000 and it is an industrial centre. Cartagena was founded by the Carthaginese as "Qart Hadasht" and the name means ''New City''. ==Other websites== Category:Cartagena
