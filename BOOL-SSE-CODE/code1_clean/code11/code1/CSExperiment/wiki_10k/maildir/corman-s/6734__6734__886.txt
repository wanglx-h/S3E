Subject: 886
== Events == * The Glagolitic alphabet, devised by Saint Cyril and Saint Methodius, missionaries from Constantinople, is adopted in the Bulgaria. * Alfred the Great captures London and renames it Lundenburgh. The boundaries between Wessex and the Danelaw are shifted. * Alfred the Great builds a small harbour called Queenhythe slightly upstream from London Bridge. * Alfred the Great mints the first halfpenny. Previous halfpennies had been pennies cut in half. * Earl Earl Aethelred of Mercia is given control of London by Alfred the Great. Alfred's sister Ethelfleda marries Aethelred. * Leo VI succeeds his stepfather Basil I as Byzantine emperor and replaces Patriarch of Constantinople Photius with his brother Stephen I of Constantinople. * Boris I of Bulgaria establishes the Preslav Literary School and Ohrid Literary Schools. * Charles the Bald of France purchases peace with Vikings at Paris.
