Subject: Walter Mikac
'''Walter Mikac''' is an Australian writer and motivate talk. Walter Mikac's wife and two small children were killed at the Port Arthur massacre. He has written two books, one called ''To Have And To Hold'' and the other is called ''Circle of Life: Sustenance for the Soul''. It is about handling grief and tragedy.
