File:Aofhfeworewour2.jpg File:Dogon circumcision cave painting (brightened).jpg '''Drawing''' is a way of making a picture. Drawings are created by making lines on a surface. Long lines make up the shapes and small lines make the Texture. Drawing (manufacturing) can be images of real-life or abstract images. Drawing has been done for thousands of years, dating back to Cave painting. It focuses on objects made by Human for any number of Spirituality, narrative, philosophical, Symbol, Conceptual art, documentary, decorative, and even Functionalism and other purposes. == Materials == Drawing paper is a special material sometimes used for drawing, however many just use paper. Pencil or Pen are most commonly used for drawing. However, chalk, charcoal, and many others writing utensils are used. File:Parametric surface illustration (sphere).png == Shading == Shading is the darkening or Colouring of a drawing with lines or blocks of color. It can be used to make your drawings more 3D. Shading is the process of adding value to Creating an illusion of form, most importantly the light in a drawing. It should have a highlight (where the light is shining), mid tone (goes darker in color), Core shadow which is the darkest color on your drawing, then there is the reflected light. Reflecting light is where light shines on the object you are trying to draw. File:Arcering1.jpg == Hatching == Hatching is a artistic technique used to create tonal or shading Effect by drawing closely spaced parallel lines. There are Difference types of hatching. There is Parallel hatching, Contour line, crosshatching, tick hatching, and more.
