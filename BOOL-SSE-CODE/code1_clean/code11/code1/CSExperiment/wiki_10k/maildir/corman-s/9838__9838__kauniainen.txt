Kauniainen was officially founded in 1920. It became a city in 1972. There are about 8,550 people living in Kauniainen. Most of them (57 %) speak Finnish language as their first language. 39 % have Swedish as their mother tongue. Kauniainen has very good bus and train connections. There is a railway station.
