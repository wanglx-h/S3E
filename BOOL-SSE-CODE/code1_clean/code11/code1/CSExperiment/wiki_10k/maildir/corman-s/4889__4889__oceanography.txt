Subject: Oceanography
'''Oceanography''' (also called '''oceanology''' or '''marine science''') is the study of the ocean, its properties and its characteristics. This can include, and is not limited to, studying the Marine biology, the geography of the Seabed, and the water itself. Many sciences are useful in oceanography, so it is a Interdisciplinarity field. Oceanographers spend much time on boats. == Sub-Categories == The study of oceanography may be divide branches: * '''Marine biology''' or '''biological oceanography''' * '''Chemical oceanography''' or '''marine chemistry''' * '''Marine geology''' or '''geological oceanography''' * '''Physical oceanography''' * '''Marine engineering''' Category:Oceanography
