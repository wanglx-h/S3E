Subject: List
A '''list''' is a part of a text, which has many words separated by commas (,) or a group of words going down the page that may use bullet points, letters, or numbers. Semicolons (;) are also used sometimes to separate items in a list. These are lists: I have invited '''Peter, Mary, and Henry''' to my birthday. People who I have invited to my birthday are: *'''Peter''' *'''Mary''' *'''Henry''' People I invited to my birthday: '''Peter; Mary; Henry.''' == Related pages == * Mailing list Category:Basic English 850 words Category:Lists
