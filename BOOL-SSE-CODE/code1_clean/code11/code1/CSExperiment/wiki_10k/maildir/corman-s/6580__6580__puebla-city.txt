It is southeast of Mexico City in a broad valley. The city has had many names over the years including City of Angels, City of Tiles, and Heroic City of Zaragoza. Puebla was the main city of colony Mexico. The architecture and look of Puebla is the most European of all the colonial cities. This is because it was built completely by a Spain. Most cities at the time were built within an existing Native American community. Puebla is highly industrialized, having factories belonging to Volkswagen, Audi, and Bimbo. The history downtown area remains a Spanish colonial treasure. It is filled with 17th century and 18th century century European architecture.
