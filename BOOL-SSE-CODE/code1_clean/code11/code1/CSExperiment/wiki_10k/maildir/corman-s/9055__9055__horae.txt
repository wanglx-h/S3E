Subject: Horae
Image:Horen Meyers.jpg The '''Horae''' were three goddesses in Greek mythology. There are in fact two different groups of goddesses which were known at different times: today they are called the first and second generation to distinguish between them. They were the children of Zeus and Themis. ==First Generation== The first Horae were goddesses of the seasons. They were: *'''Thallo''' (or Thalatte) *'''Auxo''' (or Auxesia) *'''Karpo''' (also Xarpo or Carpo) ==Second Generation== The second Horae were goddesses of order, :wikt:justice and law. They were: *'''Dike''' *'''Eunomia''' *'''Eirene''' (or Irene) Category:Greek gods and goddesses
