Subject: Hanoi
File:Hanoi Montage.jpg '''Hanoi''' () is the capital (city) of Vietnam. It is also the second most populated city in the country. In 2023, about 5,253,000 people lived in the city. It has been the capital of the North Vietnam (North Vietnam) since 1945 and the capital of the Vietnam (all of Vietnam) since 1976. Before that, it was the capital of the country now called Vietnam for most of the time from at least the 11th century until 1802. The city is on the right of the Red River (Vietnam), and the city's name translates to English as "inside the river". Things made there include machine tools, plywood, textiles, chemical industry and handicrafts. Hanoi is at 21°2' North, 105°51' East, 1,760km north of Ho Chi Minh City (formerly Saigon). Popular tourist sites in the city include Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum, Hoa Lo Prison, and the Palace of Literature. Nearby Hanoi is also where Phở, Vietnamese beef noodle soup, was first created. ==References== Category:Hanoi
