Subject: Newport, Wales
File:Newport montage1.jpg Image:Arms of Newport City Council.svg '''Newport''' is a city in the southeast of Wales, Great Britain. It is in the county of '''Monmouthshire''', and in the historic Welsh kingdom of Monmouth. Its name in Welsh language is '''Casnewydd'''. It is the third biggest city in Wales. Newport became a city in 2002. About 140,000 people live in Newport. A few years ago, Newport had lots of people working in the steel factory and other heavy industries. This has changed, now many people work in electronics, such as making computers, phones and microchips.
