Subject: Yorkshire Terrier
File:Yorkshirenamedmeetwithsuccesofhappyblue.JPG File:Yorkshire terrier.jpg The '''Yorkshire Terrier''' or '''Yorkie''' is a small breed of terrier dog. It originated in the United Kingdom in the town of Yorkshire, from where it gets its name. They were first bred to catch Mouse and Rat, so they are often fast and energetic. Yorkies usually weigh about 7 pounds (3.18 kg) and have a dark gray or black coat (dog) with a brown face. According to the American Kennel Club (AKC), Yorkies are the second most popular dog breed in America, behind only the Labrador Retriever. They are really playful, they will always want to play.
