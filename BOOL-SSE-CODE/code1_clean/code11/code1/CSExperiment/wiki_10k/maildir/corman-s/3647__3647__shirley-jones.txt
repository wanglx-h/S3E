Jones had a supporting role in the movie ''Elmer Gantry'', starring actor Burt Lancaster. She is also famous for her role as Shirley Partridge on the television series ''The Partridge Family''. ==Personal life== She was marriage to Jack Cassidy, from 1956 to 1975, and had three sons with him, Shaun, Patrick, and Ryan. Her stepson is David Cassidy who co-starred with her on ''The Partridge Family''. After she divorced Cassidy she married Marty Ingels on November 13, 1977.
