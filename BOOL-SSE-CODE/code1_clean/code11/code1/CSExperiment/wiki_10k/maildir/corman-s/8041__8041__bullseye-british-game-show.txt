Subject: Bullseye (British game show)
'''''Bullseye''''' is a United Kingdom television game show. It is hosted by Jim Bowen. In ''Bullseye'', people play darts and answer questions to win money. The person who wins the most money has a chance to win Bully's special prize. It ran from 1981 to 1995 on ITV and there was another series in 2006 on Challenge (TV channel), hosted by Dave Spikey.
