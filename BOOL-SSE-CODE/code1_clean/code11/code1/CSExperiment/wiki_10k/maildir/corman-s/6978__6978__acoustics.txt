Subject: Acoustics
'''Acoustics''' is a science. It studies the mechanical waves in gases, liquids, and solids. These waves can make echo (acoustics), vibration, sound, ultrasound and infrasound. A scientist who works in the field of '''acoustics''' is an acoustician.
