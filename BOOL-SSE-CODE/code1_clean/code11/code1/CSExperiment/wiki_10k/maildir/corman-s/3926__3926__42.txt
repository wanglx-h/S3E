'''42''' is a year in the 1st century. It was a common year starting on Monday of the Julian calendar. At the time, it was known as the '''Year of the Roman consul of Claudius and Piso'''. == Events == === Roman Empire === * Romans take control of Ceuta. * Lands now known as Algeria and Morocco become a Roman province. * The Dalmatia (Roman province)n legate Furius Camillus Scribonianus revolts. His troops do not support him and the rebellion quickly ends. * Claudius starts building the Port of Ostia. === Asia === * King Suro of Gaya becomes the first king of Geumgwan Gaya in the Korean peninsula. * China General My Yuan puts down the rebellion of the Trung sisters in Tonkin. === Religion === *25 January: the Paul the Apostle becomes a Christian. The exact date is not known, but the Catholic Church has chosen to celebrate it on this date. == Births == * Herennius Philo (approximate date) (d. 141) * Pope Sixtus I (d. 124) == Deaths == * Arria * Gaius Appius Junius Silanus Category:40s
