Subject: Tailor
Image:South Nyanza Tailor.jpg]] A '''tailor''' is a person whose job is to make clothes to fit people. A tailor might make a new piece of clothing, or change an item so that it fits better. They also mend (fix) clothes. A tailor is also called a '''seamster''', or a '''seamstress''' for a woman. Category:Clothing Category:Artisans Category:Occupations
