Mural in Manchester Town Hall]] == Dalton's atomic theory == Dalton's Atomic theory is a scientific theory about Atom. Dalton made the theory to explain why the chemical element would combine in certain ways The idea of atoms was already known at the time, but not widely accepted. Dalton's theory of atoms was based on actual observation. Ideas about atoms were based on philosophy before John Dalton came up with his theory. His theory stated that: *Atoms cannot be divided *Everything is made of atoms *Atoms can not be made or destroyed, just as energy *Atoms are tiny particles *Law of definite composition *Atoms of the same element are the same, because they have the same atomic mass *Chemical reaction are made when atoms are moved around, put with other atoms or taken away from a group of other atoms == Further readings == * Claus Bernet: John Dalton (1766-1844), in: Biographisches-bibliographisches Kirchenlexikon, 31, 2010, 309-332. == References == == Other websites == * [http://en.citizendium.org/wiki/John_Dalton John Dalton] -Citizendium Category:1766 births Category:1844 deaths Category:English chemists Category:English physicists Category:British nuclear physicists
