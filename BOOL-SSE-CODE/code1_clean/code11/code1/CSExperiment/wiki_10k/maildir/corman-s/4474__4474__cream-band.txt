Clapton helped pioneer the use of the Pedal (music) and played a famous guitar called The Fool. Clapton developed what he called the "woman tone" on his guitar, while Bruce played a fretless bass guitar, and Baker played unusual rhythms on drums to create the band's distinctive sound. Cream's hit records included the songs "Strange Brew", "Sunshine Of Your Love" and "White Room".
