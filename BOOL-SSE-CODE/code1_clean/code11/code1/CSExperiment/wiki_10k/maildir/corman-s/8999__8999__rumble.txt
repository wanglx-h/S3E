Subject: Rumble
'''Rumble''' can mean different things: *A rumble is a dull vibration. It can also be the sound of one. *A rumble is another word for a fight. *Rumble strips are raised lines placed horizontally on a road. They are usually used to tell drivers that they must slow down or stop ahead. The strips make a rumbling sound when a car's tires cross them. *''Rumble (book)'', the original title of Harlan Ellison's book ''Web of the City''. *A stomach rumble is the growling noise the stomach makes.
