Bill Rogers (voice actor) (anime, Season 9–present)Johnny Yong Bosch (''Pokémon Origins'')Tommy Arciniega (''Pokémon Masters'') |'''Japanese language''' |Yūji Ueda (anime)Tomokazu Sugita (''Pokémon Origins'')Kōsuke Toriumi (''Pokémon Masters'') }} | full_name = Brock Harrison | portrayer = Dennis Kenney }} known by name as '''Takeshi''' in Japan, is a fictional character in the ''Pokémon'' series of video games, Manga, and Television programme. He is often seen traveling along with Ash Ketchum and Misty (Pokémon) on their journeys. One of his gags is that he falls in love with every girl he sight (except for Jessie of Team Rocket). His nickname is "The Rock-Solid Pokémon Trainer" because he uses rock-type Pokémon. ==Anime== Brock appears in the first series of the anime. In his first appearance, he has many siblings and is in charge of them and cares for them since his mother Lola is away chasing dreams and his father Flint ran away from the family. After Flint comes back, Brock then picks up and his bags and travels with Ash and Misty throughout the Kanto series. He ends up leaving the group when they get to the Orange Islands to work with Professor Ivy. He later returns and travels with Ash throughout Johto, Hoenn, and Sinnoh. He uses rock-types Geodude and Onix, as well as others like Zubat. ==Video games== Brock appears in the games Pokémon Red and Blue, and the ''Red'' and ''Blue'' remakes ''FireRed'' and ''LeafGreen'' as the gym leader of Pewter City. He gives out the Boulder Badge and uses Rock-type Pokémon.
