Subject: Mortar and pestle
File:Black peppercorns with mortar and pestle.jpg File:Wooden mortar and pestle.jpg A '''mortar and pestle''' are two tools used together to mill (grinding) (grind) and mix substances. The ''mortar'' is bowl-shaped, and used to hold the substance to be ground. Mortars have smooth, rounded bottoms and wide mouths. The ''pestle'' is a stick used for pounding and grinding. Mortars and pestles are sometimes used in pharmacy to crush various ingredients to make drugs.
