It is the largest of the Balearic Islands. About 869,067 people (as of January 1, 2010) live on Mallorca, making it the most populated island of Spain. Palma de Mallorca is the Capital (city) of Mallorca. Palma has a population of 375,000. Mallorca has one international airport, one university and its own Rapid transit. The economy of the island depends on the Tourism. Other important city in Mallorca are Calvià (43.499), Manacor (35.908), Llucmajor (29.891), Marratxí (28.237), Inca (Mallorca) (26.504), Felanitx (16.566), Pollença (15.987), Alcúdia (15.897) and Sóller (12.521).
