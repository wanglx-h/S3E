|death_place=Melun, France |burial_place=Saint Denis Basilica, Paris, France }} '''Robert II of France''' (March 27, 972 July 20, 1031) was born in Orleans, France. He was the son of Hugh Capet and Adelaide of Aquitaine. He was married to Constance of Arles. Robert was succeeded by his son Henry I of France. Robert is buried in the Saint Denis Basilica.
