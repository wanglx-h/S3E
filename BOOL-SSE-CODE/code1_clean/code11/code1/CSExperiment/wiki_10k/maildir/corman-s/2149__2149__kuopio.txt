File:Kuopio.sijainti.suomi.2013.svg File:Kuopio City Hall.JPG File:Kuopio.vaakuna.svg '''Kuopio''' is a city in Northern Savonia, Finland. It is the biggest city and cultural center of Eastern Finland. In November 2015 the population was about 112.000. It is the 8th biggest city in Finland. The city is surrounded by lake Kallavesi. Many parts of the city are built on islands. Kuopio has always been a city of education. Two important schools are the University of Eastern Finland and the Savonia University of Applied Sciences.
