Subject: Daman and Diu
'''Daman and Diu''' was a union territory in India. It covers an area of . The area is bigger than Saint Helena but smaller than Wallis and Futuna. It was made of two districts of India, Daman district, India and Diu district. The territory used to belong to Portugal. In 1961, Indian Soldier and Pilot took control of the territory. In 2020, it merged with Dadra and Nagar Haveli to make a new union territory, Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu. ==References== Category:Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu Category:2020 disestablishments in Asia Category:Disestablishments in India
