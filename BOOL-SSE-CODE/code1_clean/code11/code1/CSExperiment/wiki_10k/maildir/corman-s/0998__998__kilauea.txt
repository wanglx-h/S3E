Subject: Kilauea
File:Kīlauea - Volcano eruption.jpg '''Kīlauea''' is an active volcano in the Hawaiian Islands. It is a shield volcano. The volcano is 1,277 Metre high. Kīlauea is a large lake of lava. The crater of Kīlauea is named Pu'u'O'o. The first Erupt of Kīlauea is estimated as 300 to 600 thousand years ago. Its first recorded eruptions were in 1823. Its most recent eruption was April 7 2025. It is still erupting now. The Hawaiian name ''Kīlauea'' means "spewing" or "much spreading". It refers to the constant flow of lava. Kīlauea lies on the curve of volcanoes on the island of Hawaii that includes Mauna Loa and Kohala. The volcano of Kīlauea is one of the youngest and also is said to be the home of an ancient volcano goddess Pele (goddess). The oldest dated rocks are said to be about 23,000 years old. It has its own magma plumbing under the Earth that goes down under the Earths crust by 60km. Kīlauea has a basalt-based lava rock type Hotspot (geology). == May 2018 eruption == In May 2018, the volcano 2018 lower Puna eruption causing damage in nearby homes and streets.
