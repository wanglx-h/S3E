| birth_place = York, England, United Kingdom | yearsactive = 1987–present | spouse = Kelly Johnson (1996–present) | children = 3 }} '''Mark Addy''' (born 14 January 1965, in York, England) is an English people actor. The best known works by Addy are as Detective Boyle in the British sitcom ''The Thin Blue Line (TV series)'', Dave in the movie ''The Full Monty'', father Bill Miller in the US sitcom ''Still Standing (TV series)'' and more recently as King Robert Baratheon in the HBO fantasy epic ''Game of Thrones''. Addy has played many different roles in both television and in movies. In 2013 he played Hercules, a medieval King, and a modern-day alcoholic with family problems. Addy received the Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by a Cast in a Motion Picture for ''The Full Monty''. He was also nominated for a BAFTA, Chlotrudis Award and Satellite Award.
