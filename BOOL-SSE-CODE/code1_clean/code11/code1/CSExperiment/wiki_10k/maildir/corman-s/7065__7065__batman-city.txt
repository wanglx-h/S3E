Subject: Batman (city)
File:Batman(city).jpg '''Batman''' is a city in southeast Turkey. It is the capital (city) of the province with the same name. In 2010, 325,000 people lived there. There are many Kurds in that area. The area has much petroleum and natural gas. There is a railway track at Kurtalan, near this city. The track runs all the way to Istanbul. There is also an airport near Batman. The name is short for the Bati Raman mountains. == Other websites == * [http://www.batman-bld.gov.tr/ Batman Municipality] Category:Cities in Turkey
