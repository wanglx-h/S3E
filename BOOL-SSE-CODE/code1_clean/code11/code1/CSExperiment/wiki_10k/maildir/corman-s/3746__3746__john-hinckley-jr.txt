File:John Hinckley, Jr. Mugshot.png '''John Warnock Hinckley, Jr.''' (born May 29, 1955) is the man who attempted to Assassination attempt on Ronald Reagan President of the United States Ronald Reagan. On March 30, 1981, shortly after Reagan was elected, Hinckley shot at Reagan in Washington, D.C. Reagan was injured, but recovered quickly. Hinckley was tried, but was found not guilty, due to his insanity. He said the reason he shot the President was to get the attention of actress Jodie Foster, of whom he was a fan. Hinckley has schizophrenia, dysthymia, narcissistic personality disorder and schizoid personality disorder. He also has borderline personality disorder and passive-aggressive features.[http://www.trutv.com/library/crime/terrorists_spies/assassins/john_hinckley/9.html Dementia suburbia] He was born in Ardmore, Oklahoma. He also became a singer and song creator after he was released from psychiatric care.He posted an original and a cover song to YouTube in 2020.
