File:Mike Judge at awards.jpg '''Mike Judge''' (born October 17, 1962) is an American animator, actor, writer, and producer. He is most famous for creating and starring in the animated television programme ''Beavis and Butt-head'' and ''King of the Hill''. Judge also wrote, movie director, and played a small part in the 1999 movie ''Office Space'', and directed the 2006 movie ''Idiocracy'', which starred Luke Wilson.
