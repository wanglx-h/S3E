Subject: 1282
== Events == * March 30 – The Sicily rebellion known as the Sicilian Vespers begins * March – Dafydd ap Gruffydd, brother to Prince of Wales Llywelyn the Last, attacks an England castle * Holy Roman Empire Rudolph I of Germany makes his sons Albert I of Germany and Rudolph II of Austria as rulers of the of Austria and Styria (duchy), thus founding the Habsburg dynasty in those territories. * Floris V, Count of Holland defeats the Frisians at the battle of Vronen and retrieves the body of his father, who had been dead for 26 years.
