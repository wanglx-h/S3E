File:Prosciutto in store.jpg in a store]] '''Ham''' is pork that comes from a leg of a pig."Bacon: Bacon and Ham Curing" in ''Chambers's Encyclopædia''. London: George Newnes Ltd, 1961, Vol. 2, p. 39. Ham can be dry-cured or wet-cured. Dry-cured ham is made by rubbing the meat with salt. Wet-cured ham is made with brine. Types of dry-cured ham: *Prosciutto (Italy) *Prosciutto di Parma (Italian) *Bayonne ham (France) *Jamón serrano (Spain) *Jamón iberico (Spanish) *Country ham (United States) *York ham (England) *Westphalian ham (Germany) Ham is used in Spam (food).[https://www.livescience.com/32813-hormel-spam-no-mystery-meat.html How Do They Make Spam?].
