Since then has been featured in thirty-nine movies. During his movie career, this large reptile fought against many other wikt:mythical monsters, including the giant moth-like Mothra, the three-headed King Ghidorah, his own robotic mirror image Mechagodzilla, and several more enemies. The original Japanese language name, ''Gojira (ゴジラ)'', is a combination of the words ''gorira'' (means ''gorilla'') and ''kujira'' (means ''whale'').
