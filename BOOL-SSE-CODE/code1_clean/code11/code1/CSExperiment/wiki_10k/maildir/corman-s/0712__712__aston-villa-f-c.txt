They have played at Villa Park, in Aston, since 1897. They were one of the teams that started the English Football League in 1888. They were also one of the teams that started the Premier League in 1992.Ward, Adam; Griffin, Jeremy; p. 161. They are one of five English clubs to have won the UEFA Champions League. They won this in 1982. They have also won the Football League First Division seven times, the FA Cup seven times, the EFL Cup five times, and the UEFA Super Cup once. Villa have a large :wikt:rivalry with nearby team Birmingham City F.C.. This is called the Second City Derby, and they have been playing against each other since 1879. The team's main home colours are claret and light blue. Their badge is a claret lion (having changed from a yellow lion in 2023). The club is owned by the NWSE group (owned by Egyptian Nassef Sawiris) and American Wes Edens. == Club honours == Aston Villa have won European and domestic league trophies.
