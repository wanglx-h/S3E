Subject: Knob
Image:Knobs-for-climate-control.jpg room]] A '''knob''' is a round Handle (grip) that can be turned or pulled. A '''doorknob''' is a round handle that is turned to open a door. A '''cabinet knob''' is a small handle that is pulled to open a cabinet door or drawer. A '''control knob''' can turn a lamp on and off or make the volume on a radio go up or down. A "knob" can also be any small, round thing that sticks out. Category:Hardware (mechanical)
