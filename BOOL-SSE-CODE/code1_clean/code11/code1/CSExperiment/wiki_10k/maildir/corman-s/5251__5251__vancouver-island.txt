File:Vancouver Island, with Lasqueti Island highlighted.png '''Vancouver Island''' is an island that is part of the province of British Columbia, Canada. Its area is . The island is in the south-western part of the province and is named after the British explorer George Vancouver. The first people to live on the island, before the Europeans came, were the Nuu-chah-nulth, the Salish, and the Kwakiutl. As of 2009, over 700,000 people live here. There are no bridges or tunnels between Vancouver Island and the mainland. There are ferry, whichcarry people and cars to and from the island. One route crosses the Strait of Georgia to the Tsawwassen Peninsula,from roads go to Vancouver and other cities in Canada as part of the Trans-Canada Highway. Another ferry route crosses the Strait of Juan de Fuca and reaches Anacortes in the United States of Washington (state) from which roads go to Seattle and other cities along the West Coast of the United States. == Cities and towns == There are many cities and towns on Vancouver Island. The biggest is Victoria, British Columbia, the Capital city of British Columbia.
