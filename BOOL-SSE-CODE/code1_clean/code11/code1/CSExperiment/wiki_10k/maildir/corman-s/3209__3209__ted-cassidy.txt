Subject: Ted Cassidy
File:New Adv of Huck Finn Ted Cassidy 1968.jpg '''Ted Cassidy''' (July 31, 1932 - January 16, 1979) was an United States actor. He was best known for his roles as Lurch and Thing on ''The Addams Family (1964 TV series)''.
