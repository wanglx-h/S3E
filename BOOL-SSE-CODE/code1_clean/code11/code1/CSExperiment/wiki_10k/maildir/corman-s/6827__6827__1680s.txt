The '''1680s''' was a decade that began on 1 January 1680 and ended on 31 December 1689. It is distinct from the decade known as the '''''169th decade''''' which began on January 1, 1681 and ended on December 31, 1690. == Events == * The Treaty of Ratisbon between France and England in 1684 ended the Age of Buccaneers. * 1685: The China army attacks a Russian post at Albazin. The events lead to the Treaty of Nerchinsk. Roberts, J: "History of the World.". Penguin, 1994. * Isaac Newton publishes ''Philosophiae Naturalis Principia Mathematica''.
