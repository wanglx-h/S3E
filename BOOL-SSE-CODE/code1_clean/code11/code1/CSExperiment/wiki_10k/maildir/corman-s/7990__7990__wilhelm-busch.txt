Image:Wilhelm Busch, Portrait by Franz von Lenbach.jpg, about 1875]] '''Wilhelm Busch''' (born 14 April 1832 in Wiedensahl, near Hannover; died 9 January 1908 in Mechtshausen) was a Germany painter and poet who is known for his satirical picture stories. After studying first mechanical engineering and then art in Düsseldorf, Antwerp, and Munich he started drawing caricatures. Wilhelm Busch also wrote a number of poems in a style similar to his picture stories.
