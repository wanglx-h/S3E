He plays on different roles and various genres of movies like Action movie, Comedy movie, Drama movie, Musical movie, Romance movie and Superhero movie. ==Early and personal life== Jackman was born on 12 October 1968 in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia to English people parents. He is best known for his long-running role as Wolverine (character) in the X-Men (movie series). His performances in ''The Fountain (movie)'', ''Les Misérables (2012 movie)'' and ''Prisoners (2013 movie)'' garnered strong critical acclaim. His work in ''Les Misérables'' earned him an Academy Award nomination for Academy Award for Best Actor and a Golden Globe Award in 2013. In theater, he won a Tony Award for his role in ''The Boy from Oz''. A four-time host of the Tony Awards, winning an Emmy Award for one of these appearances, Jackman also hosted the 81st Academy Awards on 22 February 2009. Jackman married Deborra-Lee Furness in 1996 in Melbourne. The couple adopted a son and daughter. The two separated in September 2023. Jackman has been described as one of the world's Sex symbol, by various media outlets and his perceived sex appeal has been picked up by many sources, including ''Empire (magazine)'', who named him one of the sexiest stars in movie historyhttp://www.empireonline.com/100sexiest/default.asp?star=15http://www.empireonline.com/100sexiest2009/men/default.asp?star=6 and ''People (American magazine)'', who named him one of the World's Most Beautiful People. In 2008 Jackman won ''People (American magazine)'''s Sexiest Man Alive. Jackman appeared on ''Forbes'''s annual Celebrity 100 list of the 100 most powerful celebrities and In 2013, he topped ''Forbes'''s Forbes Celebrity 100. Jackman plays multiple sports, plays the guitar, piano and violin and does dancing. He is also a philanthropist. In November 2013, Jackman announced he had skin cancer removed from his nose.
