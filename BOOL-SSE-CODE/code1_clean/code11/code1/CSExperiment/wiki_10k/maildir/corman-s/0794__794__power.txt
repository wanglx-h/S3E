Subject: Power
'''Power''' has different meanings depending on the context: * Power (physics), how fast energy can be changed into work * Electric power, electricity that comes from power plants or generators * In sociology, power is the ability of a person to make another person do something. This might also be called '''influence''' or '''control'''. * Statistical power, how likely the test is to say something is true when it is really true * Exponentiation; in mathematics, "power" is multiplying a number by itself. * A life force, called chi by the Chinese and kiai by the Japanese. * In politics a great power is more powerful than other countries. * '''Joules aren't a unit of power''' ==Related pages== * Power plant * Suzerainty Category:Basic English 850 words
