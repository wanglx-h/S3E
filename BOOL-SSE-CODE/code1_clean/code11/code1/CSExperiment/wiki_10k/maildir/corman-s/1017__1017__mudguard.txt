Subject: Mudguard
File:Bicycle-Mudguard-Fender.jpg A '''mudguard''', '''fender''' or '''wing''' is a part covering the wheels of a vehicle to stop it from throwing off mud, water or objects from the road. It protects people and property from dirtiness, dampness, and damage. Mudguards protect in several ways: # They protect brakes and lights. # They protect the bicycle from getting rusted by mud and water. # They protect clothes from being dirty. # They protect from injuries.
