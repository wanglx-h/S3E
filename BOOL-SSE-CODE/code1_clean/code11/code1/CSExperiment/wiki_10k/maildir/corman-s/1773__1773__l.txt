File:L cursiva.gif '''L''' is the twelfth (12 (number)) letter in the English language alphabet. == Meanings for L == * For clothes, L sometimes means "large size". * In Roman numerals, L means the number fifty. * The city of Chicago, Illinois, Illinois has an elevated train called the "Chicago 'L'". * A character from the manga and anime Death Note. * In Wheel of Fortune (US game show), L is one of the six letters given to any contestant participating in the "Bonus Round." * L is the symbol for litre, a unit of volume. * L can sometimes mean ‘lose’ as in “He took a big L." ==Other websites==
