'''Gay bashing''' and '''gay bullying''' is when someone attacks a person or group of people because they are gay man or lesbian or bisexual or transgender.Lussenhop, Jessica. [http://www.villagevoice.com/2012-05-30/news/education-gay-bullying-abuse/ "Schools Ignore Gay Bullying at Their Own Peril,"] ''Village Voice'', May 30 2012; retrieved 2013-2-19. Studies show that bullying happens to people who are gay or lesbian more often than to others in general. As teenagers, gay young men are more likely to be bullied than lesbians.
