File:Vienna Parlament 2005.jpg building in Vienna, Austria]] '''Minerva''' was the Roman mythology goddess of crafts and wisdom whose symbol was the owl. The Greeks called her Athena but the Romans didn't make her as warlike as Athena. As Minerva Medica she was goddess of medicine and doctors.
