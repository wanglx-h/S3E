'''Verónica Michelle Bachelet Jeria''' Companion of the Order of Australia (; born 29 September 1951) is a Chilean politician. She served as the President of Chile from 11 March 2014 to 11 March 2018. She was also President of Chile from 11 March 2006 to 11 March 2010. She was the first woman president of Chile. In 2010, Bachelet became the president of UN Women. In 2013, she resigned from that position. Bachelet is a socialist. Her father was general Alberto Bachelet (19231974) and her mother was archaeologist Ángela Jeria (19262020). In 2018, Bachelet became the Office of the United Nations High Commissioner for Human Rights.
