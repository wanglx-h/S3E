== Events == * February 3 – Tulip mania collapses in the Dutch Republic (now the Netherlands) by government order * February 15 – Ferdinand III, Holy Roman Emperor becomes Holy Roman Emperor * December 17 – Shimabara Rebellion erupts in Japan *Pierre de Fermat makes a marginal claim to have proof of what would become known as Fermat's last theorem. *France places a few missionaries in the Côte d'Ivoire, a country it would come to rule more than 200 years later. *England wages war against the Mashantucket Pequots * First opera house, ''Teatro San Cassiona'', opens in Venice * René Descartes – ''Discours de la Methode'' *Elizabeth Poole becomes the first woman to have founded a town (Taunton, Massachusetts) in the Americas.
