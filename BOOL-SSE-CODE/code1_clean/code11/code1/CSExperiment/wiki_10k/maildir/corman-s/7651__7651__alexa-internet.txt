| company_type = Subsidiary | foundation = 1996 | location = San Francisco, California, U.S | industry = Internet | parent = Amazon.com (acquired 1999) | products = Alexa Web search engine (discontinued 2008)Alexa toolbar | alexa = 2,066 () | website_type = Internet traffic and ranking | language = English | registration = Optional | current_status = Discontinued May 1, 2022 }} '''Alexa''' was an Internet service that measures how many Internet users visit a website. At Alexa.com, people entered a website address and Alexa showed them how well-visited the website is. It can also tell them how its popularity has changed over time. Alexa had 6 million unique visits monthly. Alexa was created in 1996 by American businessman Brewster Kahle and Bruce Gilliat. In 1999, Alexa was bought by Amazon.com for about US$250 million in Amazon stock. Alexa provided a free API that is used by services and toolbars all around the world to easily display Alexa Rating. In December 2021, Amazon.com announced that they will shutdown Alexa.
