File:Oldest tree in Sherwood Forest park.JPG of Sherwood Forest]] '''Sherwood Forest''' is an ancient woodland near Nottingham, England. It is famous as the home of the mythical outlaw, Robin Hood and his men (and Maid Marian). Legend claims that they lived near the Major Oak.
