Subject: Antwerp
File:Antwerp riverfront, april 2012.jpg river]] File:AntwerpCS May 2012.jpg File:Flag of Antwerp (City).svg File:Coat of arms of Antwerp (City).svg '''Antwerpen''' is a port city in Antwerp (province) in the north of Belgium. It is at 51° 12 North, 04° 24 East. In French it is called '''Anvers''', its English name commonly is '''Antwerp'''. It is the capital of the province with the same name. The port is the second-largest in Europe after Rotterdam in the Netherlands. It spreads over both banks of the river Scheldt for more than 11,000 hectares. About 16,000 people work in the port. It is a centre for international Illegal drug trade, especially cocaine. In 2007, 466,203 people lived there.
