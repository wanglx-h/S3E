Subject: Serj Tankian
File:Serj Tankian.jpg '''Serj Tankian''' (born on August 21, 1967 in Beirut, Lebanon) is an Armenian-United States musician who lives in Auckland, New Zealand. He was the lead vocalist of System of a Down. He is now a solo artist and has released five solo albums. ==Early life== Tankian was born in Beirut, Lebanon.
