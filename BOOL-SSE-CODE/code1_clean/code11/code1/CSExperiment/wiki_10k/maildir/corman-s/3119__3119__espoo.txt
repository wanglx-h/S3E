File:Uusimaa.png File:Espoon Tapiola kesällä.jpg File:Espoo.vaakuna.svg '''Espoo''' () is the second largest city in Uusimaa, Finland. As of January 2014, more than 260,000 people lived in Espoo. Other municipalities near to it are Kauniainen, Vantaa, Kirkkonummi, Nurmijärvi, Vihti and the capital Helsinki. ==Nature== There are several lakes in the northern parts of Espoo.
