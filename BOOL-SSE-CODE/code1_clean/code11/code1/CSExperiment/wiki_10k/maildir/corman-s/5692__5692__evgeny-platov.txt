Subject: Evgeny Platov
File:Grishuk and Platov 1994 Europeans.jpg '''Evgeny Platov''' (born August 7, 1967, Odesa, Ukraine). He was a Russian ice dancing, and began skating when he was nine years old. He studied at the Physical Institute of Culture in Moscow. Platov won two Olympic Games gold medals and four world championships with his partner, Oksana Grishuk After the 1998 Olympics in Nagano, Nagano, Japan, Platov started skating as a professional with a different partner, Maya Usova, until he retired from performing in 2002.
