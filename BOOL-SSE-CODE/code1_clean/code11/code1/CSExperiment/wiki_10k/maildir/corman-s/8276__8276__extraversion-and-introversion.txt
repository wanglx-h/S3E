Subject: Extraversion and introversion
File:20220822 Distinguishing introversion and extraversion (extroversion) - comparison chart.svg '''Introversion''' and '''extraversion''' (also spelled '''''extroversion''''') are ways of describing a person's personality. People who are more extraverted are called '''extraverts''' (or '''extroverts'''). People who are more introverted are called '''introverts'''. ==History== This idea was proposed by psychiatrist Carl Jung in the 1920s. ==Ideas== He said that introversion and extraversion affects how people Behavior socially (with other people), and also where they get mental energy from. Introverts prefer being alone and extraverts prefer being with other people. Introverts get energy and pleasure from wiktionary:self-reflect, and may feel tired after being around other people. Extraverts are the opposite. They get energy from spending time with other people, and may feel bored and tired after being alone. File:ExtrovertIntrovertSpectrum.png ===Spectrum=== Introversion and extraversion exists on a spectrum. Everyone has a different amount of introverted and extraverted personality Trait. People with more extraverted traits are called extraverts (or extroverts), and people with more introverted traits are called introverts. People who are around the middle of the spectrum may be called ambiverts. == Introversion vs. shyness and social anxiety == File:20220801 Introversion - Shyness - Social anxiety disorder - comparative chart.svg, and Social anxiety disorder.]] Some people mistake introversion for shyness or Social anxiety disorder because they can appear similar, but they are separate things. Introversion is a personality trait, but shyness and social anxiety are caused by Distress (medicine).
