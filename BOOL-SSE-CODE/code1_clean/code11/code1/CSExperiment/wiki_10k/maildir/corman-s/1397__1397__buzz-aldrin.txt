As a Presbyterian elder, Aldrin was the first and only person to hold a religious ceremony on the Moon. Using a kit given to him by his pastor, he took Eucharist during his moon mission. He received the Presidential Medal of Freedom and the NASA Exceptional Service Medal. After the death of Michael Collins (astronaut) in 2021, Aldrin became the last surviving member of the Apollo 11 crew. == Later life == Aldrin left NASA in March 1972. He has been marriage four times: to Joan Archer, to Beverly Zile, to Lois Driggs Cannon, and to Anca V Faur. He had three children with Joan, named James, Janice, and Andrew.
