Subject: Bill Cullen
File:Bill Cullen 1954.JPG '''Bill Cullen''' (February 18, 1920 July 7, 1990) was an Americans television game show television presenter. His full name was '''William Lawrence Cullen'''. Cullen was originally from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. He suffered from polio as a child. He had a limp for the remainder of his life. Cullen was best known for hosting the original version of ''The Price is Right'' on the television networks NBC and American Broadcasting Company. That show is now on CBS and hosted by Drew Carey. He also hosted a version of ''Pyramid (game show)'', ''Child's Play'', and ''Blockbusters''. Bill Cullen died in Bel Air, California in 1990 from lung cancer after smoking for most of his life.
