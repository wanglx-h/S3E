In 2010, the city had a population of 9,100,000 people. Since Bangkok has more than 55 times as many people as the second largest city, Nonthaburi (province), it is a primate city. Bangkok has a tropical savanna climate (''Aw'' in the Koeppen climate classification). Bangkok is the world's most popular tourist destination; 23 million visitors stay overnight there per year.
