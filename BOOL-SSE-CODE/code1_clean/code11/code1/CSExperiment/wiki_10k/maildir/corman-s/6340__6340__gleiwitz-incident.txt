Subject: Gleiwitz incident
File:Sender gliwice.jpg The '''Gleiwitz incident''' was a planned German attack against the radio station "Sender Gleiwitz" in the German town of Gliwice on the night of August 31, 1939. This attack along with other Nazi activities was used to make people think that Poland attacked Germany and that Poland, not Germany started World War II.
