Subject: Mork & Mindy
'''''Mork and Mindy''''' is an United States Television programme sitcom which ran from 1978 to 1982. It stars Robin Williams and Pam Dawber as the title fictional character. The series was made by Garry Marshall. Comedian Jonathan Winters has a recurring role in the series. ''Mork and Mindy'' is a spin-off from Marshall's other popular TV series, ''Happy Days''.
