File:FaithEvansApr05.jpg '''Faith Evans''' (born June 10, 1973 in Lakeland, Florida) is an United States Rhythm and blues singer. She grew up in Newark, New Jersey New Jersey. Her first husband, The Notorious B.I.G., died in a drive-by shooting in 1997 . She has been arrested for cocaine and marijuana.
