Subject: Phoebe (Titan)
File:Pergamonmuseum - Antikensammlung - Pergamonaltar 27.JPG]] '''Phoebe''' is a Titan (mythology) in Greek mythology. She was associated with the moon. She is the daughter of Uranos and Gaia. She was married to her brother Coeus, and had two daughters: Leto and Asteria. Leto bore Artemis and Apollo, Asteria is the mother of Hecate. The Olympians beat the Titans in the ten-year war. She and the Titans were summoned by Zeus to Tarturus, in the Underworld. Category:Titans
