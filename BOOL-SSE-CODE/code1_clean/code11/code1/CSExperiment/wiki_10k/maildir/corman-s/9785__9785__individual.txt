Subject: Individual
'''Individual''' can be an adjective or a noun. As an adjective, a living thing that is individual is not in a group (by him/her/itself). An object that is individual is for only one person or thing (not shared by a group). Something that is individual can also be different from anyone else (unique). *''Some students learn better with tutors because they get individual attention.'' *''He has a very individual taste in music.'' As a noun, an individual is only one person or thing, not a group.
