Subject: Andrology
'''Andrology''' is the study of male health, especially male sexual organs and reproduction. It is similar to the study of women's health, called gynaecology. Andrology has only been studied since the late 1960s. The first specialist journal on the subject was the Germany periodical ''Andrologie'', published from 1969 onwards.''Social Studies of Science'' (1990) '''20''', p. 32 Men are more susceptible to heart disease than women. They also usually have a slightly shorter natural average life span.
