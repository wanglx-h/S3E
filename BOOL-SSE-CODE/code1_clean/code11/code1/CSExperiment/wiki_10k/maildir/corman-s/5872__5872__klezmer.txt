Subject: Klezmer
File:Ensemble Klesmer Wien 20090618 'Lebedik un Freilech' triptych.jpg '''Klezmer''' is a kind of music. It was made by the Jews of Eastern Europe, and it is a mix of Russian, Greece and Romanian folk music, based on Jewish prayer music. In old times, it was played during Jewish weddings and religious festivals, and it was made for dancing. The most common klezmer musical instrument are the clarinet and the violin, because they are the best to play in the sad klezmer style. Category:Folk music
