She is the younger sister of pop singer Britney Spears. She played the lead role in the television programme ''Zoey 101''. Spears is now a country music singer. In November 2013 Spears released her first single, "How Could I Want More". She has been a celebrity contestant on ''Dancing with the Stars'' and I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here!. == Personal life == In late December 2007 it was revealed that Jamie Lynn was pregnant.
