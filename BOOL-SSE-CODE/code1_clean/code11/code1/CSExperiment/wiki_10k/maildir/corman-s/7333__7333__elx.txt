Subject: Elx
Image:Flag of Elx.svg Image:Escut d'Elx.svg '''Elx''' (in Catalan language) or '''Elche''' (in Spanish language) is the third biggest city in Valencia (autonomous community), and the second in the Alicante province, in eastern Spain. The city has 215,000 inhabitants and is famous for its palm trees and the Mystery of Elx, a theatrical piece.
