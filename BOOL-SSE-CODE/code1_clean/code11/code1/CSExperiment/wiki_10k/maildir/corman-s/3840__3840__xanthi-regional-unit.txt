Subject: Xanthi (regional unit)
Image:Πύργος του Ρολογιού (Ξάνθη).jpg '''Xanthi''' is a Greek city and its one of 51 prefectures in Greece. Its population is 46,464 inhabitants. It is located in the north-east of Greece, in the district of Western Thrace. Many Muslims live in Xanthi. So this city has an oriental color. == References == Category:Cities in Greece
