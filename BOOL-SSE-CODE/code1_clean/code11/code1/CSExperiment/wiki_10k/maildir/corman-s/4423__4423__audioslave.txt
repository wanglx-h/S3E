* }} | years_active = | label = | associated_acts = | past_members = * Chris Cornell * Tim Commerford * Tom Morello * Brad Wilk }} '''Audioslave''' was an United States rock music band. The band is made up of Chris Cornell (singer), who was with Soundgarden until 1997, and Tom Morello (Guitarist), Tim Commerford (bass guitar) and Brad Wilk (Drummer), who were with Rage Against The Machine until 2000. Their first album ''Audioslave'' was released in 2002 and the next one, named ''Out of Exile'', followed in 2005. On May 6, 2005 Audioslave became the first American rock group to play in Cuba.
