Subject: David Crosby
File:David Crosby 2019 by Glenn Francis.jpg '''David Van Cortland Crosby''' (August 14, 1941 January 18, 2023) was an Americans guitarist, singer and songwriter. He was most celebrity as a founding member of the bands The Byrds and Crosby, Stills, and Nash. He was born in Los Angeles, California. In the 2020 United States presidential election, Crosby supported Vermont United States Senator Bernie Sanders. Crosby died suddenly on the night of January 18, 2023 at his home in Santa Ynez, California. He was 81.
