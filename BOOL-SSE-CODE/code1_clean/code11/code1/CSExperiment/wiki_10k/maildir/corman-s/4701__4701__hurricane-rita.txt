Subject: Hurricane Rita
'''Hurricane Rita''' was a powerful hurricane in the 2005 Atlantic hurricane season. It hit the United States' Gulf Coast of the United States. At its peak intensity, it was a strong Saffir-Simpson Hurricane Scale storm with sustained winds of 175 miles per hour. It made landfall as a Saffir-Simpson Hurricane Scale storm, and caused more flooding in New Orleans, which already had weakened Levee due to Hurricane Katrina.
