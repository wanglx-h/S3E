Subject: Eckerö
Image:Eckeroe-StLarsKyrka_02.jpg Image:Eckerö.vaakuna.svg '''Eckerö''' is a municipality in the Åland Islands, Finland. As of March 2006, there are 926 people living in Eckerö. Eckerö is the westernmost municipality of Finland. Mariehamn, the capital (city) of Åland, is located 35 km away. 95 % of the people in Eckerö speak Swedish language as their first language. == Other websites == * [http://www.eckero.aland.fi/ The official Eckerö page], in Swedish, Finnish, English and German Category:Municipalities of the Åland Islands
