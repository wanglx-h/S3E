Subject: 1530s
The '''1530s''' was a decade that began on 1 January 1530 and ended on 31 December 1539. It is distinct from the decade known as the '''''154th decade''' which'' began on January 1, 1531 and ended on December 31, 1540. == Events and trends == * Spanish conquest of Peru * Beginning of colonization of Brazil * Henry VIII and his control over the English church. * Reformation in Denmark and Norway.
