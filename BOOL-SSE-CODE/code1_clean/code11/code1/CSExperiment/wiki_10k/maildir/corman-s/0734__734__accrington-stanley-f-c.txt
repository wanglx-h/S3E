Subject: Accrington Stanley F.C.
'''Accrington Stanley Football Club''' is an England football (soccer) club. The club will play in Football League Two (the English fourth division) in the 2015/16 season. They were re-wikt:founder in 1968 after going bankrupt (having no money) in 1962. They were first founded in 1921. They are thought of a hard working club but having no chance of success. Their nickname is Stanley. They play at the Wham Stadium in Accrington in Lancashire, in England.
