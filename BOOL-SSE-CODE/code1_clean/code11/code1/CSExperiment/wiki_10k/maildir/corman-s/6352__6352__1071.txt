Subject: 1071
== Events == * April — The last Byzantine-controlled city in southern Italy, Bari, is captured by Robert Guiscard. * August 26 — Byzantine Empire loses Battle of Manzikert to Seljuk Turks army under Alp Arslan. Control of much of Asia Minor passes to the Turks. * Construction of Richmond Castle in North Yorkshire, England begins. Category:1071
