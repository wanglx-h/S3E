Subject: George of the Jungle
'''''George of the Jungle''''' is an animation television series created by Jay Ward Productions in the late 1960s as a parody (pretending to be but in a way that makes fun of) of Tarzan. In 1997, it was made into a live-action movie by Walt Disney Pictures, with Brendan Fraser as the main character. There was a sequel George of the Jungle 2 released direct-to-video in 2003. There was and is a video game based on the film which was and is 2D Platformer game ''George of the Jungle'' by Ignition Entertainment/Crave in 2008.[http://uk.gamespot.com/wii/adventure/georgeofthejungle/index.html?tag=result%3Btitle%3B0 Gamespot UK] ==References== Category:Animated television series
