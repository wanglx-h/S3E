Subject: Office Space
'''''Office Space''''' is a 1999 United States comedy movie starring Ron Livingston and Jennifer Aniston. The movie was movie director by Mike Judge. The movie is a satire of white collar office work in the United States. ==Reception== '''Office Space''' received mostly positive reviews from reviewers, which means that most of them thought that it was a good movie. However, it did not make very much money at movie theaters, only earning $10,827,810 from movie tickets.
