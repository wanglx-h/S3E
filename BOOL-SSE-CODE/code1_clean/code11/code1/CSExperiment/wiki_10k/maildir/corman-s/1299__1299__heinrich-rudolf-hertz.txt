File:Heinrich Rudolf Hertz.jpg '''Heinrich Rudolf Hertz''' (22 February 1857 â€“ 1 January 1894) was a germany physicist. In 1888 he discovered the radio waves previously predicted by Maxwell's equations. He also proved that light is a kind of electromagnetic waves. The unit for frequency is named after him. Hertz was born in Hamburg in 1857. He studied engineering in Frankfurt and later at the University of Munich. He completed his Doctor of Philosophy at the University of Berlin.
