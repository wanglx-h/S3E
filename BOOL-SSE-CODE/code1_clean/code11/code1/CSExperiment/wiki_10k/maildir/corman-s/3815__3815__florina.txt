Subject: Florina
Image:Sakoulevas.jpg '''Florina''' (known also by the South Slavic name Lerin) is a city in Greece in the region of Macedonia (Greece). It is the capital of the '''Florina''' prefecture. Its population is 15,265 inhabitants. Florina is build at the foot of Varnus mountain. It is built at an altitude of 600 meter approximately. Around Florina there are many forests.
