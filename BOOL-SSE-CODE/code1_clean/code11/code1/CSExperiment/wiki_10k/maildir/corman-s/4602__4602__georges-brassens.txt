Subject: Georges Brassens
File:Brassens TNP 1966.jpg '''Georges Brassens''' (October 22, 1921 - October 29, 1981) was a France singer and songwriter. He sang ''La mauvaise réputation'' (the bad reputation) and ''Le gorille'' (the gorilla).
