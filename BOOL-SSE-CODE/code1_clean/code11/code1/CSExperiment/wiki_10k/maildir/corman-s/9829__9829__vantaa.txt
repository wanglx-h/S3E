File:Vantaa.vaakuna.svg '''Vantaa''' () is the fourth largest city in Finland (after Helsinki, Espoo and Tampere). It is part of the Greater Helsinki area. The most important parts of Vantaa are Tikkurila, Hakunila, Koivukylä, Korso, Martinlaakso and Myyrmäki. Tuusula, Kerava, Sipoo, Helsinki, Espoo and Nurmijärvi are neighbour community and city of Vantaa. Vantaa has been a city since 1974. As of April 2014, the population was about 208,800. The current mayor is Juhani Paajanen. The biggest airport in Finland, Helsinki-Vantaa (airport), is in Central Vantaa. Finnish language and Swedish language are the official languages of Vantaa.
