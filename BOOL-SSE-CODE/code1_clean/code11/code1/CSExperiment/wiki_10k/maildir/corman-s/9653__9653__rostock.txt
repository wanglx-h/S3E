It is in the States of Germany of Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania. Rostock has over 200,000 people (204,260 in December 2011). It has an old university (founded in 1419). The town is on the river Warnow. The mayor is Roland Methling. The city today has the name ''Hansestadt'' (Hanseatic city) in memory of its history, where it has been an important part of the Hanseatic League. The architecture of the city and the region is influenced by this historic period. Rostock is on the Baltic Sea. It has an important harbor. A district of the city, Warnemünde, is famous for its long beaches. It has a humid continental climate (''Dfb'' in the Koeppen climate classification). The most important association football club of Rostock is F.C. Hansa Rostock. It is in the German Bundesliga.
