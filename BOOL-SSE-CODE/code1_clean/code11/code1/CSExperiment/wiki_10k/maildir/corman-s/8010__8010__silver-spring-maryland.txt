Subject: Silver Spring, Maryland
'''Silver Spring''' is a community in the U.S. state of Maryland. It is a suburb of Washington, DC. It is the fifth biggest place in Maryland by population.1970 United States Census, as adapted in :en:Template:Largest cities of Maryland. Its population in 2020 was 81,015.
