Subject: Emission
'''Emission''' is a word that originally comes from Latin language. Originally it means something that is sent out. Several things can be sent out: * The most common use talks about emission of toxic gases. Those gases get produced by factories and motors, like motors in cars. * People talk about radio emissions. Those are programmes that people can listen to if they have a radio receiver. * There could be emissions of loud noise, someone might be making loud noises. * There also are emissions of light.
