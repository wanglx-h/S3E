File:Female hands.jpg File:Scheme human hand bones-en.svg A '''finger''' is a type of digit attached to the hand. Our type of fingers are similar to those of other primates.#Cha1998#OxfIll They are used for doing things and Wikt:sensation things. Humans have five digits, the bones of which are termed phalanges. The first digit is the thumb, followed by the index finger, middle finger, ring finger, and little finger or 'pinky'. According to different definitions, the thumb can be called a finger, or not. The four fingers have three phalanges each; the thumb has two. == References == Category:Basic English 850 words Category:Limbs and extremities
