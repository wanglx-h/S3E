Subject: Weapon of mass destruction
'''Weapons of mass destruction''' are weapons that can cause a lot of damage to many people. In general, people see the following as weapons of mass destruction: *Chemical weapons, like poisonous gases and substances (e.g. Nerve agent) *Biological weapons. Those are usually Pathogen, or other small living things that may cause a disease. Examples are smallpox or Anthrax (disease). *Nuclear weapons == Related pages == * CBRNE Category:Weapons
