Subject: Stadium
File:Croke Park from the Hill, 2004 All-Ireland Football Championship Final.jpg]] A '''stadium''' is a building in which lots of people may watch an event. They are often used for football and Sport. Some stadiums are paid to add a company name to the name of their stadium. For example, ''Comerica'' Park in Detroit, Michigan, Michigan, or the ALLTEL Stadium in Jacksonville, Florida. When people do this the company has the ''naming rights'' to the stadium. The given name of a stadium can change when another company buys the naming rights to that stadium. An '''arena''' is a small indoor stadium. Arenas are often used for many different types of events. These include sports and music. == Other websites == * [http://www.worldstadia.com worldstadia.com] Category:Stadiums
