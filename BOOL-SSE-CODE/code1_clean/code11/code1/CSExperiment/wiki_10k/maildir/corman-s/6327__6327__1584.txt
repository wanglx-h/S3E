'''1584''' (Roman numerals) was a leap year Leap year starting on Sunday in the Gregorian calendar and a leap year starting on Wednesday in the Julian calendar. == Events == * June 1 – With the death of the François, Duc d'Anjou, the Huguenot Henry IV of France becomes heir-presumptive to the throne of France. * July 5 – Maronite College established in Rome * July 10 – William I of Orange assassinated * Change to the new Gregorian Calendar in many countries. Dates between October 5 – October 14 ignored. * The first translation of the complete Bible into Slovenian language: ''Bibilija, tu je vse svetu pismu stariga inu noviga testamenta, slovenski tolmačena skuzi Jurija Dalmatina'' (Wittenberg), i.e. The Bible, it is a Complete Holy Scripture of the Old and of the New Testament, Translated into Slovenian by Jurij Dalmatin. * Archangelsk is founded in northern Russia. * Feodor I of Russia succeeds his father Ivan IV of Russia as Tsar of Russia * Latin Book of Concord published in Germany * Ghent falls to the Spain.
