R. Pertwee, mmn = Scholts– 20 May 1996), better known as '''Jon Pertwee''', was an English actor. He is best known for playing the role of the Third Doctor on ''Doctor Who'' from 1970 to 1974 and also the title scarecrow in the television children's comedy television programme ''Worzel Gummidge (TV series)''. His son is actor Sean Pertwee.
