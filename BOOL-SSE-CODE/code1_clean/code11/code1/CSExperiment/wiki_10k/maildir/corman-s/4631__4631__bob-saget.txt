He was nominated for a Grammy Award in 2014 for a comedy album that he made. ==Personal life== Robert Lane Saget was born on May 17, 1956, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, to Dolly and Benjamin Saget. He attended Abington Senior High School in Abington Township, Montgomery County, Pennsylvania, Montgomery County, Pennsylvania, Pennsylvania, before attending Temple University. Saget married Sherri Kramer on May 16, 1982, and had three children with Kramer: Aubrey, Lara, and Jennifer. The pair divorced on November 10, 1997. He married Kelly Rizzo on October 30, 2018. ==Death== On January 9, 2022, Saget was found dead at his hotel room in Orange County, Florida near Orlando, Florida at the age of 65.
