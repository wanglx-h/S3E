Subject: Head
The '''head''' is the part of the body where the brain is, which is protected and surrounded by the cranium, which is part of the skull. It is also where the face is, which has a lot of your wikt:sense like seeing and hearing. Not all wikt:animal have heads, like wikt:worm or a lot of other things that wikt:sponge. == In medicine == Some people get pains in their head wikt:occasionally, known as headaches. Also, some people have worse pains in their head called migraines. Some people heal people's heads, sometimes these people are special kinds of doctors called wikt:Neurology or Otorhinolaryngology doctors. == Uses == Different things may be worn on the head, for example hats or wigs. == Other meanings == A ''head'' in English language can also mean a person in charge of something, such as the head of a company. Also, the word ''head'' can also mean the wikt:front of something. An example of this is the word "Headline", meaning large words on the front page of a newspaper.
