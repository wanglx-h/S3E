| runtime = 110minutes | country = United States | language = English | budget = $41 million | gross = $242,875,078 }} '''''8 Mile''''' is a 2002 United States drama movie starring rapper Eminem, Kim Basinger, Brittany Murphy and Mekhi Phifer. It is set in 1995 in Detroit, Michigan. The film tells about a young white rap musicper living in inner city Detroit. He tries to make a rap career but most rappers are African Americans. The film was a critical and financial success.
