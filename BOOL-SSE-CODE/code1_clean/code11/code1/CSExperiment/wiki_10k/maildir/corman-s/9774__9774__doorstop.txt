Subject: Doorstop
Image:Rubber door stop in action.jpg A '''doorstop''' (or '''door stop''' or '''doorstopper''') is a tool. There are two types. One holds a door open (or ''stops'' it from closing), and the other stops the door from hitting a wall. Doorstops that keep the door from hitting a wall can be put on the door, or on the wall, or on the floor in front of the wall. Doorstops that are used to keep a door open come in two varieties: *Attached to the door they are supposed to stop *Wedge (mechanics) that can be put under the door they should stop, or anything heavy put in front of the door Category:Tools
