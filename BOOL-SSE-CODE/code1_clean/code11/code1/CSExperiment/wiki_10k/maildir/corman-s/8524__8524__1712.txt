'''1712''' (Roman numerals) was a leap year starting on Friday in the Gregorian calendar and a leap year starting on Tuesday in the Julian calendar. == Events == * January 26 – Tuscarora War: More than half of the Indigenous peoples in the United States in Colonel John Barnwell's Carolina militia desert during the crossing of the Cape Fear River. * January 29 – Tuscarora War: Colonel John Barnwell's Carolina militia attack the sprawling Tuscarora farming community of Torhunta by focusing on Fort Narhantes. Many Tuscarora captives are enslaved. Category:1712
