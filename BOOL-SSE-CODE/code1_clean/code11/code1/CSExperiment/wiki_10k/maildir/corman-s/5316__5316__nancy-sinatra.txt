Subject: Nancy Sinatra
File:Nancy Sinatra (1967).png '''Nancy Sandra Sinatra''' (born June 8, 1940) is an Americans singer and actress. She was born in Jersey City, New Jersey. She is the elder daughter of Frank Sinatra. She is best known for the song "These Boots Are Made for Walkin'".
