Subject: Whitchurch, Shropshire
'''Whitchurch''' is a small market town in the north of the county of Shropshire, England. It is the oldest town inhabited in Shropshire. The town lies 2 miles (3 km) east of the Wales border. As of 2011, the town has a population of 9,781. The town was founded by the Ancient Rome and there are still some Roman artifact (archaeology) that can be visited in a Museum.
