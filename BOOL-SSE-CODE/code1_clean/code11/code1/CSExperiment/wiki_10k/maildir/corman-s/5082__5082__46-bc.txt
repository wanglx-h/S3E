Subject: 46 BC
Year '''46 BC''' was the last year of the Roman calendar. At the time, it was known as the '''Year of the Consulship of Julius Caesar and Marcus Aemilius Lepidus (triumvir)'''. ==Events== * Titus Labienus defeated Julius Caesar in the Battle of Ruspina. * Julius Caesar defeated the combined army of Pompey followers and Numidians at Battle of Thapsus. * Caesar leaves for Farther Hispania to deal with rebellion in Rome. * Caesar changes the Roman calendar to create the Julian calendar. The ''Julian Calendar'' remains the standard in the western world until the Gregorian Calendar in 1582. * Caesar appoints his nephew Augustus Caesar his heir. ==Births== * Publius Quinctilius Varus, Roman politician (d. 9 AD) == Deaths == *Cato the Younger (Marcus Porcius Cato) (b. 95 BC) *Quintus Caecilius Metellus Pius Scipio Nasica (b. c. 100 BC) *Vercingetorix, Gaul leader Category:46 BC
