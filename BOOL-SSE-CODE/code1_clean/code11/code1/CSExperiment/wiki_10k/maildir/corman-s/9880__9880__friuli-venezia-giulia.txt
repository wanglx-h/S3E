The Capital city is Trieste. The population is . Friuli-Venezia Giulia has a border with the Italian region of Veneto to the west, Austria to the north and Slovenia on the east. People in this region speak the Italian language, Friulian language, Slovene language and German language languages. == Provinces == * Province of Trieste (capital) * Province of Udine * Province of Gorizia * province of Pordenone == Geography == Friuli-Venezia Giulia has an area of and is Italy's most north-eastern region.
