Image:Flag of Dubai.svg Image:Coat of arms of Dubai.svg The city of '''Dubai''' is the largest and most Population city in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) and the capital city of the emirate of Dubai (emirate), UAE. The city is sometimes called "Dubai City" to prevent it from being mixed up with the emirate. Dubai is a city and emirate in the United Arab Emirates known for luxury shopping, ultramodern architecture and a lively nightlife scene. Burj Khalifa, an 828m-tall tower, dominates the skyscraper-filled skyline. At its foot lies Dubai Fountain, with jets and lights choreographed to music. On artificial islands just offshore is Atlantis, The Palm, a resort with water and marine-animal parks. File:Dubai from the plane.jpg == History == There are records of the town of Dubai from 1799. The city was a dependent of Abu Dhabi until 1833. The town of Dubai was an important Harbour of call for foreign tradesmen (chiefly Indians), who settled in the town. Summers are extremely hot, windy, and humid. The highest recorded temperature in the UAE is 52.1 °C (126 °F), recorded in July 2002. According to the UN, about 14 percent of the world population will face water scarcity by the year 2025. This shortage of water is expected to be worse in arid countries like the UAE.
