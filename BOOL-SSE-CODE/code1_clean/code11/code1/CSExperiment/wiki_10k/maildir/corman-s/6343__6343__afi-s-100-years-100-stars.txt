'''AFI's 100 Years... 100 Stars''' is a list of the top actors in Hollywood history. It was announced by the American Film Institute on June 16, 1999. The list promised one hundred people on it, but in the end there were only fifty. As of 2023, of the fifty stars listed, only one is still alive (Sophia Loren).
