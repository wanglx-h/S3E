Subject: 1090s
The '''1090s''' was the decade that started on January 1, 1090, and ended on December 31, 1099. It is distinct from the decade known as the '''''110th decade''''' which began on January 1, 1091 and ended on December 31, 1100. == Events == * 1090 - Béjaïa becomes the capital of the Hammadid Dynasty in Algeria. * 1091 - King William II of England invades Normandy. * 1093 - Sviatopolk II of Kiev becomes Grand Prince of Kiev and ruler of Kievan Rus'. * 1094 - El Cid becomes ruler of Valencia (autonomous community), Spain. * 1095-1099 - The First Crusade Category:1090s
