Subject: Moss, Norway
Image:Moss komm.svg '''Moss''' is a coastal town in the Norway county of Østfold. It served an important role in the declaration of independence from the union with Sweden. The city used to have a lot of heavy industry, like shipyards and paper mills. Today it has mostly service sector businesses (shops, banks etc.) The city is also the centre of Moss Municipality which is 63km² (square kilometers) large and has 28,000 people as of 2006.
