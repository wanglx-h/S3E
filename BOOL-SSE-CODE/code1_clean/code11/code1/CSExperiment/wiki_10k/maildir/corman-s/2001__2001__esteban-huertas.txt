Subject: Esteban Huertas
File:Esteban Huertas.jpg '''Esteban Huertas López''' (1876-1943) was a Colombian military leader from Panama. He was born in Boyacá Department, Colombia. When he was 8 years old he ran away from home and he joined the army. He won many medals and in 1902 he became General officer. In 1900 he lost an arm when he fired a cannon. In 1903 he was one of the people who made Panama independence. A year later president Manuel Amador forced Huertas to retire because he was afraid Huertas wanted to become dictator. Huertas died in Panama City in 1943. Category:Panamanian people Category:1876 births Category:1943 deaths Category:Boyacá
