Subject: Joensuu
Image:Joensuu.jpg]] Image:Joensuu.lippu.svg Image:Joensuu.vaakuna.svg '''Joensuu''' is a city in Finland. It is the centre of the region of North Karelia. Joensuu has an area of 2.751,13 km² and a population of around 77,000 people. 369.3 km² of the area is water. The Finnish capital (city), Helsinki is located 437 km away from Joensuu. There is a university in Joensuu. The municipalities of Tuupovaara and Kiihtelysvaara were merged with Joensuu on January 1 2005.
