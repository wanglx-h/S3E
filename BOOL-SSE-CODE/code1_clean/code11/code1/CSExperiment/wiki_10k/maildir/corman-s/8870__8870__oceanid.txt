Subject: Oceanid
The '''Oceanids''' are the daughters of the Titan (mythology) Oceanus and Tethys (mythology) in Greek mythology. They are water and ocean Nymph. Also Oceanids can mean all the children of Oceanus and Tethys, which includes the other water and river gods. Among them are: *Klymene *Metis *Pleiades (mythology) *Styx Category:Nymphs
