Subject: Caregiver
A '''caregiver''', or '''carer''', is someone who has the job of caring for wikt:people. They usually care for people who are unable to care for themselves, for example, children, disability people, or the elderly. When a caregiver is assigned to look after a baby or a child they are sometimes called a babysitter. ==Related pages== *Parent *Infant attachment Category:Personal service occupations Category:Social care
