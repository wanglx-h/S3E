Subject: Lourdes Flores Nano
File:Medalla de Honor del Congreso a Lourdes Flores (cropped).jpg Dr.'''Lourdes Celmira Flores Nano''' (born October 7 1959) is a Peruvian lawyer and politician. She is the leader of the Christian People's Party. She tried to become the mayor of Lima in 2010. Flores Nano was born in Lima in 1959. She studied law at the Pontifical Catholic University of Peru. She studied for a Phd in Law from the Universidad Complutense de Madrid, one of the world's oldest universities in Madrid, Spain. She was a member of the Lima Municipal Council and then a member of the National Congress of Peru. In 2001 and 2006 she was a candidate in election for the President of Peru. ==References== Category:1959 births Category:Living people Category:Politicians from Lima
