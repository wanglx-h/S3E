That is why there is an idiom, "in the pink" — example: "These animals are in the pink today". === In particular regions === * In West nowadays, pink is the color for the female gender. (In the past, however, it was associated with the male gender.) * In India, pink is the color of masculinity. * In Japan, pink is the color most associated with springtime, and loosely associated with young men. == Other occurrences of Pink == * Pink is the stagename of United States singer Pink (singer).
