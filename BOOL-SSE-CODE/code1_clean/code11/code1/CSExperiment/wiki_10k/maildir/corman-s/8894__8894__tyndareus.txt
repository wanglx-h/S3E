Subject: Tyndareus
'''Tyndareos''' (or Latin language '''Tyndareus''') was a character in Greek mythology. He was the king of Sparta, and husband of Leda. He was the father of Klytaimnestra and Castor . His wife Leda also had Helen and Pollux (mythology) by the god Zeus. Menelaos, husband of Helen, became the next king of Sparta after Tyndareos.
