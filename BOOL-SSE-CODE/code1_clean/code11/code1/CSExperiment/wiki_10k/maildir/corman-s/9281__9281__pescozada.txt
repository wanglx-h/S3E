Subject: Pescozada
'''Pescozada''' is a hip-hop group from El Salvador. Their members are two men named Debil Estar and Fat Lui. They hip hop about gangs, politics, and the future of El Salvador. Their songs include "Primer Acto" and "I Could Fly". They rap in Spanish language.
