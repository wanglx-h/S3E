He was a United States Supreme Court Judge. He was nominated by President of the United States Gerald Ford in 1975. He voted with minority in George W. Bush v. Al Gore case (2000). He also voted with majority in Furman v. Georgia case (1976), but later became against the death penalty. In 2010, he announced his retirement in the Supreme Court.
