Subject: The Lost World: Jurassic Park
'''''The Lost World: Jurassic Park''''' (known also as '''''The Lost World''''') is an United States movie Film director by Steven Spielberg. It was released in 1997. It is the sequel to the movie ''Jurassic Park (movie)''. ''The Lost World'' is the second in a series of three movies. It was followed by ''Jurassic Park III'' and ''Jurassic World''. It is based on a book by Michael Crichton, and sticks generally to the plot of the book.
