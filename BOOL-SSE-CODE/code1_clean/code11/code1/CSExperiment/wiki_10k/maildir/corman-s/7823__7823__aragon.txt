Aragon has three Provinces of Spain (from north to south): Province of Huesca, Province of Zaragoza and Province of Teruel. Its Capital (city) is Zaragoza (sometimes called ''Saragossa'' in English). ==Geography== Aragon is one of the 17 Spanish autonomous communities. It has a surface area of , 9.4 percent of the territory of Spain. By Ranked list of Spanish autonomous communities by area, it is the fourth largest Spanish autonomous community after Castile and León, Andalusia and Castile-La Mancha. Aragon is bordered to the north by France (Nouvelle-Aquitaine Regions of France). Within Spain, the community is bordered to the east by Catalonia (Lerida Province and Tarragona Province Provinces of Spain), to the south by Valencia (autonomous community) (Castellón Province and Valencia Province provinces) and Castile-La Mancha (Cuenca Province and Guadalajara Province provinces), and to the west by Castile and León (Soria Province province), La Rioja and Navarre. ===Relief=== Most of Aragon has an altitude of .
