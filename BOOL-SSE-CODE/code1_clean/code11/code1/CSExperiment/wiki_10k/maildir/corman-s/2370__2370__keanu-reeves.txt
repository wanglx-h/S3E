| birth_place = Beirut, Lebanon | occupation = Actor, musician | height = 1.84 m | citizenship = | years active = 1984 - present | partner = *Jennifer Syme (1998 - 2000) *Alexandra Grant (2019-present) | children = }} '''Keanu Charles Reeves''' (born September 2, 1964) is a Canadians actor. He is known for his roles in movies like the ''Bill and Ted'' series, ''The Matrix'' series, ''Speed (movie)'', and ''John Wick (movie)''. He also played John Constantine in the 2005 movie ''Constantine (movie)''. At age 9, he started his acting career by appearing onstage, and at age 14, he appeared in Canadian television series and commercials. He also appeared in short movies and stage work in early 1980s. Keanu acted his first significant role in ''River's Edge'' (1987), and the next year (1988), he appeared in some movies such as ''Dangerous Liaisons'' and ''Permanent Record''. In 1989, he appeared in the successful comedy ''Bill & Ted's Excellent Adventure'', which was one of the ''Bill and Ted'' series. In 2008 he played the alien in ''The Day the Earth Stood Still''. In 2014, he began playing John Wick (character) in three movies: ''John Wick (movie)'', ''John Wick: Chapter 2'' and ''John Wick: Chapter 3 – Parabellum''. In 2022, he voiced Batman in the animated movie ''DC League of Super-Pets''. ==Early life== Reeves was born in Beirut, Lebanon. He is the son of Patricia Bond (née Taylor), an English-born costume designer/performer, and Samuel Nowlin Reeves, Jr. His father, who is a Hawaiian-born United States, has English, Native Hawaiian, Chinese, Irish, and Portuguese ancestry. Reeves has said: "My grandmother is Chinese and Hawaiian, so I was around Chinese art, furniture and cuisine when I was growing up".
