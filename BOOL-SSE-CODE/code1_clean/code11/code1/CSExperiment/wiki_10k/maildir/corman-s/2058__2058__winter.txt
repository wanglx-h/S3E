File:CathedralofLearningLawinWinter.jpg during winter.]]File:Kuznetsk Alatau 3.jpgFile:Winter forest silver.jpgFile:Countries receiving snowfall.svg'''Winter''' is one of the four season and it is the Cold time of the year. The days are shorter, and the nights are longer. Winter comes after autumn and before Spring (season). ==Overview== Winter begins at the winter solstice. In the northern hemisphere, the winter solstice is usually December 21 or December 22. In the southern hemisphere, the winter solstice is usually June 21 or June 22. Some animals Hibernation in this season. In Temperate climate there are no Leaf on deciduous Tree. People wear warm clothing, and eat food that was grown earlier. Many regions have snow in winter. ==Holidays== ===Christmas=== Holiday in winter for many countries include Christmas and New Year's Day. The name comes from an old Germanic word that means the "time of water" in reference to the rain and snow of winter in middle and high Latitude. Some nations do not have a time of the year which has Temperature too different than in other seasons.
