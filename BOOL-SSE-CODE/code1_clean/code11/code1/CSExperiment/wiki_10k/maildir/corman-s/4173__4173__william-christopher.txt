File:William Christopher Good Times 1975 (cropped).JPG '''William Christopher''' (October 20, 1932 December 31, 2016) was an Americans actor. He was best known for playing Father Mulcahy on the television series ''M*A*S*H''. He also played Private Lester Hummel on ''Gomer Pyle, U.S.M.C.'' Christopher was born in Evanston, Illinois. He studied at Wesleyan University. His wife was Barbara O'Connor; together they wrote a book, ''Mixed Blessings'', about their experiences raising their autism son. He retired in 2012.
