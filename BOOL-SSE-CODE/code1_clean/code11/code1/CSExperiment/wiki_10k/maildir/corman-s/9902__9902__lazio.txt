It is bordered to the northwest by the Tuscany region, to the north by the Umbria region, to the northeast with the Marche region, to the east with the Abruzzo and Molise regions, to the southeast is the Campania region and to the west is the Tyrrhenian Sea. The Vatican City is within the region. The main river in the region is the Tiber River. The highest mountain in the region is Monte Gorzano, on the border with the Abruzzo region, with an altitude of .
