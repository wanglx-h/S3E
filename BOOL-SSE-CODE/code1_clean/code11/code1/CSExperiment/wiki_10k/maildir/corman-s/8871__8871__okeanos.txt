Subject: Okeanos
'''Okeanos''' (Latin: '''Oceanus''') is a Titan (mythology) in Greek mythology. He is titan of the earth-encircling river Okeanos. His parents are Gaia (mythology) and Uranos. With his sister Tethys (mythology) his children are the river gods, the sea and spring nymphs, the Okeanids. Category:Titans Category:Gods and goddesses of water
