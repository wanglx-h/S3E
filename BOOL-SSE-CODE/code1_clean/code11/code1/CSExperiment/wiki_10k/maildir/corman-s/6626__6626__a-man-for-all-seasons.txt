Subject: A Man for All Seasons
'''''A Man for All Seasons''''' is a Play (theatre) by Robert Bolt about the life of Sir Thomas More that was first put on in 1960. It was made into a movie in 1966 that won the Best Picture Academy Award that year. It was also made into a TV movie in 1988.
