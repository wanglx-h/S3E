| Notable_instruments = | Current_members = | Past_members = Marie FredrikssonPer Gessle }} '''Roxette''' is a Sweden pop music group formed in 1986. The band had 2 members, Per Gessle and Marie Fredriksson. Their song "It Must Have Been Love" was one of the most sold single (music) by the duo. It was released first as a Christmas song in 1987 and then as an "ordinary song" in 1990. In 1991, Roxette release the very popularity album ''Joyride''. For this album, they went on concert worldwide, playing 107 concerts. "The Look" and "Listen to Your Heart", are other popular songs by Roxette. They are very popular in South America and Europe. They had released 8 studio albums and 9 compilation albums. One album, ''Baladas En Español'', was singing in Spanish language. They are the second most successful Swedish music group of all time, behind ABBA.
