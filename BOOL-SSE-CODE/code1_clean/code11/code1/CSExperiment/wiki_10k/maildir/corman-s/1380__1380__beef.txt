Subject: Beef
File:Standing-rib-roast.jpg '''Beef''' is a type of meat that comes from cattle. Beef can be cooked in many ways; cuts are usually used for steak, which can be cooked to many levels of doneness. Trimmings are usually Ground beef, like most hamburgers. Beef has protein, iron, and vitamin B12. High consumption of beef or other kinds of meat is linked to a higher risk of colorectal cancer and coronary heart disease, more so when processed meat. Beef has a high Environmental impact of meat production and is a main cause of deforestation. Beef makes the most Greenhouse gas of any farmed product. == Types of beef == * Ground beef * Steak * Corned beef * Steak rump Beef can also be in different types of grading such as: * Prime Beef * Choice Beef * Select Beef Category:Beef
