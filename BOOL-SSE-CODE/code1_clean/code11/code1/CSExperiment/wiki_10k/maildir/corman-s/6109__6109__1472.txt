== Events == * February 20 – The Orkneys and Shetlands are annexed to the crown of Scotland * Possible discovery of Bacalao (possibly Newfoundland, North America) and João Vaz Corte-Real. * Beginning of extensive slave trade in modern Cameroon, as the Portugal sail up the Wuori River. * Fernão do Po claims the central-African islands Bioko and Annobón for Portugal. * Banca Monte Dei Paschi Di Siena, the world's oldest bank, is founded.
