| runtime = 143 minutes | country = United States | language = English | budget = $140 million | gross = $654,264,015 }} '''''Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl''''' is a 2003 in film movie from Walt Disney Pictures. It is the first film of the ''Pirates of the Caribbean'' movie series. Audiences and Movie criticism gave the movie good reviews. It became one of the highest-Gross income movies in cinema history. == Story == When the movie begins, a young girl named Pirates of the Caribbean#Elizabeth Swann (Keira Knightley) is on a ship with her father, who is the major of a British colonization of the Americas in the Caribbean. Elizabeth sees a boy in the water. The crew of the ship saves him. He is named Pirates of the Caribbean#Will Turner (Orlando Bloom). Elizabeth finds a chain around his neck with a golden medallion. This leads her to believe that Will is a Piracy. She takes the medallion and keeps it. Eight years later, Elizabeth has grown up, and her father is now governor of Port Royal. James Norrington, a commodore in the Royal Navy, Marriage proposal to her. She accepts, even though she is in love with Will Turner, who is her father's blacksmith. When James Norrington proposes to her, she Faint (seemingly overcome by Norrington's proposal, but actually because her corset was too tight and she could not breathe). She falls off the ledge into the water below. Pirates of the Caribbean#Jack Sparrow (Johnny Depp) sees her fall and rescues her. Nevertheless, James Norrington believes Sparrow should be Arrest. Jack then threatens Elizabeth's life, and tries to escape. However, Will Turner stops his escape and Sparrow is taken to prison. That night, the Curse crew of the ''Black Pearl'' attack Port Royal and Kidnapping Elizabeth. Jack Sparrow escapes from prison. He steals a ship to travel to Tortuga (Haiti), a pirate port where he hopes to assemble a crew and find treasure. Will Turner goes with him to rescue Elizabeth. On their journey to Tortuga, Will finds out that his father was a pirate. Elizabeth, on board the ''Black Pearl'', learns that the crew is under the curse of a chest of Aztecs gold. They need the blood of each crew member to break the curse. However, a single member of the crew, named Bootstrap Bill, was killed. To escape the curse, the crew still needs his blood - or the blood of his Ancestor. Believing that Elizabeth descended from Bootstrap Bill, the crew plans to spill her blood on the chest of Aztec gold in hopes to break the curse. Will secretly takes Elizabeth to safety while the zombies of the ''Black Pearl'' Invasion a British Empire ship positioned not far from the land. There is an epic fight between the zombies, the British, Jack Sparrow (the former Sea captain of the ''Black Pearl''), Captain Barbossa (the current captain), Will Turner, Elizabeth Swan, and some leftover zombies. Jack Sparrow uses the one Bullet in his weapon to shoot Captain Barbossa. At the same time, Will cuts himself and puts his blood on the Aztec Gold, breaking the curse. The zombies were now humans, and Jack's Gunshot wound Captain Barbossa. The British win the fight against the zombies that are now humans.
