Here are a few electronic aids for writing Basic English. ==Hard word identification == ===Offline=== OpenOffice.org (OOo) is a program that does everything that Microsoft Office Professional does. OpenOffice is a free download (115 MB) or can be bought on Compact Disc (CD). The Basic English Institute has a spell checking word list [http://www.basic-english.org/down/download.html download] ::Replace the one of the number of languages of OOo spell checker with the [http://www.basic-english.org/down/readooo.html Basic English word list] of 5,000+ spellings. This includes derivatives and compound words which most other spell checkers overlook. Non-Basic words are marked as if they were spelled wrong. The BEI now offers a Simple English spell checking wordlist for OpenOffice software. Each non-Simple English word is underlined in red. [http://www.basic-english.org/down/readsimple.html Readme] ===Online=== The [http://www.hemingwayapp.com/ Hemingwayapp] and [http://splasho.com/upgoer6/ upgoer6] tools can help to identify complex writing and vocabulary. == Automated translation into Simple English == ===Offline=== [http://www.ilovelanguages.com/IDP/ Internet Dictionary Project] software called IDP Companion translates between several languages. Replace one of the minor languages with the [http://www.basic-english.org/down/readidp.html Basic English definitions] from the Institute [http://www.basic-english.org/down/download.html download] page. Enter the "misspelled" word and copy and paste the desired Basic replacement. A beta release of a thesaurus-like drop-down list for '''translation''' of the non-Simple English words into Basic English is also available. [http://www.basic-english.org/down/readthes.html Readme] .
