'''''Goodfellas''''' is a 1990 United States biopic crime movie movie director by Martin Scorsese. It is about life in the Mafia. It gained six wikt:nominate for Academy Awards. It won one (for Joe Pesci's role). Many people thought the movie was great. It is set in New York City. The movie is based on the life of mafia Henry Hill (1943-2012) from 1955 - 1980. ''Goodfellas'' uses information from New York crime reporter Nicholas Pileggi's book ''Wiseguy''. Scorsese and Pileggi worked together to write the movie. ''Goodfellas'' was first shown at the 1990 Venice Film Festival. Scorsese received the Silver Lion award for Best Director.
