Subject: Sailor
File:Five US Navy petty officers in uniform.jpg A '''sailor''' (or '''seaman''') is a person who works at sea on a boat or ship. The sailor works under a captain. The term is only used when the person is working professionally (earning money or make a living). Sailor is also the lowest rank in a navy.
