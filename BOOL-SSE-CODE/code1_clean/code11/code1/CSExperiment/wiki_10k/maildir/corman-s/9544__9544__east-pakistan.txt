Subject: East Pakistan
File:Dominion of Pakistan & Indian Controlled Kashmir (orthographic projection).svg '''East Pakistan''' was part of Pakistan. Between 1955 and 1971, Pakistan was made of West Pakistan (which is now simply called Pakistan) and East Pakistan, which is now known as Bangladesh. In 1971, there was the Bangladesh Liberation War, where East and West Pakistan fought each other. India also took part in the war, on the side of East Pakistan.
