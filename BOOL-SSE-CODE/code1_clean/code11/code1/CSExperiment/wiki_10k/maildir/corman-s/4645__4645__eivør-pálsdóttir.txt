| death_date = | origin = Faroe Islands | instrument = | genre = folk music rock music | occupation = | years_active = 1999–present | label = Tutl | associated_acts = Clickhaze | website = [http://www.eivor.com EIVOR.COM] | current_members = | past_members = }} Image:Eivor vid kommoduna.jpg '''Eivør Pálsdóttir''' (born 21 July 1983 in Syðrugøta, Faroe Islands) is a Faroe Islands music and composer. She sings in Faroese language, but also in English language, Icelandic language, Swedish language and Danish language. Eivør Pálsdóttir is one of the most well-known singers from the Faroe Islands. "Krakan", her second solo album, won the nomination for an Icelandic Music Award in three categories.
