They are often companions of Greek deity. Nymphs can be divided into different kinds, such as: * Dryads (trees) * Naiads (fresh water) * Oreads (mountains) * the '''Okeanids''', the daughters of Okeanos, and the '''Nereids''', the daughters of Nereus (seas) * the '''Pleiades (mythology)''' (stars) They are not immortal, like Greek gods or the titans from Greek mythology, but were said to live a long time. == Other websites == Category:Nymphs
