Subject: Schleswig-Holstein
'''Schleswig-Holstein''' (Danish language: ''Slesvig-Holsten'', Low Saxon: ''Sleeswiek-Holstain'', Frisian language: ''Sleeswyk-Holstein'', North Frisian language: ''Schleswig-Holstian'') is the northernmost of the 16 States of Germany (German: Bundesländer) in Germany. Schleswig-Holstein borders on Denmark in the North, the North Sea in the West, the Baltic Sea and Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania in the East, and Lower Saxony and Hamburg in the South. Kiel is the Capital (city) of this state. There are 4 independent towns: * Kiel * Lübeck * Neumünster * Flensburg ==Countryside== Image:Bungsberg 14.jpg|left]] The countryside is lowlands and hills with no mountains. The highest elevation in the state is the Bungsberg at only . There are many lakes, especially in the eastern part of Holstein called the Holstein Switzerland ("Holstein Switzerland") or Schweiziann-Schwezalia.
