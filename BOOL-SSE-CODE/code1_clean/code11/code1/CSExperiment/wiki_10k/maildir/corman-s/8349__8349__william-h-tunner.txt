Subject: William H. Tunner
Image:William Tunner at desk.jpg Lt. General '''William H. Tunner''' (July 14, 1906 - April 6, 1983) was an United States general. After the Burma Road was cut by the advancing Japanese in early 1942, he organized the Allied Logistics airlift from India in to China over the Hump which was to support Chiang Kai-Shek's Chinese forces and the Flying Tigers until the opening of the Ledo Road. He was the organizer of the Berlin Airlift (1948-1949).
