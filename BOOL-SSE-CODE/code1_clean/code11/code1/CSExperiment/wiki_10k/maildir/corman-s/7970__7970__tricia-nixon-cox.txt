Image:Nixon with daughter Tricia marriage 1971.jpg '''Patricia "Tricia" Nixon Cox''' was born on February 21, 1946 in Whittier, California. She is the daughter of former President (United States) Richard Nixon and Pat Nixon. She is the older sister of Julie Nixon Eisenhower. Unlike her sister, Tricia performed many ceremonial jobs, like going with her father to campaign stops and state visits. She married Edward F.
