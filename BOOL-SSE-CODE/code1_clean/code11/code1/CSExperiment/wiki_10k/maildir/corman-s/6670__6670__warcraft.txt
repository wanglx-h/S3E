Subject: Warcraft
'''''Warcraft''''' is a video game series made by Blizzard Entertainment. The games in this series are real-time strategy games, where players play as their characters and gain experience to level up. == Series == The different games from the series are ''Warcraft: Orcs and Humans'', ''Warcraft 2: Battle.net edition'' (with an expansion pack) and ''Warcraft 3'' and its expansion pack, ''The Frozen Throne''. The most successful game of the series is ''The Frozen Throne'' expansion pack, which sold more than 12 million copies. The MMORPG ''World of Warcraft'' is based on these games.
