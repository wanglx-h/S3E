File:Toyota Aygo 1.0 VVT-i Connect STYLE (2. Facelift) – Frontansicht, 10. März 2012, Ratingen.jpg The '''Toyota Aygo''' is a car made by the Japanese company Toyota in the Czech Republic since 2005. It has four seats and either two or four doors. The car has a 1-litre petrol or 1,4-litre diesel engine. The car is similar to the Peugeot 107 and Citroën C1. It entered its second generation in 2014. == Engines == * ''1.0 VVT-i'' 50 kW (68 hp) * ''1.4 D4-D'' 40 kW (54 hp) Category:Toyota automobiles Category:Front wheel drive vehicles Category:2000s automobiles Category:2010s automobiles
