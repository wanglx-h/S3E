Subject: Carly Simon
File:Carly Simon (1989).jpg '''Carly Simon''' (born June 25, 1945) is an Americans singer-songwriter and occasional children's book writer. She was born in New York City to businessman Richard L. Simon (1899-1960) and Andrea Heinemann Simon (1909-1994). She was raised in Riverdale, The Bronx. She has two sisters - singer Joanna Simon (mezzo-soprano) (born 1940) and composer Lucy Simon (born 1943). She also has a brother, photographer Peter Simon (photographer).
