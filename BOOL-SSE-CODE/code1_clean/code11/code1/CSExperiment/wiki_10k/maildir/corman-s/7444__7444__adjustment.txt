Subject: Adjustment
An '''adjustment''' is when something is changed in a small way. This is usually to make it better. The word can be used as a verb, as in "Bill needs to adjust his tie". Examples of adjustments: * The wrong word is written on a piece of paper. By erasing the word and writing the right one, an ''adjustment'' has been made to the paper. * A picture hung on a wall is not straight. It can be ''adjusted'' by moving it and making it straight.
