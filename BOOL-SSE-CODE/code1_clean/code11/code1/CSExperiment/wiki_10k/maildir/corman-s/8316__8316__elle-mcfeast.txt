Subject: Elle McFeast
'''Elle McFeast''' was a fictional character played by Australian comedienne '''Libbi Gorr''', who started off on a show with Andrew Denton about sport in Australia called "Live and Sweaty". Gorr made up her character's name to make fun of Australian model (person) Elle Macpherson. "McFeast" was the name of a hamburger from McDonald's. McFeast had her own show for many years in Australia. She talked about many scary things, like jails and criminals, and made fun of them. One day she interviewed Chopper Read on her show. He was so scary that she had to stop her show. Category:Television characters
