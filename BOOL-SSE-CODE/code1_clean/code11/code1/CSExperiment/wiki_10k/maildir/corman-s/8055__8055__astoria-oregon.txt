Subject: Astoria, Oregon
'''Astoria''' is a city in the United States U.S. state of Oregon. It is named after John Jacob Astor, who started a fur trading post, there in 1811. Many movies have been filmed there like ''The Goonies'', ''Kindergarten Cop'', and ''Free Willy''. Category:Cities in Oregon Category:County seats in Oregon
