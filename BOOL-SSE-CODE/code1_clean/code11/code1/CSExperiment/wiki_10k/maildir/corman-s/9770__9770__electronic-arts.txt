('''EA Games''') is a large company which makes video games all over the world. They made famous games such as ''FIFA (video game series)'', ''The Sims'', ''Madden NFL'' and ''Medal of Honor''. They also made the ''Need for Speed'' series. EAs most popular products are the games which are sold under the EA Sports label. == History == The company was started in February 1982 by Trip Hawkins. They first shipping their games in May 1983. They have since then produced video games every year. == Studios == EA owns other smaller game companies which they call 'studios'. A few of these include Maxis (creators of ''The Sims''), Bullfrog (creators of ''Theme Park (computer game)'' and ''Theme Hospital''), BioWare (makers of ''Mass Effect''), and PopCap Games (creators of Plants vs. Zombies). == Games == *''Pinball Construction Set'' (1983) by Bill BudgeIn 2008, ''Pinball Construction Set'' was awarded at the 59th Annual Technology & Engineering Emmy Award for "User Generated Content/Game Modification": [http://kotaku.com/342028/2008-tech-emmy-winners 2008 Tech Emmy Winners] *''Archon (computer game)'' (1983) and ''Archon II: Adept'' (1984) by Free Fall Associates *''M.U.L.E.''
