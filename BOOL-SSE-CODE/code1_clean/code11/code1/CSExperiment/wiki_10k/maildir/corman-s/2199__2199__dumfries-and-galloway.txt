Subject: Dumfries and Galloway
File:Dumfries and Galloway in Scotland.svg.]] '''Dumfries and Galloway''' is a Council area (Scotland) in south west Scotland. In takes in three county: * Dumfriesshire * Kirkcudbrightshire * Wigtownshire The main and biggest town of the region is Dumfries. Dumfries is 60 miles south of Edinburgh, the capital of Scotland.
