== Events == * December 22 – Afraid that Cairo would be captured by the Crusaders, its Fatimid Caliphate orders the city set afire. The city burns for 54 days. * Prince Richard of England becomes duke of Aquitaine. He later becomes King Richard I of England. * Emperor Takakura ascends to the throne of Japan. * King Valdemar I of Denmark conquers Arkona on the Island of Rügen, the strongest pagan fortress and temple in Northern Europe.
