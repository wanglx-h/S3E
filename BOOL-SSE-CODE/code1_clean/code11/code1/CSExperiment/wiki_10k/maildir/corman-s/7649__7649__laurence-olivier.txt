He was one of the most famous and revered actors of the 20th century.Hodgdon, Barbara. Shakespeare Quarterly, "From the Editor", Fall, 2002 He was the youngest actor to be Knight Bachelor and the first to be raised to the Life peer. Olivier married English actresses Jill Esmond, Vivien Leigh and Joan Plowright. Actor Spencer Tracy said that Olivier was 'the greatest actor in the English-speaking world'.Tracy quoted in ''By Myself and Then Some'' by Lauren Bacall (Harper Paperbacks 2006), pp. 214-15. In 1999, the American Film Institute named Olivier among the AFI's 100 Years...100 Stars, at number 14 on the list. Olivier had many awards: twelve Academy Award nominations, with two wins (for Best Actor and Best Picture for the 1948 film ''Hamlet (1948 movie)''), plus two honorary awards including a statuette and certificate. He was awarded five Emmy Awards. He was a three-time Golden Globe Award and British Academy of Film and Television Arts winner. In 1976 he received the BAFTA Academy Fellowship Award, a lifetime achievement award.
