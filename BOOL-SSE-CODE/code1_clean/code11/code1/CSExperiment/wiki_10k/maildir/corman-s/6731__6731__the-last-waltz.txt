Subject: The Last Waltz
'''The Last Waltz''' was final concert of the Canada/United States rock and roll band The Band. It took place on Thanksgiving (November 26) in 1976 at the Winterland Ballroom in San Francisco, California. It was also made into a movie by Martin Scorsese, that came out in 1978.
