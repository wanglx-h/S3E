Subject: Emeril Lagasse
File:Emeril Lagasse book signing.jpg '''Emeril Lagasse''' (born October 15, 1959) is an Americans celebrity chef. He is best known for his television programme ''Emeril Live'', which airs on the Food Network. He hosts a Cooking Channel show named ''Emeril's Florida''. He also has several restaurants and wrote some cookbooks. He is also famous for his catchphrases "Bam!" and "Kick it up a notch!"
