Subject: Soy milk
File:004-soymilk.jpg '''Soy milk''' (also called '''soymilk''', '''soya milk''', '''soybean milk''', '''soy bean milk''', '''soy drink''', or '''soy beverage''') is a milk-like product made from soy beans.
