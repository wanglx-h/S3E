Subject: Arcangelo Corelli
Image:Arcangelo Corelli 2.jpg '''Arcangelo Corelli''' (17 February 1653 8 January 1713) was an Italian violinist and composer. He composed some of the most important Italian music of the Baroque music period. He was also very famous as a violin player. Other violinists learned from his style of playing and developed the art of playing the violin. His works include sonatas and concerto grosso.
