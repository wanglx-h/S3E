Image:Zeulenroda-Rathaus.jpg Image:Wappen Zeulenroda-Triebes.svg '''Zeulenroda-Triebes''' is a German town in the Rural Districts of Germany of Greiz Rural District in Thuringia. It has about 18,000 inhabitants.
