Subject: 1565
'''1565''' (Roman numerals) was a common year starting on Monday in the Julian calendar and a common year starting on Friday in the proleptic Gregorian calendar. == Events == *March 1 – the city of Rio de Janeiro is founded. *April 27 – Cebu City is established becoming the first Spain settlement in the Philippines. *May 18 – The Ottoman Empire troops landed on the island of Malta. Siege of Malta (1565) began. *June 17 – Matsunaga Hisahide assassinates the 13th Ashikaga Shogun, Ashikaga Yoshiteru. *August 28 – St. Augustine, Florida (named after Augustine of Hippo), established. It is the oldest remaining European settlement in the United States. *September 4 – Spain fleet of Pedro Menéndez de Avilés lands on modern-day Florida to oust the Military history of France under Jean Ribault. He later destroys the French colonial empires colony of Fort Caroline. *September 8 – Pedro Menéndez de Avilés settles in St. Augustine, Florida. *September 8 – The Knights of Malta lift the Turkey Siege of Malta (1565) (siege started on May 18).
