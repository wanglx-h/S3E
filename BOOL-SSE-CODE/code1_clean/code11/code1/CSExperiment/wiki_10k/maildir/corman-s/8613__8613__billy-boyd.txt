He is best known for playing Peregrin Took in the The Lord of the Rings movie trilogy (2001–2003) and Barrett Bonden in Peter Weir's movie ''Master and Commander: The Far Side of the World'' (2003). == Talents == As well as being an actor, he can sing, and play the guitar, Bass guitar, and drums. He wrote and sang a song in Peter Jackson ''The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King (movie)''. He worked as a book-binder for 6 years before becoming an actor. One of the books he bound was ''The Lord of the Rings''. He lives in a house in Lesmahagow with his girlfriend, Alison McKinnon.
