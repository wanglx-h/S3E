Subject: Fountain of Youth
The '''Fountain of Youth''' is a legendary Spring (water). People talked about it for thousands of years. They thought its water could make people young and healthy. Herodotus put it in the Horn of Africa. Later stories said it was in India. A Spain explorer, Juan Ponce de Leon, was said to have looked for it while in present-day Florida. Category:Mythology
