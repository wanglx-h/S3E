Subject: Fire Brigades Union
The '''Fire Brigades Union''' ('''FBU''') is the main Trade union for firefighters in the United Kingdom. A union is a group of workers. They make the group to improve pay and working conditions. It was founded in 1918 in the London area as the ''Firemans Trade Union''. It soon grew to cover the entire country.
