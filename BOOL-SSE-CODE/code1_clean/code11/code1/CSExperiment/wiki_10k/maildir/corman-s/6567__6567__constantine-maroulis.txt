Subject: Constantine Maroulis
File:Constantine Maroulis star of off-broadway musical Rock of Ages.jpg '''Constantine Maroulis''' (born September 17, 1975) is an Americans singer, actor and writer from New York (state). He was on the popular television programme ''American Idol''. With his past work experiences, he stands out from other ''American Idol'' singers. Before he was on television, he sang in the rock music band Pray for the soul of Betty. Right now, he is taking a break from his band to sing on his own. He is the first ''Idol'' Alumni to sign a contract for the making of a new television sitcom.
