Subject: Satyr play
File:Satyros Cdm Paris DeRidder509.jpg '''Satyr plays''' were an Theatre of ancient Greece form of theatre, similar in spirit to burlesque. They featured Greek choruses of satyrs, were based on Greek mythology, and were full of mock drunkenness, brazen sexuality (including Phallus props), Practical jokess, sight gags, and general merriment.
