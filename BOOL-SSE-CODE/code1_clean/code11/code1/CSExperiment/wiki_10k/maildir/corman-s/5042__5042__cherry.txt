Subject: Cherry
A '''cherry''' is a fruit that grows on a tree or a Shrub. It belongs to the genus ''Prunus''. It is red in color, with a seed in the middle. It tastes slightly sour, and is often used to flavour cakes and ice cream, or is baked in a pie or cobbler. Cherries are also a good source of b vitamin complex.
