Subject: Fatu Hiva
Image:Rainforest Fatu Hiva.jpg on Fatu Hiva]] '''Fatu Hiva''' is a tropical island in the Pacific Ocean. It is part of French Polynesia and the Marquesas Islands. There are about 600 people living on Fatu Hiva. It is 22km away from Motu Nao. The three main villages are Hana Vave, Omo'a and Uia. Category:Islands of French Polynesia
