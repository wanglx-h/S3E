Subject: Estádio Palestra Itália
'''Estádio Palestra Itália''', also known as '''Parque Antártica''', was a football stadium of Sociedade Esportiva Palmeiras in São Paulo. It was demolition in 2010. When it closed, its capacity was 27,650. Category:Football stadiums in Brazil Category:Buildings and structures in São Paulo
