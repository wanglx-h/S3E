File:100m women Golden League 2007 in Zurich.jpg.]] '''Sprints''' are short running races in Athletics (track and field). They are events in which runners will not have to "pace themselves", but can run as fast as possible for the entire distance. These are often some of the more popular events in the Olympic Games.
