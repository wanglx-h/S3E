'''1736''' (Roman numerals) was a leap year starting on Sunday in the Gregorian calendar and a leap year starting on Thursday in the Julian calendar. == Events == * January 26 – Stanislaus I of Poland abdicates his throne * February 12 – Francis I, Holy Roman Emperor weds Maria Theresa, mother of Marie Antoinette * April 14 – Porteous Riots erupt in Edinburgh after execution of a smuggler Andrew Wilson when town guard captain John Porteous orders his men to fire at the crowd. Porteous is arrested later * May 8 – Marriage of Frederick, Prince of Wales and Augusta of Saxe-Gotha. * May 26 – Battle of Ackia: Great Britain and Chickasaw Native Americans defeat France troops. * September 7 – Edinburgh crowd drags John Porteous out of his cell in Tolbooth prison and lynching him * A French expedition led by Pierre Louis Maupertuis is sent by King Louis XV of France to Lapland to measure the length of a degree of the meridian, and proves that the Earth is flattened at the poles * ''Real Arissona'', wikt:namesake of the U.S. state Arizona is founded in what is now that state. * Isaac Newton Publishes his ''Method of Fluxions'' * Thomas Bayes publishes a defense of Isaac Newton's calculus * Leonhard Euler solves the mathematical problem known as the seven bridges of Königsberg Category:1736
