Its name comes from the Iowa River, which was named after the Ioway people, one of the Native American tribes that lived in Iowa. Iowa was a part of New France, but was sold to the United States as part of the Louisiana Purchase. Its settlers were mostly farmers: Iowa is part of the Corn Belt and is often known as the "Food Capital of the World." However, Iowa's landscape, culture, and economy are diverse, with the economy changing in the second half of the 20th Century to include many kinds of business. Iowa has about 3,200,000 people . Its Capital (city) and largest city is Des Moines, Iowa. Iowa became a state on December 28, 1846. It was the 29th state to join the United States. Iowa is divided into 99 counties. ==Geography== File:Iowa topography.jpg Iowa is in the Midwestern United States. To the north of Iowa is Minnesota; to the west are Nebraska and South Dakota; to the south is Missouri; and to the east are Illinois and Wisconsin. The eastern border of the state is marked by the Mississippi River which runs between Iowa and Illinois. The western border is marked by the Missouri River and Big Sioux River rivers. The northern border is a line 43 degrees, 30minutes north latitude. The southern border follows the northern border of Missouri.Iowa’s borders are definition in the Preamble to the [http://www.legis.state.ia.us/Constitution.html#pre1 state Constitution] . The Missouri and Mississippi river boundaries are as they were mapped in the 19th century, which can be different than their modern wikt:course. Iowa and Missouri disagreed about the location of the Iowa-Missouri border. This argument was ended by the Supreme Court of the United States in the 1896 case, ''State of Missouri v. State of Iowa'',[https://supreme.justia.com/cases/federal/us/48/660/case.html ''State of Missouri v. State of Iowa''], 48 U.S. (7 How.) 688 (1896). after a standoff called the Honey War. The border follows the Des Moines River for the far eastern part of the state and is at close to 40 degrees, 35minutes north for the rest of the state. Iowa has 99 County#United States, but 100 county seats because Lee County, Iowa has two. The state capital, Des Moines, Iowa, is in Polk County, Iowa. Most of Iowa is considered to be a plain. ==Law and Government== The government of Iowa has three branches, similar to the federal government of the United States. The executive (government) branch is headed by the governor, currently Kim Reynolds (R) since May 24, 2017. The legislature branch is the Iowa General Assembly, composed of two houses - the Iowa Senate and the Iowa House of Representatives. The judiciary branch is headed by the Iowa Supreme Court under the judge, currently Susan Christensen . There are two major political party in Iowa, the Iowa Democratic Party and the Republican Party of Iowa, as well as several unofficial third party (politics). The Republican Party controls the government ; the governor is a Republican and the House and Senate both have Republican majorities. Iowa sends two United States Senate and four United States House of Representatives to United States Congress. Iowa is well known for its agriculture. Its main agricultural outputs are hogs, corn, soybeans, oats, cattle, and dairy products. Its industrial outputs include food processing and machinery.
