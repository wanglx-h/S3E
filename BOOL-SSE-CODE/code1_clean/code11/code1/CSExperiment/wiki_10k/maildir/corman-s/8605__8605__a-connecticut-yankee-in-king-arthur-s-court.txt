Subject: A Connecticut Yankee in King Arthur's Court
File:Connecticut Yankee frontispiece by Beard.jpg '''''A Connecticut Yankee in King Arthur's Court''''' is a novel. It was written by Mark Twain. It was published in 1889. It is one of the first stories about time travel. == Story == Hank Morgan is an United States from late 19th century Connecticut. He travels back in time to King Arthur Camelot. He has a lot of knowledge of science and technology. He becomes very powerful. People call him "The Boss". He tries to improve Middle Ages society according to the morals and technology of his time. In the end, Merlin puts Hank in a magic sleep. Hank wakes in his own time. Category:Books by Mark Twain Category:1889 books Category:19th-century American novels
