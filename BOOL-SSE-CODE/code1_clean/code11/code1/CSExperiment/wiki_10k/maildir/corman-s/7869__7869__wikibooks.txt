| commercial = No | type = Textbooks wiki | language = multilingual | registration = Optional | owner = Wikimedia Foundation | author = b:User:Karl Wick and the Wikimedia Community | launch_date = | current_status = Active }} '''Wikibooks''' (previously called '''Wikimedia Free Textbook Project''' and '''Wikimedia-Textbooks''') is a collection of free books. It is one of many projects that are run by the Wikimedia Foundation. It is a wiki, which makes it possible for anyone to edit. Some books are original, but others have started as text copied from other sources of free-content textbooks found on the Internet. The CC-BY-SA license covers all the content that is in Wikibooks. The project consists of both a magazine and a website and is currently being developed in English, Danish, Finnish, French, German, Italian, Japanese, and Spanish. It is funded by a grant from the Beck Foundation.
