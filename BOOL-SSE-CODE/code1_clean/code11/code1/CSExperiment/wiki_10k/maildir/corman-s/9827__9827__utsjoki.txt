Subject: Utsjoki
Image:Utsjoki.vaakuna.svg File:Nuorgam by i november 2012 7599.jpg '''Utsjoki''' is a municipality in Lapland, Finland. Utsjoki is the only municipality in Finland where most people speak Sami language as their first language. As of February 2005, 1,373 people lived in Utsjoki. The municipalities next to it are Inari, Finland in Finland and Karasjok, Tana and Nesseby in Norway. Nuorgam in Utsjoki is the northernmost village in Finland and the European Union.
