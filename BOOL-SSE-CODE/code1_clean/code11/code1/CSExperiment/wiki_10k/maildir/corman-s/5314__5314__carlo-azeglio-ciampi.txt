| birth_place = Livorno, Kingdom of Italy | death_date = | death_place = Rome, Italy | nationality = Italians | profession = Economist Politician | alma_mater = Scuola Normale Superiore di Pisa University of Pisa | residence = Rome, Italy | children = | spouse = Franca Pilla | party = Democratic Party (Italy)(2007-2016) }} '''Carlo Azeglio Ciampi''' (9 December 1920 in Livorno, Italy 16 September 2016 in Rome, Italy) was the 10th President of the Italian Republic. His work started in 1999 and ended in 2006. He was followed by Giorgio Napolitano He is appreciated very much by the Italian people because of his equality and rightness. Since 1979 he had been President of the Bank of Italy, but his task ended in 1993. He was also Prime Minister of the Italian government, but his task lasted just a few months, because it was just a technical government,which purpose was to lead the country in a moment of difficulties.
