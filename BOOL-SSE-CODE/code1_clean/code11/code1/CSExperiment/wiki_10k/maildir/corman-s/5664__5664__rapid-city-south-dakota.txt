Subject: Rapid City, South Dakota
'''Rapid City''' is the second most populous city in the U.S. state of South Dakota. It is in the western part of the state on the eastern edge of the Black Hills. Rapid City is the county seat of Pennington County, South Dakota. It is a good starting point for the area around it, which is a tourist area. Local tourist sites include Mount Rushmore, the Black Hills, the Badlands (South Dakota), and Custer State Park. == Other websites == * [http://www.rcgov.org/ Rapid City government website] Category:County seats in South Dakota Category:Cities in South Dakota
