It is the Capital (city) of the federal States of Germany of Hesse. Wiesbaden is on the right (north) bank of the Rhine (German: "Rhein"), near the city of Mainz (about away from Wiesbaden centre) the opposite side of the river, and a short distance, about , from Frankfurt am Main, to the east. About 274,000 people live in the city (2005). Wiesbaden has an oceanic climate (''Cfb'' in the Koeppen climate classification).
