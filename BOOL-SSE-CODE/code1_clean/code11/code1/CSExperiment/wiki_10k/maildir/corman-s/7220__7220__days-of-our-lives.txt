In November 2015, the show celebrated 50 years on TV. It is currently in its 59th season with 15,175 episodes (as of July 25, 2025). They began filming the show in HD in 2010. ''Days of Our Lives'' takes place in the city of Salem. The main families on the show are the Bradys, the DiMeras and the Hortons. Many good and bad things happened to the Brady family and the Horton family.
