Subject: Mesoamerica
File:Region Mesoamerica.png '''Mesoamerica''' (literally, "central America and some of north america") is a Geography region. It begins from around the Tropic of Cancer in central Mexico and ends near Costa Rica. The term is especially used to mean the native peoples and cultures that were there before the Spain took over that region. The cacao tree is native to this area. Category:Mesoamerica
