Subject: Toleration
'''Tolerance''' is not taking action against people who do things one does not like. The opposite of tolerance is intolerance. Intolerance is often found in dictatorships historically and often causes the hate crime and hate speech. Tolerance is often about religion (religious toleration), sex, or politics opinion, nation, race (sociology), disability, sexual orientation or gender identity.
