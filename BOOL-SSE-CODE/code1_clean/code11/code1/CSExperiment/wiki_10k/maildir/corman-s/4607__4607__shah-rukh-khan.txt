He is also known as the ''"Badshah of Bollywood",'' "King of Bollywood" and "King Khan". He was born in New Delhi; his grandparents moved there from Peshawar. His parents used to speak Hindko language, a widely spoken language in Peshawar. He started his career appearing on television in the late 1980s. He is married to Gauri Khan and has three children: Aryan, Suhana and Abram. He also has a sister named Shehnaz Lalarukh Khan. He is one of the biggest stars not just in Bollywood, but also around the world.
