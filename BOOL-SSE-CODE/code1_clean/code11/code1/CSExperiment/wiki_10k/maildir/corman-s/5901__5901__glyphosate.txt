Subject: Glyphosate
File:Glyphosate.svg '''Glyphosate''' is a non-selective herbicide used in agriculture for the control of weeds, shrubs, and grasses. One brand name it is sold as is Monsanto's ''Roundup''. It is also used in genetically modified crops.http://aob.oxfordjournals.org/cgi/content/abstract/mcp152v1 There are many studies indicating glyphosate is dangerous for animals, including humans. == References == == Other websites == * [http://www.pesticideinfo.org/Detail_Chemical.jsp?Rec_Id=PC33138 Fact sheet] * [http://crop.scijournals.org/cgi/reprint/45/1/329 glyphosate resistance] Category:Herbicides
