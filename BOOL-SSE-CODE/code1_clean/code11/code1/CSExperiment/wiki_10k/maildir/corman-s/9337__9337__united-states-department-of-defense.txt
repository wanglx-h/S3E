Subject: United States Department of Defense
File:United States Department of Defense Seal.svg The '''United States Department of Defense''', also known as the '''USDoD''', is a department in the United States government that is in charge of the military of the United States. It was created in 1947 and its headquarters is in The Pentagon in Washington, D.C.. The person in charge of the Department of Defense is called the Secretary of Defense. The current Secretary of Defense is Pete Hegseth. The Secretary of Defense answers directly to the President (United States). The Department of Defense is made up of three Military Departments:the United States Department of the Army (United States Army),the United States Department of the Navy,and the United States Air Force along with the National Security Agency,and the Defense Intelligence Agency (DIA). The Department of the Navy includes the United States Navy and the United States Marine Corps. Also, during times of war the United States Coast Guard is under the authority of the Department of Defense.
