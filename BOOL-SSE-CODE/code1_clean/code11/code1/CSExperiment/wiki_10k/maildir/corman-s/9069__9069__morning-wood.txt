'''Morning wood''' is an informal term for a morning erection, for which the technical medical term is "nocturnal penile tumescence". It means an erection that a man sometimes has while sleep or right after waking up. Men who do not have Erectile dysfunction#Difficulty having an erection can experience this sensation.
