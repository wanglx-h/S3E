File:Defense.gov photo essay 090826-D-8719J-53.jpg]] File:Nepalese Rangers attend US Army Leadership School 150811-F-LX370-120.jpg A '''soldier''' is a person who is a part of an army. A soldier can be Conscription or Volunteering to join. ==Names and types== There are many different types of soldiers: Trooper (rank)s, tankers, commandos, dragoons, infantryman, United States Space Forces, artilleryman, paratroopers, grenadiers, Ranger (disambiguation)#Militarys, snipers, Combat Engineers, sappers, Royal Electrical and Mechanical Engineers, signallers, Combat medics, and Gunner (rank)s. In many countries there are soldiers that have special names for other reasons. For example, military police personnel in the British Army are called "red berets" because their berets are red. ==Related pages== * Military * Militia * marine (military) * Sailor * Military ranks ==References== ==Other websites== * Category:Military occupations
