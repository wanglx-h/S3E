Subject: Glastonbury Abbey
Image:Glastonburyabbey.jpg '''Glastonbury Abbey''' is in Glastonbury, Somerset, England. It was King Ine of Wessex who decided to build Glastonbury Abbey and out of stone. King Edmund I of England was buried at the Abbey in 946 AD. It is said to be the burial place of King Arthur. ==References== Category:Abbeys Category:Churches in England Category:Buildings and structures in Somerset Category:12th-century buildings and structures
