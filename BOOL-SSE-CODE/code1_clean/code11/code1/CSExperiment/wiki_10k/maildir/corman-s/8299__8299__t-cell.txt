Subject: T cell
A '''T cell''' is a type of lymphocyte, a white blood cell. T-cells play an important role in the immune system. They attack infected cells, foreign cells, and cancer cells and completely destroy them. This stops viruses or cancer from spreading to healthy cells. The "T" in T cell stands for thymus. The thymus is where T cells wikt:mature.
