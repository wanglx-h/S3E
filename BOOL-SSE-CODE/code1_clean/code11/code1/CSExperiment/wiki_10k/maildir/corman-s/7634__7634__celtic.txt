Subject: Celtic
The words '''Celt''' and '''Celtic''' can have many meanings. Pronounced /kel-(tik)/: *Ancient Celtic religion, a Paganism religion. *Celt, either ancient or Modern Celts *Celtic languages, descending from the Proto-Celtic language, spoken by these people and their modern descendants *Celtic mythology *Celtici, Celts from ancient Lusitania (which is now Portugal). (See also Celtiberians for a Celtic people on the Iberian peninsula) *Irish music, folk music that started in "Celtic" cultures. *Celtic art, art from the "Celtic" culture *Celtic Christianity *Celtic calendar The following should only be pronounced /selt(ik)/: *Boston Celtics professional basketball team *Celtic F.C. a football team in Glasgow
