The territory is made up of three islands: Grand Cayman, Little Cayman, and Cayman Brac. Its capital (city), George Town, Cayman Islands, is on the main island (Grand Cayman). == Government == The Cayman Islands are governed by the United Kingdom. The Monarchy of the United Kingdom chooses a Governor of the Cayman Islands to represent the territory. The Governor can use his powers freely, but the King still has executive power. == Taxation == There are no Direct tax that residents and companies have to pay. The government receives most income from Indirect tax. However, there are tourist taxes and airport taxes. == Climate == The Cayman Islands have a Tropical climate. The wet season runs from May to October, and the dry season runs from November to April. == Tourism == The Cayman Islands are a popular tourist spot because they have Coral reef. This is why many tourists travel on airplanes or on Cruise ship to go snorkeling and scuba diving. == Airports == There are three airports in the Cayman Islands. The main one is :en:Owen Roberts International Airport.
