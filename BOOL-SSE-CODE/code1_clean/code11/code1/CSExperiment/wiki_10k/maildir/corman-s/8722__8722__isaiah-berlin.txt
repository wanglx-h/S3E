He was born in Riga, Latvia, which at the time was a part of the Russian Empire. He was Jewish, and was the first Jew to win the prize of a fellowship at All Souls College, Oxford University. ==Reputation== Isaiah Berlin was an important person of the 20th century. People see him as an important thinker of Liberalism ideas. He was made a member of the Order of Merit and was a professor at the University of Oxford. He made a distinction between positive liberty and negative liberty. His most famous work is probably ''Two Concepts of Liberty'' (where he introduced this distinction.) He died 5 November 1997.
