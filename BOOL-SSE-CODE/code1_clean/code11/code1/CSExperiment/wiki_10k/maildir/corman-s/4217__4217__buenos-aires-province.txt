}} The '''Buenos Aires Province''' is the largest, richest, and most populated province of Argentina. The province has a population of 13,827,203 (2001) and its capital (city) is La Plata (850,000 inhabitants). == Geography == The Buenos Aires Province has an area of 307,571 square kilometre.
