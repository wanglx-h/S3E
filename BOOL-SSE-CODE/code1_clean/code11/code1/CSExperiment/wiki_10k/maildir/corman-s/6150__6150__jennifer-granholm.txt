| birth_place = Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada | death_date = | death_place = | signature = Jennifer Granholm (signature).svg | party = Democratic Party (United States) | spouse = Daniel Mulhern | children = 3 | education = University of California, Berkeley (Bachelor of Arts)Harvard University (Juris Doctor) }} '''Jennifer Granholm''' (born February 5, 1959) is a Canada born United States politician. Granholm was the 16th United States Secretary of Energy from 2021 to 2025 under the Joe Biden. She was the Governor of Michigan from 2003 to 2011. Granholm was born in Canada and raised in California. She ran for governor of Michigan in 2002, even though she had lived in the state for less than 15 years. On December 15, 2020, President-elect Joe Biden nominated Granholm to be Secretary of Energy.
