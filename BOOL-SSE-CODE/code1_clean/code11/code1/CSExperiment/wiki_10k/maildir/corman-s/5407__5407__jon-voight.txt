Voight came to publicity in the late 1960s with his Academy Award–nominated performance as Joe Buck, a would-be gigolo, in Midnight Cowboy (1969). He has received an Academy Award for Best Actor, having received it for his role in the 1978 film Coming Home (1978), in which he played a Paraplegia Vietnam War veteran. In the 1970s, he became renowned as a Hollywood. Voight is a Republican Party (United States) who supports anti-communism. ==Early life and family== His mother was of Germany ancestry; his father was of Slovaks ancestry. Voight is the middle of three brothers: volcanologist Barry Voight was born in 1937; musician Chip Taylor was born in 1940.
