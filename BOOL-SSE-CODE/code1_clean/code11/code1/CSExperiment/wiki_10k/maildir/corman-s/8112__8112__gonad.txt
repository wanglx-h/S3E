Subject: Gonad
A '''gonad''' is a gland that makes Gamete and Sex hormone. The male gonad is the testicle. Testicles make Sperm. The female gonad is the ovary. Ovaries make Ovum. Some animals have different gonads because they are Hermaphrodite. They may have gonads such as the ovotestis. This is a gland that is similar to testicles and ovaries. == References == Category:Glands
