Subject: Bu Bing
King '''Bu Bing''' of Shang Dynasty of China, in chinese language:"卜丙", born '''Zi Sheng''', in Chinese:"子勝",. He was the forth king of the Shang Dynasty (1600 BC - 1046 BC). He was the second son of King Tang of Shang of China. In Records of the Grand Person, he was recorded as the second king of the Shang Dynasty. In that book he is given the name "Wai Bing", in Chinese:"外丙". But in the oracle script, he was recorded as the forth king of the Shang Dynasty. In these records his name is "Bu Bing",in Chinese:"卜丙". Category:Kings of Shang Dynasty
