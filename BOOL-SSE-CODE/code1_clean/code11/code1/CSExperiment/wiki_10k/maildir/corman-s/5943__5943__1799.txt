'''1799''' (Roman numerals) was a common year Common year starting on Tuesday in the Gregorian calendar and a common year starting on Saturday in the Julian calendar. == Events == * March 1 – United States Federalist Party James Ross becomes President Pro Tempore of the United States Senate. * March 7 – Napoleon I of France captures Jaffa in Palestine (region) and his troops proceed to kill more than 2,000 Albanian captives. * March 29 – New York (state) passes a law aimed at gradually abolishing slavery in the state. * July 7 – Ranjit Singh's men had taken their positions outside Lahore. * July 15 – In the Egyptian port city of Rosetta, France Captain Pierre Bouchard finds the Rosetta Stone. * July 25 – At Aboukir in Egypt, Napoleon I of France defeats 10,000 Ottoman Empire Mamluk troops under Mustafa Pasha. * November 9 – Napoleon overthrows the French Directory. * December – Napoleon becomes First Consul. * The Dutch East India Company is dissolved. * The Place Royale in Paris is renamed Place des Vosges when the Department of Vosges becomes the first to pay new Revolutionary taxes. * The American System of manufacturing is invented. Deaths December 14 - George Washington, 67, first President of the United States, throat infection (b. 1732) *April 17 - Edward Williams Clay, American artist (d. 1857) * August 29 – Pope Pius VI, Roman Catholic pope during the French revolution.
