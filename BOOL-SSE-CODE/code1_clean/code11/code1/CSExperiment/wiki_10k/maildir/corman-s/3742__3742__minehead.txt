It is in the borough of Somerset West and Taunton. In 2011, the town had a population of 11,981. It is close to the border with Devon. The town is 21 miles (34 km) north-west of Taunton, Somerset's county town. It is close to the Exmoor National Park. Minehead is famous for being the location of Butlin's Minehead, a popular holiday camp owned by Butlin's. The town is the starting point of the South West Coast Path, the longest countryside walking trail in the United Kingdom. ==Twinning== Minehead has one twin town.
