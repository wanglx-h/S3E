| years_active = – | label = }} | associated_acts = | website = | past_members = * John Berry (musician) * Mike D * Kate Schellenbach * Adam Yauch * Ad-Rock }} '''Beastie Boys''' were an United States Hip hop group. According to an interview with Charlie Rose, the word "Beastie" is the backronym of ''Boys Entering Anarchic States Towards Internal Excellence''. They formed in 1981 as a punk rock band. Their most famous albums are probably ''Licensed To Ill'' (1986) and '':en:Paul's Boutique'' (1989). ''Licensed To Ill'' is their first album, and it is an album that is given credit for starting the type of music "rap rock", which is rapping done over rock guitar or beats. ''Paul's Boutique'' is an album famous for its use of samples, or snippets of music, and the fact that the samples were put together in creative, interesting ways. Adam Yauch (1964-2012) was one of the founding members of the band until his death on May 4, 2012 after almost 3-years with cancer of the parotid salivary gland.
