Subject: County seat
A '''county seat''' is the location of a County (United States) government, and is usually the largest, oldest, or most central town or city within a county. In the United Kingdom and Ireland, the name county town is used rather than county seat. In the United States state of Louisiana, counties are called parishes and have '''parish seats''' rather than county seats; while in Alaska, counties are called Borough and have '''borough seats'''. ==Related pages== *County town Category:Local government Category:County seats in the United States
