Subject: List of Atlantic hurricane seasons
The following is a '''list of Atlantic hurricane seasons'''. All began on June 1 in their respective years. They ended on November 30. These dates are when tropical cyclones usually form in the Atlantic Ocean. The '''Atlantic hurricane season''' is the time of year that Tropical cyclone form in the northern Atlantic Ocean. The season starts on June 1 and ends November 30. There is an average of 10 Tropical cyclone#Classifications, six hurricanes, and two or three major hurricanes in the Atlantic. The National Hurricane Center helps watch for hurricanes in the northern Atlantic and makes Weather forecast about were the storms will go. == Before 1800s == * Pre-1600 Atlantic hurricane seasons *
