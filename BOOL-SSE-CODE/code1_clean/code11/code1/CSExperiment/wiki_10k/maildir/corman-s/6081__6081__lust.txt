Subject: Lust
Image:Hieronymus Bosch- The Seven Deadly Sins and the Four Last Things - Lust.JPG. He called it ''Lust''.]] '''Lust''' is a term for a strong desire or wanting, or maybe even needing, often for sexual intercourse, although it is also commonly used to talk of a ''lust for life'' or a ''lust for power'' or for other goals. As a sexual desire, it is considered a sin (a failing) by Christianity, which lists it as one of the seven deadly sins. "Lust" was originally an Anglo-Saxon word which meant "want" or "appetite".
