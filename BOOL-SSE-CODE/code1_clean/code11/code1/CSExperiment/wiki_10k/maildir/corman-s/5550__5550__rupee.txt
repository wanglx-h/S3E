Subject: Rupee
The '''rupee (रुपया)''' is the name of a Denomination (currency) of money in various countries. It is used in the Republic of India, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, Nepal, Mauritius, and the Seychelles, Indonesia, and the Maldives. It was also used in British India, the former Bangladesh until 1971. In past times, rupees were also the name of the money used in Burma and in the Germany and British colonies in East Africa.
