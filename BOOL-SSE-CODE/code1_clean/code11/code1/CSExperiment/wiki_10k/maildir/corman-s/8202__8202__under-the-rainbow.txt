'''''Under the Rainbow''''' is a 1981 comedy movie starring Chevy Chase, Carrie Fisher, Eve Arden, and Billy Barty. The plot is loosely based on the gathering of dwarfism in a Hollywood hotel, to audition for roles as "munchkins" in the movie ''The Wizard of Oz (1939)''. The movie also has nobility, assassinations, spying, and tourism.
