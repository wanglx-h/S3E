The '''1060s''' was a decade that began on 1 January 1060 and ended on 31 December 1069. It is distinct from the decade known as the '''''107th decade''' which'' began on January 1, 1061 and ended on December 31, 1070. == Events == * 1061 Normans conquer Messina in Sicily * 1062 Marrakech starts. * 1066 Normans conquer England * 1066 William I of England became King of England on Christmas Day in Westminster Abbey. * 1068 Japan changes emperors. == Important people == * William I of England * Harold Godwinson * Harald Hardrada Category:1060s
