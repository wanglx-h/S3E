File:Melissa-joan-hart-portrait.jpg '''Melissa Joan Catherine Hart''' (born April 18, 1976) is an Americans actor. She was born and raised in Long Island, New York (state). She played the title roles in the television programme ''Clarissa Explains It All'' and ''Sabrina, the Teenage Witch (TV series)''. In 1999 she played the lead role in the movie ''Drive Me Crazy''. From 2010 to 2015, she starred as Mel Burke in the ABC Family show ''Melissa & Joey''. == Personal life == Since July 19, 2003, Hart has been married to musician Mark Wilkerson (born 1976).
