Subject: Theia
'''''Theia''''' is a Titan (mythology) in Greek mythology. She is the titan of sight and of the light-filled sky.Her parents are Gaia (mythology) and Uranos. With her brother Hyperion (Titan) her children are Helios, Selene, and Eos. She was also considered the moons eyes because Selene was her daughter, and Selene was the titan of the moon.
