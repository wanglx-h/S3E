Subject: Sunrise (company)
Image:Sunrise-Logo.svg '''Sunrise''' is a Japanese animation company. The staff uses the name '''Hajime Yatate''' in all their productions by Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer, but this is not a real person--it is a name that all the people who work for the studio use for themselves.
