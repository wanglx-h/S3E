== African diasporic religions == These religions are those of African cultures who have settled outside of Africa (diaspora). They are also sometimes called Creole language religions. They include a number of related religions that developed in the Americas among African slave trade and their descendants in various countries of the Caribbean Islands and Latin America, as well as parts of the southern United States.
