== Events == * March/April – Pope John XV dies before being able to crown Otto III, Holy Roman Emperor, List of German Kings and Emperors as Holy Roman Emperor. Otto resides in Pavia while waiting for the election of the next Pope. * May 3 – Twenty-four year old Bruno of Carinthia (duchy), grandson of Otto I, Holy Roman Emperor and first cousin of Otto III, is elected Pope Gregory V. He is the first Holy Roman Empire Pope. * May 21 – Sixteen year old Otto III, Holy Roman Emperor is crowned Holy Roman Emperor by his cousin Pope Gregory V. * October 24 – Hugh Capet of France, King of France dies and is succeeded by his son Robert II of France. * Abu Mansoor Nizar al-Aziz Billah, fifth Fatimid Caliphate in History of Egypt dies and is succeeded by his son Al-Hakim bi-Amr Allah. * November 1 – Emperor Otto III, Holy Roman Emperor issues a deed to Gottschalk, Bishop of Freising, which is the oldest known document using the name ''Ostarrîchi'' (Austria in Old High German). * Niujie Mosque constructed in Beijing Category:996
