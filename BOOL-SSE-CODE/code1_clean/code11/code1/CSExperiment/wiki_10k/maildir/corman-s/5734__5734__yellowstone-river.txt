Subject: Yellowstone River
Image:Yellowstone River in Hayden Valley.jpg The '''Yellowstone River''' is a river in the northwestern part of the United States. It is a tributary of the Missouri River and flows through the states of Wyoming, Montana, and North Dakota. It also flows through Yellowstone National Park and feeds Yellowstone Lake.
