Subject: Buffalo, New York
Image:Flag of Buffalo, New York.svg Image:Seal of Buffalo, New York.svg '''Buffalo''' is a city in the U.S. state of New York (state). Buffalo is the second-biggest city in New York State. There are about 278,000 people in the city of Buffalo. There are more than a million people in the surrounding area. Buffalo is on the east end of Lake Erie near the Niagara River. It is close to Niagara Falls, and to Canada. Buffalo was a small village when the Erie Canal connected it to the Hudson River and New York City. It became a city. ==Sports== Buffalo has an National Football League team called the Buffalo Bills (who play in the suburb of Orchard Park, NY) and an National Hockey League team called the Buffalo Sabres. There is also a Minor League Baseball team called the Buffalo Bisons (baseball).
