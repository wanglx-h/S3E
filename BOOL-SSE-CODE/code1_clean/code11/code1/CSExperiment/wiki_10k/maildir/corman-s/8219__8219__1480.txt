== Events == * March 6 – Treaty of Toledo – Ferdinand II of Aragon and Isabella of Castile of Spain recognize African conquests of Afonso of Portugal and he cedes the Canary Islands to Spain * Great standing on the Ugra river – Muscovy becomes independent from the Golden Horde and Moscow becomes the capital of Russia. * Ludovico Sforza becomes Regent of Milan (reigns until 1499) * Concorts and co-rulers Ferdinand II of Aragon and Isabella of Castile initiate the Spanish Inquisition (looking for heretics in converted Jews) * Leonardo da Vinci invents the parachute * Mohamed II fails in his attempt to capture Rhodes from the Knights of Rhodes * Magdalen College School, Oxford starts. * The Inca Empire conquers northwest Argentina. == Births == * Ferdinand Magellan, Portugal explorer who was the first to sail around the world. * Pier Gerlofs Donia, a huge pirate who fought for the independence of Friesland. * Pir Sultan Abdal, Turkey poet. * Peter Henlein, Germany inventor who may have first invented the watch. == Deaths == * Antonio Squarcialupi, Italian composer Category:1480
