'''William Edgar (Bill) Oddie''' Order of the British Empire (born 7 July 1941) is a British people comedian, actor, writer and television personality. Oddie was born in Rochdale, Lancashire and grew up in Birmingham. He read English Literature at Pembroke College, University of Cambridge where he gained an Master of Arts. He is most famous for his birdwatching and conservation biology work. In the 1970s, he was a member of the BBC One television comedy series, The Goodies. Medical doctors found that his mother, Lilian, had schizophrenia. Oddie says that he does not remember her living at home because she spent a lot of her life in mental hospitals.
