Subject: Trout
Image:Trout.jpg]] Image:Biwa Trout(Lake Biwa-JP).jpg A '''trout''' is a kind of teleost fish that belongs to the Salmoninae subfamily of the Salmonidae family. There are many different kinds of trout. They belong to the same family as the salmon. Most trout are freshwater fish. They live in clear streams, rivers and lakes in The Americas, Europe, and Asia. It is possible to work out the difference between a trout and any other fish, by looking for a second dorsal fin (or adipose fin). Also, you can find for spots in mature trout and parr marks in the young. Category:Salmoniformes Category:Edible fish
