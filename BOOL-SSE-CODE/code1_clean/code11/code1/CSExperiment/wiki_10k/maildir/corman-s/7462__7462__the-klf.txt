Subject: The KLF
'''The KLF''' were a band from England. They made music from the late 1980s until the early 1990s. A lot of the music they made was pop music or dance music. The band members were two men, Bill Drummond and Jimmy Cauty. Bill Drummond is a musician, singer, and writer from Scotland. Jimmy Cauty is a musician and artist from England. Sometimes they used the names '''The Justified Ancients of Mu Mu''', '''The JAMs''' and '''The Timelords''' as their band name, instead. The KLF adopted the philosophy based on the series of the Esotericism novels ''The Illuminatus! Trilogy''.
