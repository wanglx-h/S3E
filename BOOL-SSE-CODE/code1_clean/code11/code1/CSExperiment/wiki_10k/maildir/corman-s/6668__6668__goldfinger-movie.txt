Subject: Goldfinger (movie)
'''''Goldfinger''''' is a 1964 spy movie based on the seventh James Bond book writer by Ian Fleming. ''Goldfinger'' is the third James Bond movie in the series. It is also third to star Sean Connery as the United Kingdom Secret Service Espionage James Bond. Shirley Bassey sings the title song of the movie. ==Plot== In this movie, the main bad guy is named Auric Goldfinger, and he likes gold a lot. In the movie, James Bond tries to find out what Goldfinger's bad plan is, and in the end James Bond wins and stops Goldfinger. His plan is to blow up Fort Knox so that the price of gold will go up. Goldfinger has a sidekick called Oddjob who throws his metal hat at people.
