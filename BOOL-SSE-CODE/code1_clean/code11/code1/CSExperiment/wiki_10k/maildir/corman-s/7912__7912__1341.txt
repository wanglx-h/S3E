== Events == * The Queen's College, Oxford, a constituent college of the University of Oxford, is founded. * Petrarch is crowned poet laureate in Rome, the first man since antiquity to be given this honor. * Beginning of the Breton War of Succession over the control of the Duchy of Brittany * Margarete Maultasch, Countess of Tyrol, expels her husband John Henry of Bohemia, to whom she had been married as a child. She subsequently marries Louis V the Brandenburger without having been divorced, which results in the excommunication of the couple. Category:1341
