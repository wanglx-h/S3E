Sun Life Stadium (2010-2016) **a.k.a. Hard Rock Stadium (2016-Present) }} The '''Miami Dolphins''' are an American football team in the National Football League based in Miami, Florida. The Dolphins began playing in 1966 and in 1972 they had the only perfect season in NFL history and won a Super Bowl, with Bob Griese leading the team as quarterback. The other Super Bowl win came a year later, in 1973. Another quarterback, Dan Marino, is a more recent famous player from this club.
