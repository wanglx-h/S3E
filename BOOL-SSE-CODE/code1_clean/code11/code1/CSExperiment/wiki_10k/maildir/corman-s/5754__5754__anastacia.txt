Anastacia's father was of Germany ancestry; her mother is of Irish people ancestry. Anastacia has had several health problems in her life. She was diagnosed with Crohn's disease when she was 13. In early 2003 she was diagnosis with breast cancer and had surgery to remove it. She was again diagnosed with breast cancer in early 2013.
