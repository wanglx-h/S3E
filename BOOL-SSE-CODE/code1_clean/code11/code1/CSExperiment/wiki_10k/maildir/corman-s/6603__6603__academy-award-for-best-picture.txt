Subject: Academy Award for Best Picture
The '''Academy Award for Best Picture''' is one of the Academy Awards. Also called '''Oscars''', the Academy Awards are given to people working in the movie industry by the American Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences (AMPAS). The name of the award has changed over time. It was first '''Outstanding Picture''' in 1927. In 1930, the name was changed to '''Best Picture'''. It is still called that today. In the list below are the winners of the award for each year.
