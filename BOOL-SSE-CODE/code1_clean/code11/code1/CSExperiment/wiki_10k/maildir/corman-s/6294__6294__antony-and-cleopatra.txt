Subject: Antony and Cleopatra
'''''Antony and Cleopatra''''' is a Play (theatre) by Shakespeare. It is a tragedy. Shakespeare's source was Plutarch's ''Lives''. The play was first performed between 1606 and 1607. It was probably first printed in the First Folio of 1623. The play describes the romantic love and suicides of Mark Antony and Cleopatra. ==References== * [http://www.william-shakespeare.info/shakespeare-play-antony-and-cleopatra.htm Antony and Cleopatra] Category:Plays by William Shakespeare Category:1600s plays
