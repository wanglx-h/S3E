Subject: Estádio do Morumbi
File:sao paulo e juventude - campeonato brasileiro de 2006 - 01.jpg.]] '''Morumbi Stadium''' or '''Estádio do Morumbi''' is the biggest stadium in São Paulo, Brazil. It used to have 120,000 seats available, but now its maximum capacity is 77,000 seats for safety reasons. It is the home ground of São Paulo Futebol Clube and its formal name honors Cícero Pompeu de Toledo, who was São Paulo FC's chairman during most of the stadium's construction.
