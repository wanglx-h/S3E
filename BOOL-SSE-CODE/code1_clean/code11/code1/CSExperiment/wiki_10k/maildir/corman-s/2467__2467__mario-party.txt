Subject: Mario Party
'''''Mario Party''''' is a series of video games made by Nintendo. The idea of each game is that Mario and his friends must travel across a game board and earn stars and coins. Each new game in the series contains new characters, playing boards, and minigame. Every few turns, each character must participate in a mini-game.
