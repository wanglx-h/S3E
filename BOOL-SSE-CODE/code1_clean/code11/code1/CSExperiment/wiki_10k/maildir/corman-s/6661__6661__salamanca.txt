|elevation_m = 802 |postal_code = 37001-37010 |area_code = 34 (Spain) + 923 (Salamanca) |website = [http://www.salamanca.es/ www.salamanca.es] }} '''Salamanca''' is a city in central Spain. It is the capital city of the province with the same name. The city has a very important university started in 1218. It has two wikt:cathedrals and a famous Plaza Mayor (Main Square) built in the 18th century. Frogs are a mascot of Salamanca. Souvenirs of frogs can be found in almost every shop in the Plaza Mayor.
