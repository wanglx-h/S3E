It is in north-central Ukraine on the Dnieper River. Over three million people live there. It is not a part of any Oblasts of Ukraine. However, it is surrounded by Kyiv Oblast, and is the headquarters of Kyiv Oblast. The city hosted the UEFA Euro 2012 final. Kyiv also hosted the Eurovision Song Contest 2017.
