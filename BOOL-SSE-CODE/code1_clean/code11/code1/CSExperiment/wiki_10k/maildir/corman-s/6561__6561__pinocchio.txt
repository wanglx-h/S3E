Subject: Pinocchio
Image:Pinocchio.jpg '''Pinocchio''' is a fictional character from the 1883 book ''The Adventures of Pinocchio'' by Carlo Collodi. Pinocchio is a puppet, made by wood carver, Geppetto. When he tells lies, his nose gets longer. Pinocchio learns about being a real life boy and wants to be a real human being instead of a puppet made of wood and strings. The most well-known and beloved version of the story is the 1940 animation Walt Disney Pictures movie ''Pinocchio (1940 movie)''. The other two most famous and beloved versions and adaptations of the story are from the Guillermo del Toro's Pinocchio and the Un burattino di nome Pinocchio. ==Other websites== Category:Characters in written fiction
