'''FC United''' are an England football (soccer) team. They are from Manchester and were formed in 2005 by wikt:fans when Malcolm Glazer bought Manchester United. FC United of Manchester play their home matches at Broadhurst Park, Moston, Manchester. They play in the National League North.
