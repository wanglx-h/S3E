Subject: Maya Lin
Image:Maya Lin, 2014 (cropped).jpg '''Maya Ying Lin''' (born October 10, 1959) is a China-United States architect and artist. She is most famous for her Vietnam Veterans Memorial in Washington, DC and the Civil Rights Monument in Montgomery, Alabama. In 2016, Lin was awarded the Presidential Medal of Freedom by Barack Obama. ==References== Category:American architects Category:1959 births Category:Living people
