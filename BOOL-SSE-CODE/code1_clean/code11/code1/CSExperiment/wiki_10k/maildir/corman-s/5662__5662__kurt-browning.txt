File:Kurt-Browning.jpg '''Kurt Browning''' (born June 18, 1966 in Rocky Mountain House, Alberta) is a Canadians figure skater. He won four world championships and four Canadian championships. Browning did the first ever quadruple jump (four turns) in competition. He is well known for his footwork. As a professional, he has skated for Stars on Ice and Champions on Ice, won several professional championships, and did some T.V. skating specials. He has also talked during skating events on T.V. He has helped the Charitable organization Muscular Dystrophy. Browning was raised in Caroline, Alberta. He married a dancer with the National Ballet of Canada, Sonia Rodriguez, in 1996.
