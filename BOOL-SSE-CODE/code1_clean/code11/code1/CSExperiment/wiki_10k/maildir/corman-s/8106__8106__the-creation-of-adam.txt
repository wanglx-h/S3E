Subject: The Creation of Adam
File:God2-Sistine Chapel.png '''''The Creation of Adam''''' is part of a painting by Michelangelo which can be seen on the ceiling of the Sistine Chapel in the Vatican City. It is of Adam and God touching hands so that God can give the spark of life to Adam.
