}} '''Newcastle upon Tyne''' is a city in north-east England. In 2021, 286,445 people lived in the city. Newcastle upon Tyne is called '''Newcastle''' for short. It is famous for its big bridge called the Tyne Bridge. The local dialect and accent is called the Geordie. People from Newcastle are also called Geordies. == References == Category:Newcastle upon Tyne
