Subject: Wausau, Wisconsin
Image:WausauWisconsinCityHall.jpg '''Wausau''' is a city in Marathon County, Wisconsin in the U.S. state of Wisconsin. It is the largest city in the County#United_States, and it is the county seat (where the government of the county works). According to the 2020 census (official count), 39,994 people lived in the city.
