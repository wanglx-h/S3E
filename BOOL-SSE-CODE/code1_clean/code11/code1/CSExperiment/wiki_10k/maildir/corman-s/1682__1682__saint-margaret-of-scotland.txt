Subject: Saint Margaret of Scotland
'''Saint Margaret of Scotland''' (c. 1045 - 16 November 1093) was a Scottish queen and English princess. She was married to Malcolm III of Scotland. She was the mother of David I of Scotland, Alexander I of Scotland and Edgar, King of Scotland. She was the daughter of Edward the Exile, and the granddaughter of Edmund Ironside. She was probably born in Hungary.
