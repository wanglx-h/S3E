Subject: Alyson Hannigan
File:Alyson Hannigan.jpg '''Alyson Lee Hannigan''' is an Americans actress. She was born March 24, 1974 in Washington, D.C. She moved to Atlanta, Georgia (U.S. state) when she was 2 years old to live with her mother. She started to act in advertisement for McDonalds, Six Flags theme park and Oreo cookies when she was small. When she was 11, she went to Los Angeles, California with her mother to go to school and become celebrity. She started getting noticed with her roles in ''Buffy the Vampire Slayer'' as Willow Rosenberg and in the ''American Pie (movie)'' movies as Michelle Flaherty.
