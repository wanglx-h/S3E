NBC started airing Its first television programs in 1944. NBC is owned by Comcast, and was previously owned by General Electric and Universal Studios. NBC owns the cable news channels CNBC and MSNBC. It is nicknamed "The Peafowl Network" because of the animal in its logo. File:NBC logo.svg The network is famous for its three-Note (music) chimes, and NBC Sunday Night Football, which is the most watched TV series after American Idol.
