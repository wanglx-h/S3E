Subject: Mersey Beat
'''The Mersey Beat''' or '''Mersey Sound''' or '''Liverpool Sound''' is the name for a type of music that came out in the city of Liverpool, England in the early 1960s. Maybe the first group to be thought of as a part of genre were Kingsize Taylor and the Dominoes. The most famous group of this genre were The Beatles.
