Subject: Button
A '''button''' can have three meanings. Image:Clothes button.jpg *A button is a fastener that keeps your shirt or pants closed. *A Push-button is something that sticks up from a flat surface, and when you push it, it makes something happen. *A button is a picture on a computer monitor that is ''pressed'' with a computer mouse to make the computer do something.
