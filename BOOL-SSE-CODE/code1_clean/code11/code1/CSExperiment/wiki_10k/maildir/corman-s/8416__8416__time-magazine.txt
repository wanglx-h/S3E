Subject: Time (magazine)
File:Time Magazine logo.svg '''''Time''''' is an United States news magazine. It was founded in 1923. It is printed every week in the United States. It is published by Time Inc. It has articles on politics and current events. It is read by more people than any other news magazine. There is also a magazine called ''Time For Kids'', aimed at children. That magazine is eight pages long and contains more images and shorter articles than the regular ''Time'' magazine.
