Subject: Ballerina
File:IrinaBaronova.jpg]] A '''ballerina''' is a name given to a female ballet dancer. Only very good female ballet dancers are called ballerinas. A ballerina is a principal dancer in a classical ballet company. The highest ranking female dancer in a company was usually called the '''prima ballerina'''. Today the term 'ballerina' has gone rather out of fashion. Now they are simply called 'Principal dancers'.Crane, Debra and Mackrell, Judith 2000. ''The Oxford dictionary of dance''. Oxford University Press. p40 Dancers in a company are ranked by the company, and paid accordingly. They have to prove themselves in lesser parts before they can get promoted.
