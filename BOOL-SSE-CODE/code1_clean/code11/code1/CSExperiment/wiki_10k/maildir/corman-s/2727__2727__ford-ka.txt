File:Ford Ka Kool of Oetz.jpg File:Ford_ka_blueprint.jpg File:Ford Ka Sudamerica.jpg File:Ford Ka 2009 Front.jpg The '''Ford Ka''' is a car made by the Ford Motor Company in Spain, Poland and Brazil. It has three doors and was first made in 1996. The Ka is very popular in Europe, about 1.5 million cars have been sold.
