He has appeared in many movies and television programme since the 1970s. In 1988, He was involved in a motorcycle accident, but he recovered. He was born in Baytown, Texas, Texas and grew up in Oklahoma. He was a heavy cocaine user for many years, before giving it up and becoming a born-again Christian. His elder son Jake Busey (born 1971) is also an actor. Busey was a contestant in two celebrity series of ''The Apprentice'' (2011 and 2013).
