Subject: Dining room
Image:table and chairs.jpg A '''dining room''' is a room used for eating and is usually near the kitchen. This is to make serving food easier. Usually a dining room will contain a Table (furniture) with a set of chairs, normally positioned at the sides and end of a table. People often only use their dining rooms now for formal occasions and eating in the kitchen has become more popular. Category:Rooms
