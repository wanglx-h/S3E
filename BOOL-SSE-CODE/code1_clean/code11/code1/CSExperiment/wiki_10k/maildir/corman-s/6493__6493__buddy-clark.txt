Subject: Buddy Clark
'''Buddy Clark''' (born '''Samuel Goldberg''', July 26, 1912 October 1, 1949) was an American popular singer of the Big band era. He had success in the 1930s and 1940s, returning to the United States after World War II. He died in a plane crash in 1949.
