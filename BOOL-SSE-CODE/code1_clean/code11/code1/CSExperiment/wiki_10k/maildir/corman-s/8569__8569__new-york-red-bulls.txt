The '''New York Red Bulls''' are an United States football (soccer) team. They play in Major League Soccer in Harrison, New Jersey. Until 2006, they were known as the '''MetroStars'''. Their home ground is Sports Illustrated Stadium in Harrison. ==History== The club was founded in 1995 by John Werner Kluge and Stuart Subotnick, two managers for MetroMedia, under the name MetroStars. The club was a founding member of Major League Soccer. In 2006 the team was sold to the Austrian company Red Bull and renamed the New York Red Bulls. The team is the only one in the MLS which never reached a major title.
