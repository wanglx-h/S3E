Subject: Carl Radle
'''Carl Dean Radle''' (born June 18, 1942 Tulsa, Oklahoma, died May 30, 1980) was an Americans musician, best known for being the bassist in Derek and the Dominos. Radle worked with many well-known artists and worked on many gold and platinum singles and albums. Radle is thought by many to have been the best bass guitar sideman in the music business. On May 30, 1980, Radle died at age 37, of problems from a kidney infection caused by his alcoholism and drug addiction.
