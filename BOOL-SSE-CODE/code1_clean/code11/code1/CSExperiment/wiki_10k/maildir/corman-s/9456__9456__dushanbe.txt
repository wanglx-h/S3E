Subject: Dushanbe
File:A view of Dushanbe, Tajikistan.jpg Image:Coat of Arms of Dushanbe.png '''Dushanbe''' (, ) is the capital city of Tajikistan. About 562,000 people live in Dushanbe as of 2000. The name is borrowed from the Persian language word for "Monday" (du "two" + shamba or shanbe "day", lit. "day two") and refers to the fact that it was a popular "Monday" market place.
