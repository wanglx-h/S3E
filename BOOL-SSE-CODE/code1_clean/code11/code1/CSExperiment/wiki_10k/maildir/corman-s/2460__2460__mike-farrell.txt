Subject: Mike Farrell
File:Mike Farrell 2016.jpg '''Mike Farrell''' (born February 6, 1939) is an Americans actor, writer and producer. He played BJ Hunnicutt on the television series ''M*A*S*H''. He played Michael "Mike" Hawks in the television movie ''Battered'' in 1978. He played Chicago real estate developer Lee Miglin in the limited crime series ''The Assassination of Gianni Versace: American Crime Story''. Farrell was born in St. Paul, Minnesota. He was raised in Hollywood, California. He was part of the United States Marine Corps. Farrell was married to Judy Farrell from 1963 until they divorced in 1983. Then, he married Shelley Fabares in 1984. He has two children with Hayden. Farrell is also an activist for various causes, including animal rights and world peace.
