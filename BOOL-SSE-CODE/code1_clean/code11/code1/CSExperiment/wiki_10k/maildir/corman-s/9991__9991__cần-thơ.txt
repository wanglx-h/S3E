Subject: Cần Thơ
'''Can Tho''' is a city in the south of Vietnam. Can Tho had a population of about 1.57 million in 2019. It is the biggest city in the Mekong river delta. It is about 160 km away from Ho Chi Minh City and the capitol of the district of Can Tho, until 1975 named Hau Giang.
