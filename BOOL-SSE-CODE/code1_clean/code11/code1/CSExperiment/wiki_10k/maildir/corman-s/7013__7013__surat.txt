File:Sun city surat.jpg File:Bharthana Althan area.jpg '''Surat''' is a city in the States and territories of India of Gujarat, India. It is on the banks of the Tapti river. Surat used to be a large seaport. It is the eighth largest city in India. According to the 2020 reports on the ‘cleanest cities of India’, Surat ranked second out of 4,242 cities in India. ==References== ==Other websites== Category:Cities in Gujarat
