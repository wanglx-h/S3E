Subject: Sedan (automobile)
File:09 Toyota Corolla LE.jpg is a sedan]] A '''sedan''', or '''saloon''', is an automobile that has a low wikt:trunk, a roof and one or two doors on each side. The trunk is reachable only from outside. The word sedan is used in American English, but in British English such an automobile is called a saloon. Category:Sedans Category:Automobile body styles
