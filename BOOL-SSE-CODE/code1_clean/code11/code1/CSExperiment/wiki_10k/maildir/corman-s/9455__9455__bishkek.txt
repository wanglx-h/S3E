Subject: Bishkek
File:Bischkek.jpg File:42 kg chui alaarcha glacier3.jpg Image:Flag of Bishkek.svg Image:Coat of arms of Bishkek Kyrgyzstan.svg '''Bishkek''' (Бишкек) is the capital city of Kyrgyzstan. It has a population of 900,000 (2005). It was originally founded in 1878 as the Russian fortress of '''Pishpek''' (Пишпек). From 1926 until 1991 it was known as '''Frunze''' (Фрунзе), after the Bolshevik military leader Mikhail Frunze. In the Kyrgyz language, a Bishkek is a churn used to make stirred horse milk (kumis), the Kyrgyz national drink. Category:Bishkek
