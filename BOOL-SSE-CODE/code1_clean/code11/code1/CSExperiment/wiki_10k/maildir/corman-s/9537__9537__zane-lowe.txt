File:Zane Lowe 1 Big Weekend.jpg '''Alexander Zane Reid Lowe''' (born 7 August 1973) is a New Zealand radio disc jockey in the United Kingdom. He presents an early evening show on the Great Britain music radio station BBC Radio 1 on Mondays to Thursdays.
