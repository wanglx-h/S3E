File:A map of Canaan (8343807206).jpg of Canaan made in 1692 Anno Domini.]]'''Canaan''' was an Ancient history region in the Levant. In the Bible, it was the land promised to Abraham and his children by God. ==History== The Canaanites lived in the area from about 2,000 Anno Domini until the time of the Bible.
