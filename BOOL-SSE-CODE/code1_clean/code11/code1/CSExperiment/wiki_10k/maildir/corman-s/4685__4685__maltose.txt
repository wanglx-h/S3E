Subject: Maltose
File:Maltose_cyclic_horizontal.png for maltose]] '''Maltose''' (known as malt sugar) is a disaccharide (sometimes called di-glucose). It is formed from two glucose molecules joined at carbons 1 and 4. It is found in germination seeds such as barley as they break down their starch stores to use for food.
