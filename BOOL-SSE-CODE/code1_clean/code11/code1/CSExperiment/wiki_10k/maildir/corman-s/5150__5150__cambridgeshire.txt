Subject: Cambridgeshire
File:Cambridgeshire_Flag.svg of Cambridgeshire]] File:Arms of Cambridgeshire County Council.svg of Cambridgeshire]] File:EnglandCambridgeshire.png '''Cambridgeshire''' is a county in England. It is located in the East of England part of the country. The counties around Cambridgeshire are Lincolnshire to the north, Norfolk to the northeast, Suffolk to the east, Essex and Hertfordshire to the south, and Bedfordshire and Northamptonshire to the west. The county town is Cambridge. Category:Ceremonial counties of England Category:Cambridgeshire
