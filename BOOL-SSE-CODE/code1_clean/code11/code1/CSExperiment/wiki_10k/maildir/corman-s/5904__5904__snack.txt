It can be any kind of food that you do not eat in large amount. People eat snacks if they are hungry between meals. For example, eating potato chips after lunch but before dinner is eating a snack. Other foods that can be eaten as snacks are fruit, candy, muffins, ice cream, and popcorn. Snacking has surged during the Coronavirus disease 2019 pandemic in the U.S. Now only a few people do not snack.
