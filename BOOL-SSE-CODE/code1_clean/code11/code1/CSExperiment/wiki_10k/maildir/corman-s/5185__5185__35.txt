'''35''' is a year in the 1st century. It was a common year starting on Saturday of the Julian calendar. At the time, it was known as the '''Year of the Roman consul of Gallus and Nonianus'''. == Events == * Pliny the Elder is brought to Rome before this year * Tiridates III of Parthia becomes king of Parthia == Births == * Ban Zhao, China history (or 45 AD) (d. 116) * Quintilian, Roman Empire rhetorician (approximate date) (d. 100 AD) == Deaths == * Epaticcus, prince of the Catuvellauni * Artaxias III, king of Armenia (b. 13 BC) * Mahajabeen II, King of Esbee Category:35
