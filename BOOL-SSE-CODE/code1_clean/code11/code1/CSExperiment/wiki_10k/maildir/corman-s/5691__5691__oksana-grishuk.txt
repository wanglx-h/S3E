File:Grishuk and Platov 1994 Europeans.jpg '''Oksana Vladimirovna Grishuk''' (born March 17, 1972 in Odesa, Ukraine) is a Russians retired ice dancing. She and her partner, Evgeny Platov, won two Olympic Games gold medals and four world championships. Grishuk began training in skating at the age of four. She moved to Moscow in 1986, and studied at the Sport University of Moscow from 1988 to 1992. After they won the gold medal in the 1998 Winter Olympics in Nagano, Nagano, Japan, Platov decided to skate as a professional with a different partner, Maya Usova, so Grishuk began skating professionally alone. ==Amateur competitions== *World Championships: first in 1994, 1995, 1996, and 1997 *Olympic Games: gold medal in 1994 and 1998 *European Championships: first place in 1996, 1997, and 1998 ==References== ==Other websites== * [http://www.pashagrishuk.org The largest fansite about Oksana Grishuk, AKA Pasha] * [http://www.pasha-grishuk.de Oksana Grishuk's official website] * [http://members.tripod.com/~usova-platov1/index.html Platov and Usova site] Category:1972 births Category:Living people Category:Olympic gold medalists for Russia Category:Sportspeople from Odesa Category:Russian figure skaters
