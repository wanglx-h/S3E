Subject: General election
A '''general election''' is an election in which all or most members of a given political body are chosen. The term is usually used to mean the elections held for a nation's primary Legislature. This is different from primary elections, by-elections or local elections. In the United Kingdom when Parliament is wikt:dissolved every seat in the House of Commons of the United Kingdom becomes wikt:vacant and a general election is held. == References == Category:Types of elections Category:General elections
