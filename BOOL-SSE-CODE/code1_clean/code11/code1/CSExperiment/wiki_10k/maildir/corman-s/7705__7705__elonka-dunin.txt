Subject: Elonka Dunin
'''Elonka Dunin''' (; born December 29, 1958) is an Americans video game developer. Her job is to make computer games at Simutronics Corporation, in Missouri. She also knows a lot about famous puzzles, especially a puzzle called Kryptos, which no one knows all the answers to yet.
