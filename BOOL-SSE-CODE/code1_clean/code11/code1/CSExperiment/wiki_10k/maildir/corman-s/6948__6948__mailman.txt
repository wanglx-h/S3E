File:Privet iz Kharkova, Russian Empire Postman.jpg depicting a Russian Empire mailman, with an inscription in Russian "Hello from Kharkov"]] A '''mailman''' or '''mail carrier''' or '''postman''' is a person who brings people their postal mail. Normally he works for the post office of the respective country. Of course, there are also Woman doing this job. They are called '''mailwomen'''. For a layman, this occupation is called "letter carriers".
