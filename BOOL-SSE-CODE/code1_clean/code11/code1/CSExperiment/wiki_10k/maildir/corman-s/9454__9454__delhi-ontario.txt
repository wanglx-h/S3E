Subject: Delhi, Ontario
File:Delhi Community Center ON 01.jpg File:Delhi secondary school ON front.jpg '''Delhi''' is a tobacco town in Norfolk County, Ontario, Canada that has fewer than 5000 people. It has a public library, a convience store, a cemetery, a funeral home, various other stores, and a tobacco museum. The town also has a Delhi District Secondary School that has been threatened with closure numerous times by the Grand Erie District School Board for lack of attendance. Children from this town participate in little league baseball, ice hockey, and competitive swimming. Category:Towns in Ontario
