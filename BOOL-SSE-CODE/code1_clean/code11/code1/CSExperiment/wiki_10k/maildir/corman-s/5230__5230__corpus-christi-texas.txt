Subject: Corpus Christi, Texas
'''Corpus Christi''', Texas is a city in the United States. It is near the coast of the Gulf of Mexico and is one of the fastest growing cities in America. Its economy is based around petroleum refinery and tourism. The city was founded in 1839 as ''Kinney's Ranch'' and has been known as Corpus Christi since 1847.
