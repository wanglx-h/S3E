File:Map of Oklahoma highlighting Panhandle.svg of Oklahoma, with its ''panhandle'' marked in red]] A '''panhandle''' is a word used in geography to mean a narrow strip of land that is surrounded on all sides, except one, by one or more country or regions. The strip of land is connected to the country or region it belongs to on one side only. This looks like the Handle (grip) of a frying pan.
