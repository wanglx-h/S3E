Subject: 1169
== Events == * Nur ad-Din invades Egypt, and his nephew Saladin becomes the sultan over the territory conquered by Nur ad-Din. * Prince Andrey Bogolyubskiy sacks the Kievan Rus' capital Kiev. * Start of the conquest of Ireland. Richard de Clare, 2nd Earl of Pembroke ('Strongbow') makes an alliance with the exiled Irish chief, Diarmait Mac Murchada, to help him recover his kingdom of Leinster. * Eleanor of Aquitaine leaves the English court of Henry II of England to establish her great court in Poitiers where the Courts of Love flourished.
