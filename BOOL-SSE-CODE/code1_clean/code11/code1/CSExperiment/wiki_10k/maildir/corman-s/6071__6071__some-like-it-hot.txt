Subject: Some Like It Hot
'''''Some Like It Hot''''' is a 1959 United States comedy movie starring Marilyn Monroe, Tony Curtis, Pat O'Brien, Jack Lemmon and George Raft, and directed by Billy Wilder. It is seen by some as a remake of the 1951 Germany movie, ''Fanfaren der Liebe''. (Michael Logan wrote the story for the version) The movie is set in 1929. It has been remade for the Theatre twice (in 1972 and 2002).
