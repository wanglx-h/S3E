Subject: Peter Davison
'''Peter Davison''' (born '''Peter Moffett''' 13 April 1951) is an England actor. He is best known for his roles as Tristan on ''All Creatures Great and Small'', and as the Fifth Doctor on ''Doctor Who'' from 1981 to 1984 and 2022.
