Subject: Diesel fuel
File:Dieselrainbow.jpg File:Mercedes-Benz W124 E300 Diesel 1994 (2).jpg '''Diesel oil''' or '''diesel fuel''' is a type of fuel for cars, trucks, trains, etc, using a diesel engine. It is also an oil used for an energy source. It is made from petroleum and from various other sources. It is named after Rudolf Diesel who in 1892 invented the diesel engine.[http://www.epa.gov/OMS/regs/fuels/diesel/diesel.htm United States EPA] ==Related pages== * Biodiesel ==References== Category:Fuel
