It is a Himalayan language of Tibeto-Burman branch of the Sino-Tibetan languages group. Newah Bhaaye is the term used for Nepal Bhasa by its native speakers. The term 'Newari' has been used in derogatory form to replace the original name of the language. == Linguistics == Nepal Bhasa shares the feature of Kirant and Tibetan dialects of Northern Himalayas. It consists of five major dialects and several sub-dialects spoken by Newa people living throughout the country. ==References== Category:Sino-Tibetan languages Category:Newar
