Subject: Evita (musical)
'''''Evita''''' is a musical theater. The lyrics were written by Tim Rice. The music was Musical composition by Andrew Lloyd Webber. The musical is based on the life of Eva Perón (1919-1952), the wife of Argentina President Juan Perón, which Che Guevara is telling. ''Evita'' opened on the West End theatre in 1978, and on Broadway in 1979. It won the Laurence Olivier Award for Best New Musical and the Tony Award for Best Musical. In 1996, a Evita (movie) was released starring Madonna Ciccone.
