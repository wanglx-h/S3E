Subject: Fingerplate
A '''fingerplate''' also known as a pushplate, is a plate that is fixed to a door near the handle or keyhole. It is for stopping dirt getting on the door. And is made of metal or glass. Their installation are no longer practical, and are now used for decoration. File:Door_of_Gamble_House,_Pasadena.jpg Category:Doors
