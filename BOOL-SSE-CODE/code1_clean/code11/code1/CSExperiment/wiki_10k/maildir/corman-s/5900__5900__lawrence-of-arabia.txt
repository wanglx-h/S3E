Subject: Lawrence of Arabia
'''''Lawrence of Arabia''''' is a United Kingdom epic movie from 1962, based on the life of T. E. Lawrence. It stars Peter O'Toole as the title character, along with Omar Sharif and Alec Guinness. The opening scene of the movie shows Lawrence dying in a motorcycle accident. The movie then goes into a series of flashbacks (past events) from his life. The 1982 movie ''Gandhi (movie)'' follows the story pattern of ''Lawrence of Arabia's'': after the main character dies at the start, it moves into a series of flashbacks from Gandhi's life. ''Lawrence of Arabia'' won seven Academy Awards, one of them for Best Picture. Considered one of the greatest movies ever made, it has appeared on American Film Institute's AFI's 100 Years... 100 Movies list (as the number five selecion).
