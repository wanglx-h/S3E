Subject: Design
'''Design''' is the visual appearence or shape given to an object. Design aims to make things prettier, more comfortable, or better in some way by making them work well and look good. Good design decisions can make a space, for example, a hospital or an airport, more Accessibility or an Picture more attractive for Advertising, adding value in the market. Designer use knowledge and rules from geometry and art to create their designs. Design is sometimes divided to sub-categories, including graphic design, interior design, vehicle design, furniture design, Product (business) design, Typography, fashion design and web design, to name a few. Design does not always lead to physical objects; it can involve Computer program, Website, Prototype, Presentation, or serve as a plan for creating something else. Design is also a concept used to create an object (virtual or not).
