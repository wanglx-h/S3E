== Events == * May 4 – The Municipality of Ilagan was founded in the Philippines. * July 17 – A meeting took place at Lüneburg between 'some evangelical Princes and Electors' and representatives of the King of Navarre, the King of Denmark and the Queen of England. The object of this meeting is the formation of an 'evangelical' league of defence against the Catholic League, called the 'Confederatio Militiae Evangelicae'.Statement is made on folio 35 of the dedication of the ''Naometria'' to the Duke of Wurttemberg, repeated on folio 122. Cf. A.E. Waite, ''Brotherhood of the Rosy Cross'', London, 1924, pp. 639 ff. * July 22 – New York City and Albany, New York are granted city charters by the colonial governor. * September 2 – The forces of the Holy League of 1684 liberate Buda from the Ottoman Empire rule that leads to the end of Turkish rule in Hungary during the subsequent years. === Undated === * The League of Augsburg (:en:League of Augsburg) is founded. * Russia, Saxony, Brandenburg and Bavaria join the Holy League against the Ottoman Empire. * A group of conspirators met at Charborough House in Dorset to plan the overthrow of
