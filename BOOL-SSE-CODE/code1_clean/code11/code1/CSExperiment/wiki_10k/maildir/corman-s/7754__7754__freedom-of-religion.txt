Subject: Freedom of religion
File:"Freedom of Worship" - NARA - 513537.jpg of 1943]] '''Freedom of religion''', also known as '''freedom of worship''', is the Human rights and Civil liberties to be able to practice a religion without fear of being Religious persecution. It usually involes Religious toleration that are not the majority religion and usually comes alongside a separation of church and state, although not always. According to the United Nations, freedom of religion is part of international law. == By country == === United States === In the United States, freedom of religion is part of the First Amendment to the United States Constitution, which states:United States Congress shall make no law respecting an State religion, or prohibiting the free exercise thereof; or abridging the freedom of speech, or Freedom of the press; or the right of the people Protest, and to petition the Federal government of the United States for a redress of grievances. ==Related pages== * Blasphemy * Religious conversion * Religious toleration * Separation of church and state * State religion * Secularism == Other websites == * [http://www.un.org/en/universal-declaration-human-rights/ Text of the United Nations Universal Declaration of Human Rights] Category:Religious ethics Category:Human rights Category:Freedom Category:Libertarianism
