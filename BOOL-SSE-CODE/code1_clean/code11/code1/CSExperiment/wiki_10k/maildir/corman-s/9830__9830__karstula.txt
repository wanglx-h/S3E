File:Karstula sijainti Suomi.svg Image:Karstula.vaakuna.svg '''Karstula''' is a municipality in Central Finland. More than 4,900 people live in Karstula. Karstula is located 100kilometres from the city of Jyväskylä. The municipality was founded in 1867.
