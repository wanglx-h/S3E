File:Statue de la nouvelle liberte Douala.jpg in Douala]] '''Douala''' is the largest city in the African country of Cameroon. It is the capital city of Littoral Region (Cameroon). The city is placed near the Wouri River. Its population is about 2,000,000 people. The first European people who came to Douala were from Portugal. They found Duala in about 1472. Before 1884 it was named '''Cameroons Town'''. In 1884 German people came to Duala and changed its name to '''Kamarunstadt''' ("Camaroon City") and made it the capital of German Kamerun. In 1907 its name was changed to Duala. It became the capital of French Cameroons in 1919. From 1940 to 1946, it was the capital of Cameroon. Duala is an wikt:expensive city. It ranked 24th most expensive city of the world in 2007. == Transport == Douala is linked by rail transport to Yaoundé, Ngaoundéré, Kumba and Nkongsamba.
