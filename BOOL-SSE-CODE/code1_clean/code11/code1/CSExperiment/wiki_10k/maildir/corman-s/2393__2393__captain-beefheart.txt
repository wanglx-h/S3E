File:Captain Beefheart in Toronto.jpg '''Don Van Vliet''' (born '''Don Glen Vliet''' January 15, 1941â€“ December 17, 2010) was an Americans singer. He is better known by his stage name '''Captain Beefheart'''. He was best known for his collaborations and (sometimes) rivalry, with long time friend, Frank Zappa. He is an inspire to many "Alternative rock" singers. He retirement from music due to illness and spent his time painting. He was born in Glendale, California, California. His stage name came from a movie project Frank Zappa was writing, but soon abandoned, "''Captain Beefheart battles the Grunt People''". His best known album was recorded in 1969. It was titled "Trout Mask Replica". He and his group ''Magic Band'' also made albums in the early 1980s that received positive music critic attention. Van Vliet was also a well known painter of abstract art. His work has been shown in Art gallery in New York City and Europe.
