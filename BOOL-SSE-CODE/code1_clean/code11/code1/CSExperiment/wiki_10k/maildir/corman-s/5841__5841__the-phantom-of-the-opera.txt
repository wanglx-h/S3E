Subject: The Phantom of the Opera
'''''The Phantom of the Opera''''' () is a French novel by Gaston Leroux. It was published in 1910. An English language edition came out a year later. The book was adapted to The Phantom of the Opera (musical) in 1986. The musical was made into a movie in 2004. Movie versions include The Phantom of the Opera (1925 movie) in 1925, one from 1943 with Claude Rains, another from 1962 with Herbert Lom, and ''The Phantom of the Paradise'' (1974). The story has been adapted to made for television movies several times. The band iron maiden made a song name the phanTom of the Opera from their self titled album iron maiden.
