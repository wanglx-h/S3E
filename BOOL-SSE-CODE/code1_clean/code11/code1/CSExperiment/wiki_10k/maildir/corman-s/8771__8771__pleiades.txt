Subject: Pleiades
File:Pleiades large.jpg The '''Pleiades''' (also known as '''M45''' or the '''Seven Sisters''') are a star cluster of stars in the night sky. You can see them in the constellation Taurus (constellation), the bull. Charles Messier gave it the name M45. They are named after the Pleiades (mythology) in Greek mythology. The brightest star in the Pleiades is the giant star Alcyone (star). This an open star cluster of middle-aged hot Stellar classification#Hot B emission star classes in the constellation of Taurus (constellation). It is one of the nearest star clusters to Earth, and is the cluster most obvious to the naked eye in the night sky. The cluster is dominated by Stellar classification#Hot blue emission star classes stars that have formed within the last 100 million years. Category:Open star clusters
