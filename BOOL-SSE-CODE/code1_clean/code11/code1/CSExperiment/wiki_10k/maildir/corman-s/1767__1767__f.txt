File:F cursiva.gif '''F''' is the sixth letter in the English language alphabet. == Meanings for F == * In calendars, F is often an abbreviation for Friday, or for the month February. * In chemistry, F is the symbol for fluorine. * In education, F is a failing Grade (education) * In music, F is a Note (music), sometimes referred to as “Fa”. * In temperature, °F is degrees Fahrenheit. * In logic, F stands for False, as opposed to T for True * In slang, F stands for the curse word, but could also stand for respect of the person. ==Other websites==
