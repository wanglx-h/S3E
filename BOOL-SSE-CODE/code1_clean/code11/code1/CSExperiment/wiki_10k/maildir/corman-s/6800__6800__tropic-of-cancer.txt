Subject: Tropic of Cancer
File:Earth-lighting-winter-solstice EN.png File:World map with tropic of cancer.svg The '''Tropic of Cancer''' is one of the five major circle of latitude (imaginary lines around the Earth) that are often marked on maps of the Earth. This is the Circle of latitude of latitude that is degree (angle) north of the Equator. At every June Solstice the Sun passes straight overhead on the tropic.
