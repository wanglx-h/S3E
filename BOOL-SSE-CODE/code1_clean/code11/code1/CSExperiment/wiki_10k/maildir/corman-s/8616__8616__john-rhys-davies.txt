Subject: John Rhys-Davies
Image:John Rhys-Davies byVetulani.JPG '''John Rhys-Davies''' (born 5 May 1944, in Ammanford, Carmarthenshire, Wales) is a Welsh actor. Although he has played in many movies, Rhys-Davies is probably best known for his characters in two blockbuster movie series: Sallah in the ''Indiana Jones'' movies and the dwarf Gimli in the The Lord of the Rings movie trilogy.
