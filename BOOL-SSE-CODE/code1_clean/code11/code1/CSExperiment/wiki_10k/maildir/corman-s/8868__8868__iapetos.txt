Subject: Iapetos
'''Iapetos''', father of man kind and mountains, also known as Iaepetus or Japetus, is a Titan (mythology) in Greek mythology. He is the son of Uranus and Gaia (mythology). With the Oceanid Klymene his children are Atlas (titan), Prometheus, Epimetheus and Menoitios. After the war against the Twelve Olympians he was banned to the Tartarus with his brother, Kronos and all of the other Titans. Category:Titans
