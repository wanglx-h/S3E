Subject: Loreto Region
Image:Bandera_Región_Loreto.png Image:Escudo Región Loreto.png '''Loreto''' is Peru's most northern Regions of Peru. Covering almost one-third of Peru's territory, Loreto is the nation's biggest region. It is also one of the least populated ones, because of its remote location in the Amazon River rainforest. Its capital is the city of Iquitos. ==Boundaries== * Northwest: Ecuadorian Provinces of Ecuador of Sucumbíos, Orellana Province, Pastaza Province and Morona-Santiago * North: Colombian Departments of Colombia of Putumayo Department * Northeast: Amazonas Department, also in Colombia * East: Brazilian States of Brazil of Amazonas (Brazilian state) and Acre (state) * South: Ucayali Region and Huánuco Region regions * West: San Martín Region and Amazonas Region regions == Administrative divisions== The region is divided into eight provinces (''provincias'', singular: ''provincia''), which are composed of 53 districts (''distritos'', singular: ''distrito'').
