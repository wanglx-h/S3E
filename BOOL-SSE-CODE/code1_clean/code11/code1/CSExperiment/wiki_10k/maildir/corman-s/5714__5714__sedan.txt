Subject: Sedan
'''Sedan''' can mean any of a few things: *A Sedan (automobile) is a car body style *The much older form of transport, the sedan chair, from which the name of the car body style is borrowed. *Sedan, Ardennes, a ''sous-préfecture'' of the Ardennes département in France, where the Battle of Sedan occurred in 1870 *Sedan, Kansas, a community in the United States *Sedan, Minnesota, a community in the United States * Sedan (nuclear test), an Operation Plowshare nuclear test conducted by the United States at its Nevada Test Site in 1962, which resulted in a massive crater.
