Subject: Monounsaturated fat
A '''monounsaturated fat''' is a kind of fat found in foods such as Nut (fruit), Olive (fruit), and avocados.Note the pronunciation: it is "mono-unsaturated" They are the main fat in olive oil. The group contrasts with, on the one hand, unsaturated fats. and on the other hand, polyunsaturated fats."Essential Fatty Acids". Micronutrient Information Center, Oregon State University, Corvallis, OR. May 2014. Retrieved 24 May 2017. [https://lpi.oregonstate.edu/mic/other-nutrients/essential-fatty-acids] In general, these fats are considered to be healthier than others. They have the same amount of energy, but they reduce blood cholesterol levels.
