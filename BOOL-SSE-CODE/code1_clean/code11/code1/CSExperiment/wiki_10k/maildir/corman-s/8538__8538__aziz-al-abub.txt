Subject: Aziz al-Abub
'''Aziz al-Abub''' (a.k.a. Ibrahim al-Nadhir or al-Nahdhir) was a Lebanon Hezbollah psychiatrist and mind control expert. Aziz was a disciple of Ewen Cameron and a graduate of the Soviet Union's :en:Peoples'_Friendship_University_of_Russia that specialized in mind control techniques. Aziz al-Abub used mind-control, drugs and physical torture in the infamous 444-day (1984-1985) torture and mental derangement of United States :en:William_Francis_Buckley in Beirut.
