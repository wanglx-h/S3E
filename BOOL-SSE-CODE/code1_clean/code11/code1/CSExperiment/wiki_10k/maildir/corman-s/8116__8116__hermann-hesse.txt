File:Hermann Hesse 1927 Photo Gret Widmann.jpg File:Hermann Hesse Der Steppenwolf 1927.jpg '''Hermann Hesse''' (2 July 1877 – 9 August 1962) was a Germany-Switzerland poet, novelist, and painter. In 1946, he received the Nobel Prize in Literature.
