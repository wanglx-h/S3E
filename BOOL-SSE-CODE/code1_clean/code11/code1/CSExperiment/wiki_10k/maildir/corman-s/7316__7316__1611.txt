'''1611''' (Roman numerals) was a common year starting on Saturday in the Gregorian calendar and a common year starting on Tuesday in the Julian calendar. == Events == * June 23 – Henry Hudson's crew maroons him, his son and 7 others in a boat * November 1 – At Whitehall Palace in London, William Shakespeare's romantic comedy ''The Tempest (play)'' is presented for the first time. * Gustavus Adolphus of Sweden becomes king of Sweden * Denmark attacks Sweden * King James Version of the Bible first published in England * George Abbot (Archbishop of Canterbury) becomes archbishop of Canterbury, Kent * Johannes Fabricius and David Fabricius discover sunspots * End of the reign of Emperor Go-Yozei of Japan * Emperor Go-Mizunoo of Japan ascends to the throne of Japan * Gov. Sir Thomas Dale arrives at Jamestown from England announcing new rules called "Laws Divine, Morall and Martial." * Thomas Dale founds the city of Henricus on the James River Virginia a few miles south of present-day History of Richmond, Virginia Category:1611
