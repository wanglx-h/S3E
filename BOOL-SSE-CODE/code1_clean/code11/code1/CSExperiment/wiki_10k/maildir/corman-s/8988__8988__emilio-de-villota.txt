Subject: Emilio de Villota
'''Emilio de Villota Ruíz''' (born in Madrid, July 26, 1946) is a former racing driver from Spain. He participated in 15 Formula One World Championship Grands Prix, debuting on May 2, 1976. He scored no championship points.
