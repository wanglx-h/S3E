The municipality is the seventh largest in Spain. The city is in the Segura Valley, in an area called la ''Huerta de Murcia'', which is a very important agricultural center with Orange (fruit), lemons, tomatoes, onions, etc.; the varied fruits and vegetables typical of the Mediterranean lands. It has a hot semi-arid climate (''BSh'' in the Koeppen climate classification). The city has a Baroque cathedral and a university. Category:Capital cities in Spain Category:Murcia
