Subject: Biltine (prefecture)
File:Chad-Biltine.PNG '''Biltine''' was one of fourteen prefectures, or areas, of Chad. The capital of Biltine is '''Biltine'''. In 1993, it had a population of 148,807 people and an area of 46,850 square kilometers of land. As of 2002, the area is now part of Wadi Fira Region Category:Prefectures of Chad
