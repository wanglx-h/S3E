Subject: Aymara people
File:Banner of the Qulla Suyu (1979).svg The '''Aymara''' are members of a tribe of Native Americans and live in Bolivia and Peru. They are native to the area around Lake Titicaca and speak the Aymara language. They call themselves Aymaras. The president of Bolivia, Evo Morales, was Aymara. The many-colored flag of the tribe is called the Wiphala. Category:Ethnic groups in South America Category:Native Americans
