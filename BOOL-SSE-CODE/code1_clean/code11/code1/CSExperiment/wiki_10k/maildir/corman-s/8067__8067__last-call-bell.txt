Subject: Last call bell
The '''last call bell''' is a bell found in Bar (business). The last call bell is rung to: *Tell people that the bar is getting ready to stop selling drinks *Tell people that the bar is no longer selling drinks *Tell people the bar is closed. Last call bells are related to bells used on ships. == Other websites == *[http://pist.ca/article.php/23ggg The National Pist article] Category:bells
