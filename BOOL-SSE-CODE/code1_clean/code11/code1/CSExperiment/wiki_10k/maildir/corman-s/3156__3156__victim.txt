Subject: Victim
'''Victim''' is a word for someone suffering from bad conditions, often related to Crime committed against them. If someone is Robbery, he or she is a victim. If someone is Murder, the person is a victim. ==Victim blaming== Victim blaming refers to the act of saying that it is the victim's fault that a crime was committed against them. ==Related pages== * Rape * Genocide * Holocaust victims * Child sexual abuse Category:Criminal justice
