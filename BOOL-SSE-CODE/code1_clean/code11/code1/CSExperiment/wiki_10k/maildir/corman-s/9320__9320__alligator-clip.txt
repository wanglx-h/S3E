Subject: Alligator clip
Image:Three alligator clips.JPG '''Alligator clips''' are toothed clips on the ends of electric wires. They are hinged near the back, making them look like alligator jaws. Sometimes they are also called '''crocodile clips'''. Usually those clips are used in measuring to temporarily connect a meter to an electrical circuit or a single component.
