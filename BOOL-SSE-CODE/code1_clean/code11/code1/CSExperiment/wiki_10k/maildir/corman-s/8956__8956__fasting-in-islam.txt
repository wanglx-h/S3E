Subject: Fasting in Islam
'''Fasting in Islam''' or '''''Sawm''''' () or '''Roza''' (Farsi: روزہ) is the practice of abstaining, usually from food, drink, smoking, and sexual activity. Fourth of the Five Pillars of Islam, ''Sawm'' is observed between dawn and nightfall during the holy month of Ramadan. Category:Islamic culture Some exceptions or accommodations for ''Sawm'' are made in the case of pregnancy, illness, travel, and for young children.
