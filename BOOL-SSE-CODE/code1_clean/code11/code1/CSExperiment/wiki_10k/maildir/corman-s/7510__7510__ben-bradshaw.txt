(born 30 August 1960) is an England politician and the Member of Parliament for the Exeter (UK Parliament constituency) constituency in the United Kingdom. He was wikt:elect in the United Kingdom general election, 1997. He was a BBC Radio reporter before entering politics. He was a Minister (government) in the Department of Health, a Minister for the South West and Secretary of State for Culture, Media and Sport. He holds no posts in the current shadow government.
