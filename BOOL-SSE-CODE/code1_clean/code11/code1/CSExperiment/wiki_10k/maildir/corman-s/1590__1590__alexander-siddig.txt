Siddig is most famous for playing Dr Julian Bashir in the ''Star Trek'' television series ''Star Trek: Deep Space Nine''. Siddig married fellow ''Star Trek'' actor Nana Visitor in 1997. They had a son in 1996. Siddig and Visitor divorced in 2001.
