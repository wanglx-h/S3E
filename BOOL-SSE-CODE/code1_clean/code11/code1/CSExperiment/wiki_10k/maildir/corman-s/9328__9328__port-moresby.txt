Subject: Port Moresby
File:Papua New Guinea map.png and Port Moresby]] Image:Flag of Port Moresby.png '''Port Moresby''' (9°30′S 147°12′E) is the capital city and the main city of Papua New Guinea. In 2000, 255,000 people lived there. The city is on the shores of the Gulf of Papua, on the southeastern coast of the island of New Guinea. The area on which the city was built was first sighted by a European in 1873 by John Moresby. It was named in honor of his father Sir Fairfax Moresby.
