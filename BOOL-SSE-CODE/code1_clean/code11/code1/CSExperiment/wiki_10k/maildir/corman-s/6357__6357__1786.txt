== Events == * May 21 – Trial of the Affair of the diamond necklace ends in Paris * August 8 – Mont Blanc was climbed for the first time by Dr. Michael-Gabriel Paccard and Jacques Balmat. *August 29– Shays Rebellion begins * September 2 – Hurricane in England. * November 7 – The oldest musical organization in the United States was founded as the Stoughton Musical Society. * November 30 – Leopold II, Holy Roman Emperor of Habsburg-Lorraine, Grand Duke of Tuscany, promulgates a penal reform making his country the first state to abolish the death penalty. November 30 is therefore commemorated by 300 cities around the world as Cities for Life Day. * December 4 – The Mission Santa Barbara, was founded by Fermin Lasuen, becoming the tenth mission in the California mission chain. *Choctaw Treaty *Chickasaw Treaty *Robert Burns publishes ''Poems, Chiefly in the Scottish Dialect'' * Francis Light acquires the island of Penang from the Sultan of Kedah on behalf of the British East India Company.
