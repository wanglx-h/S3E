Subject: The Doors
'''The Doors''' were an United States Rock music band. They were one of the most Popularity rock groups of all time. Led by singer Jim Morrison, along with Ray Manzarek (Keyboard instrument), Robby Krieger (guitar) and John Densmore (Drum), the group is thought of today as a major influence on rock music, and they are still just as popular as they ever were, if not more popular. The group was widely known for its lead singer, Jim Morrison, who was famous for his love of alcohol and illegal Illegal drugs. Morrison often sang at concerts after using alcohol and drugs, sometimes so drunk that he could not remember the words to his own songs. Once, Morrison was said to have shown his private parts to the audience, and he was charged with a crime. Morrison died in Paris, France, on July 3, 1971. His death is thought to have been caused by a heart attack brought on by a drug overdose. There is a 1991 in film movie about them, also called ''The Doors (movie)''.
