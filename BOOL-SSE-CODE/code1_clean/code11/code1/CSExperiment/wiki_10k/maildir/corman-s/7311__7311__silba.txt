|location=Adriatic Sea|nickname=|native_name_link=|native_name=|map_caption=|map_image=Croatia - Silba.PNG|image_size=|image_caption=Sotorišće bay on Silba|additional_info=}}'''Silba''' is an island in Croatia. It is found between the islands of Premuda and Olib. It has an area of 15km² and its population (as of 2001) was 265; with the village of Silba located at the narrowest point in the middle. The main administrative centre is Zadar, found on the mainland. Category:Mediterranean Islands Category:Islands of Croatia
