Subject: Googolplex
A '''googolplex''' is the number 10googol, equivalent to 1010100 or 10googol or 1010,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000. Like the number ''googol'', ''googolplex'' was thought of by Milton Sirotta, the nephew of mathematician Edward Kasner. The company Google named their wikt:headquarters, the Googleplex, after the number.
