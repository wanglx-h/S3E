'''13''' is a year in the 1st century. It was a common year starting on Sunday of the Julian calendar. At the time, it was known as the '''Year of the Roman consul of Silius and Plancus'''. == Events == * Abgarus of Edessa is reinstalled as king of Osroene. * Roman Senate passed a senatus consultum restricting the reduced Vigintisexviri to the Ordo Equester. * Tiberius made his triumphant procession through Rome after siege of Germany. * Last year of ''Shijianguo'' era of the Chinese Xin Dynasty * Strabo publishes his view on the shape of the Earth == Deaths == * Empress Wang Zhengjun (b. 71 BC) Category:13
