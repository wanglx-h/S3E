| logo = | academic_staff = ~ 4026 | students = ~ 43700 | former_names = Vladimir Ulyanov-Lenin Kazan State University, Kazan Imperial University | language = Russian, English }} '''Kazan (Volga region) Federal University''' (, ''Kazanskiy (Privolzhskiy) federalnyy universitet''; ) is a university in Kazan, Tatarstan, Russia. It was founded in 1804. The famous Russian mathematician Lobachevsky was its Head teacher from 1827 till 1846. The post is now being held by Myakzyum Salakhov (Mäğzüm Säläxev).
