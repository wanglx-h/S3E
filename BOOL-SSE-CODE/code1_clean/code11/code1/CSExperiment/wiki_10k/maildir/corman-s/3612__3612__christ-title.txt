Subject: Christ (title)
In Christianity, '''the Christ''' (; ) is a title for the savior and redeemer who would bring Salvation (Christianity) to the Jew and mankind. Christ is the Greek translation of the Hebrew title ''Messiah'' (; anointed one), the figure promised by God to the Jews and mankind for the salvation of the world. Christians believe that Jesus is the Jewish Messiah called Christ of the Old Testament and fulfilled the messianic prophecies of Christian tradition.
