It is the main town of the Marburg-Biedenkopf Rural District. It has about 80,000 inhabitants and a small, but well-known university, the Philipps-University Marburg. The upper part of Old town, near the castle and above the former village 'Weidenhausen (Marburg)' at river Lahn and a bridge is called 'Oberstadt' (upper town). There are timber framing houses, similar to some houses in Alsfeld (Vogelsbergkreis).
