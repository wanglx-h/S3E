The band's original name was '''Tricky Beans'''. After releasing three demo recordings it was changed into '''Tricky Means'''. Afterwards the band adopted its current name. Before they became as famous and popular as they are, they got attention from metal fans for touring and playing with the band Nightwish. Sonata Arctica's first music album was ''Ecliptica''. It was released on November 1999 in Finland and elsewhere in 2000. 30,000 copies were sold in Japan.
