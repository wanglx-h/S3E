Subject: Talavera de la Reina
Image:Bandera de Talavera.svg Image:Coat of Arms of Talavera de la Reina.svg '''Talavera of the Queen''' (''Talavera de la Reina'' in Spanish language) is a city of the interior of Spain. It is the capital of a wide region belonging to the Autonomous communities of Spain of Castile-La Mancha. It is in the northwest of the Toledo province. Talavera of the Queen is the second biggest city of Castile-La Mancha, with a population of 85.172 inhabitants. The city is well known for its pottery. Its mistress is ''Our Lady of the Meadow''.
