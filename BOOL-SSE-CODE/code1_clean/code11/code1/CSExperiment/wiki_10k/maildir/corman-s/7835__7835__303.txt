== Events == === By place === ==== Roman Empire ==== * Diocletianic Persecution: Emperor Diocletian launches the last major persecution of Christians in the Empire. Hierocles (proconsul) is said to have started the fierce persecution of the Christians under Galerius. They are forbidden to worship in groups, and thousands of them are killed in the next 10 years. * February 24 Galerius publishes his edict that begins the persecution of Christians in his portion of the Empire. * September 25 On a voyage preaching the gospel, Saint Fermin of Pamplona is Decapitation in Amiens, France. * November 28 Diocletian makes a visit to Rome. The Augustus (title) and the Caesar (title) are united for the first time to celebrate the 20th anniversary of Diocletian's accession. * The Triumphal Arch of Maximian is erected in Salonica. ==== Asia ==== * Sassanid dynasty Shah Hormizd II of Persia succeeds Narseh of Persia. ==== America ==== * In Mexico the civilization of Teotihuacan flourishes. === By topic === ==== Religion ==== * March 4 Martyrdom of Adrian of Nicomedia. == Births == * Magnentius, Roman usurper (d. 353) * Wang Xizhi, Chinese Chinese calligraphy (d. 361) == Deaths == * April 23 Saint George, soldier of the Roman Empire * September 25 Fermin, Roman Catholic saint * Acacius of Byzantium, centurion in the Roman army and martyr * Adrian of Nicomedia, Christian saint * Anthimus of Rome * Cao Huan, last emperor of the Cao Wei * Cessianus, Roman Catholic saint and martyr * Crescentinus, patron saint of Urbino * Erasmus of Formiae * Expeditus, Roman commander and martyr * Felix and Adauctus, Christian martyrs * Lu Ji, Chinese writer and literary critic * Romanus of Caesarea, martyr * Saint Cyriacus * Devota * Saint Pantaleon * Victor Maurus, Christian martyr * Vitus, Christian saint from Sicily Category:303
