Subject: Belief
A '''belief''' is a firm wikt:thought that something is true, often based on revelation. Belief is usually a part of belonging to a religion. It is different to scientific knowledge that can be tested, but belief is not able to be tested. The word is also used to describe what a person expects will happen based on limited information. ==Examples== For instance, a person may believe in God or gods, while "I believe Amy will come around today" can also be a belief.
