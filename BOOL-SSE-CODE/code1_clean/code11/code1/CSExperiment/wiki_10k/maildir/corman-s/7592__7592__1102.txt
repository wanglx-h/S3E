'''1102''' (Roman numerals) was a common year starting on Wednesday in the Julian calendar. == Events == * Valencia, Spain is captured by the Almoravids. * Henry I of England orders the tomb of Edward the Confessor opened; the body is found undecayed. * Henry I of England takes possession of Arundel Castle. * The Hohenbaden castle is built in Baden-Baden, Germany. * Boleslaus III of Poland becomes List of Polish rulers. * coronation of Coloman of Hungary in the town of Biograd, adding the Croatian duchies of Slavonia and Dalmatia to the crown of Hungary. * Crusaders capture Caesarea Palaestina. * Crusaders defeat an Egyptian invasion of the Kingdom of Jerusalem near Ascalon. * Dagobert of Pisa is briefly deposed as Latin Patriarch of Jerusalem (and restored later in the year). * Raymond IV of Toulouse begins to besiege Tripoli, Lebanon, and takes the nominal title of County of Tripoli. * Raymond IV of Toulouse is imprisoned by Tancred, Prince of Galilee, regent of the Principality of Antioch.
