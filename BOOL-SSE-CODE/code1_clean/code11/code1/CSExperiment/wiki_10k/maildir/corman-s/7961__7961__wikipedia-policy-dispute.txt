Subject: Wikipedia:Policy dispute
A '''policy dispute''' is where a policy has been created, but there are at least two different factions which disagree on what the policy is or how it works. If a policy is wikt:dispute, the two groups should discuss the policy on its discussion page to attempt to reach an agreement or compromise. ==Related pages== *:en:Wikipedia:Policy dispute on English Wikipedia *Wikipedia:Policies and guidelines
