Subject: Mayon Volcano
File:Mt.Mayon tam3rd.jpg The '''Mayon Volcano''', also known as '''Mount Mayon''', is an active volcano in the Provinces of the Philippines of Albay, on Luzon island in the Philippines. It is a part of the Pacific Ring of Fire. It is one the most active and dangerous volcanoes in the Philippines.Greg Bloom, ''Philippines'' (Footscray, Vic.; London: Lonely Planet, 2009), p. 198 Mount Mayon is high, and it is famous for its "perfect cone" shape, which attracts tourists. In 1938 the mountain was declared a national park. It was reclassified and renamed as ''Mayon Volcano Natural Park'' in the year 2000.[http://pawb.gov.ph/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=65:region-5&catid=69:regions "Protected Areas in Region 5"] . Protected Areas and Wildlife Bureau. Retrieved on 2011-10-15.
