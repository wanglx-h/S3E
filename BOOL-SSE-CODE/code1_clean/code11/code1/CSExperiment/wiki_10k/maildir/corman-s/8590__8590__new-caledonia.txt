Subject: New Caledonia
Image:Flag of FLNKS.svg Image:Emblem of New Caledonia.svg '''New Caledonia''' (; popular name: ''Le caillou'') () is a "''sui generis'' collectivity" (in practice an List of dependent territories) of France, made up of a main island (''Grande Terre'') and several smaller islands. It is in the subregion of Melanesia in the southwest Pacific Ocean. At about half the size of Taiwan, it has a land area of 18,575.5 square kilometres (7,172square mile). At the 2004 census, 230,789 people lived there. It has an internet country code top-level domain (ccTLD) of .nc. The capital and largest city of the territory is Nouméa. The currency is the CFP franc. The inflation rate is -0.6%. The unemployment rate is 17.1% A New Caledonian independence referendum, 2018 to see if New Caledonia should declare independence from France. The results were more people wanted to stay in France than leave.
