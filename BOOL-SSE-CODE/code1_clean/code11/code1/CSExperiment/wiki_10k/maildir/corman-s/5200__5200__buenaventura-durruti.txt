Subject: Buenaventura Durruti
Image:Buenaventura Durruti, 1936 (colorized).png '''José''' '''Buenaventura Durruti Dumange''' (14 July 1896 &#x2013; 20 November 1936) was a Spanish people anarchy leader. He was birth in the town of León, Spain. Durruti was a central figure of Spanish anarchism during the Spanish Civil War civil war of 1936-1939. He fought against fascism. He robbed banks to use the money to help his friends escape prison.[http://www.theyliewedie.org/ressources/biblio/en/Unknown_-_The_Hiddens_Wanderings_of_Durruti.html The Hiddens Wanderings of Durruti] It is believed that he killed the bishop Juan Soldevilla. Some anarchists believed Soldevilla helped people who murdered people in trades union.
