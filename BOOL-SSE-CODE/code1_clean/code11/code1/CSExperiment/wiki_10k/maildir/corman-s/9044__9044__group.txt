File:Arabian orix.JPG]]A '''group''' is a set of things or a number of individuals who have regular contact and frequent interaction. ==Examples of groups== * A family of wikt:people * A herd of animals * A class of students * A sports team * Ethnic group * The apples on a tree * The hats in a box * Britain, China, France, Russia, and the United States of America are in the United Nations Security Council. * Carbon, hydrogen, nitrogen, oxygen and phosphorus are a group of chemical elements that life needs.
