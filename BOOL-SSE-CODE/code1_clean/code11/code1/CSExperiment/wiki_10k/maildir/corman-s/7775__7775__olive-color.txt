File:NCI 2 green olives.jpg]] File:Peridot2.jpg]] '''Olive''' is a color which looks like green or yellow. (when gray or black is added to yellow, the various shades of the color olive are produced). Some dark shades of olive can also be made by mixing a darker color (like brown) with green. The most common place you will find the color olive is on an Olive (fruit). It is the color of the outside of an olive. An olive's center is usually brown or sometimes Orange (color) or red.
