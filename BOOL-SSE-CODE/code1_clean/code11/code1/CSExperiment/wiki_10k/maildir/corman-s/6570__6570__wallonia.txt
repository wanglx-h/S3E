Its official languages are French language (98%) and German language (2%). Many people understand the local language Walloon language, some still speak it, and few people write it. German is spoken mainly in the area of the German-speaking Community of Belgium. The capital of the region is Namur (city). Other important cities are: Liège (city), Charleroi, Mons, Tournai and Verviers. French speaking Walloons share the French community with French speaking people of Brussels.
