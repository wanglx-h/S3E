| Notable_instruments = | Current_members = Jim CreegganKevin HearnEd RobertsonTyler Stewart | Past_members = Andy CreegganSteven Page }} '''Barenaked Ladies''' are a Canada band. They formed in Toronto, Canada in 1988. The band is made up of four men. In 1991, the band was taken off the program for a New Year's Eve concert in Toronto because someone thought the name was bad. The band started as a duo with Ed Roberstson and Steven Page. Lead singer Page left the band in 2009 to begin a solo career.
