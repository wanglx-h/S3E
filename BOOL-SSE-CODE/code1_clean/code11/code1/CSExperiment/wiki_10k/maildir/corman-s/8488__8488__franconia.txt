This region is divided in three smaller regions: Upper Franconia Government Region, Middle Franconia, and Lower Franconia Government Region. The biggest city in Franconia is Nuremberg. Other large city in Franconia are Coburg, Aschaffenburg, Würzburg, Erlangen, Fürth, Bamberg, Bayreuth, Schweinfurt and Hof. == Rivers == The most important river in Franconia is the Regnitz, which is 63.7 km (39.6 mi) in length.
