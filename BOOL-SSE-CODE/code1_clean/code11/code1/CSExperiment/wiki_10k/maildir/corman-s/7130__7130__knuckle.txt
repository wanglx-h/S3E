Subject: Knuckle
:''Knuckles the Echidna is also the name of a Sonic the Hedgehog character.'' A '''knuckle''', in anatomy, is where two bones join in the hands or foot. When talking about humans, knuckles most often refer to the joints in the fingers. Animal knuckles, such as ''pig knuckles'' are sometimes sold as food. Category:Joints
