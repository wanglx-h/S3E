Subject: Keyboardist
A '''keyboardist''' is a musician who plays one or more of the musical instruments called Keyboard instrument instruments, such as the piano, Organ (music), or electronic synthesizer. They may also use MIDI. A person who plays piano is also called a pianist. A person who plays organ is called an organist. Many types of music use keyboardists, such as Rock and roll, pop music, funk, and classical music. Category:Musicians Category:Entertainment occupations
