On June 27, 2018, Kennedy announced his retirement from the Supreme Court, effective July 31. ==Background== Kennedy was born in Sacramento, California, California, on July 23, 1936. He went to Harvard University, Stanford University, and the London School of Economics. He was a private lawyer, a professor, and in 1975, he was appointed by Gerald Ford to the Ninth Circuit Court of Appeals. In 1988, Ronald Reagan nominated him for the Supreme Court. He became a member of the court on February 18, 1988.
