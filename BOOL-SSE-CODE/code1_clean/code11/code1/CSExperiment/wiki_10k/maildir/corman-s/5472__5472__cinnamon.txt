Subject: Cinnamon
File:Cinnamomum verum.jpg '''Cinnamon''' is a spice from the inner bark of ''Cinnamomum verum''. The spice is used in both Taste#Sweetness and savoury foods. It is aromatic. People usually put cinnamon in sweet baked goods (such as cinnamon rolls), hot wine or liquors. Cinnamon was used to make the spiced wine, claret, in the Middle Ages. In India, cinnamon is commonly used in making flavoured tea. It is either directly added as small pieces or put in powdered form during the preparation of tea. It is known as "Daal-Cheeni (दाल चीनि)" in Hindi. The Sinhalese name of cinnamon is ''kurundu''.
