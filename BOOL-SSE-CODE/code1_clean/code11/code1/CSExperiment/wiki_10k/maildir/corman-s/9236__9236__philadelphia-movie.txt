Subject: Philadelphia (movie)
'''''Philadelphia''''' is a 1993 United States drama movie starring Tom Hanks and Denzel Washington. It is about Andrew Beckett (Hanks), a gay lawyer who has AIDS. The people he works with find out and dismissal him.
