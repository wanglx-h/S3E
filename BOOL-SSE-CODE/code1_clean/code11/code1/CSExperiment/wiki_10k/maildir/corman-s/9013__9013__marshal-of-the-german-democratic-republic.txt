Subject: Marshal of the German Democratic Republic
{|align="right" |File:GDR Army Arab OF6-9.jpgs]] |File:GDR_Army_OF10_Marschall_der_DDR.gif |} '''Marschall der DDR''' (Marshal of the German Democratic Republic), was the highest rank in the Nationale Volksarmee, the army of the DDR. It was founded on 25th March 1982. The reason was a strategy change in the armies of Warsaw Pact. After the change the DDR needed a new rank to be sure there was only one high command. The rank was never used and abolished in 1989. Category:East Germany
