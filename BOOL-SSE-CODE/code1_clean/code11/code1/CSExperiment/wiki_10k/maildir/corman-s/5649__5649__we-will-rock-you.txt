It was the fourth single from their second studio album, ''Invincible (Five album)'' (1999). The song has rapping from Five's members Jason "J" Brown and Abz Love, and has two members of Queen (band): Brian May on guitar and Roger Taylor (Queen drummer) on drums. Freddie Mercury had died in November 1991, nearly a decade before this version's release, and John Deacon had retired from public life three years before the release of the Five cover.
