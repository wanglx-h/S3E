File:Shallots - sliced and whole.jpgs, a type of bulb that people use for food.]] A '''bulb''' is the part of some plants that is mostly under the Soil and stores food while the plant rests from growing. Some well-known bulbs people grow to use as food are onions and garlic. Some bulbs grown for the decorative flowers are lily, tulip, and some iris (plant). Some plant storage organs that are similar to bulbs but have different names are tubers, corms (swollen stems), tuberous roots, and pseudobulbs. Category:Plant anatomy
