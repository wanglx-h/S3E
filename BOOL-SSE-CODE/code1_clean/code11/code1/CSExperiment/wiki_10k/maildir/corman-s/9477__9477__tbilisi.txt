Subject: Tbilisi
File:View of Tbilisi from Mt Mtatsminda (1).jpg File:Flag of Tbilisi.svg File:Seal of Tbilisi, Georgia.svg '''Tbilisi''' is the capital, and the largest city in Georgia (country). It was founded in the 5th century by the Georgian king, Vakhtang Gorgasali. According to legend, when he was hunting, he killed a pheasant which dropped into hot water. Gorgasali discovered there were a lot of hot Spring (water) in this place, which he called Tbilisi as ''tbili'' means '''hot''' in Georgian.
