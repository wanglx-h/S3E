Subject: Sunrise (movie)
File:Sunrise - A Song of Two Humans.jpg '''''Sunrise: A Song of Two Humans''''' or '''''Sunrise''''' is a 1927 silent movie. It is the only movie to ever win Academy Award for Unique and Artistic Production. This category was not used again. It won the award for Best Cinematography and actress, Janet Gaynor, won the Best Actress award. It was directed by F.W.
