Image:Stratford upon-Avon - Warwickshire dot.png of Stratford-upon-Avon]] Image:StratfordAvon20040717.jpg]] '''Stratford-upon-Avon''' is a town in England. It is famous as the birthplace of William Shakespeare. It lies on the River Avon, Warwickshire, 22 miles (35 km) south east of Birmingham and 8 miles (13 km) south west of the county town, Warwick.
