feature ID | blank1_info = 0627105 | website = }} '''Grand Rapids''' is an United States city in Michigan. Located in western Michigan, nearly 200,000 people were living here in 2020. Former American president (United States) Gerald Ford grew up in Grand Rapids. Grand Rapids is also the birthplace of Amway and the Meijer store chain. Grand Rapids is the county seat of Kent County, Michigan. It is the second largest city in the state, after Detroit, Michigan. The third largest is Warren. The city has many attractions, including the Frederik Meijer Gardens & Sculpture Park and John Ball Zoo. Grand Valley State University has a campus in downtown Grand Rapids.
