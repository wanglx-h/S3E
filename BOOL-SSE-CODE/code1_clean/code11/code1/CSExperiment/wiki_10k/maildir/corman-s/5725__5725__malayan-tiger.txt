Subject: Malayan tiger
The '''Malayan tiger''' (''Panthera tigris jacksoni'') is a subspecies of the tiger that was discovered in 2004. People used to think they were Indo-Chinese tigers, But now they have their own subspecies. They have the same length and weight as the Indo-Chinese tiger.
