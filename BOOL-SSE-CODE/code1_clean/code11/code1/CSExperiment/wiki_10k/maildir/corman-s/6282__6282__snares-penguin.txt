Subject: Snares penguin
File:SnaresPenguin (Mattern).jpg The '''Snares penguin''' (''Eudyptes robustus''), is a crested penguin. It is probably a subspecies of the Fiordland penguin. It is a penguin from New Zealand. It is between 50 and 70 centimetres in height and weighs between 2.5 and 4 kilograms. It breeds on The Snares, a small island group south of New Zealand's South Island. Category:Penguins
