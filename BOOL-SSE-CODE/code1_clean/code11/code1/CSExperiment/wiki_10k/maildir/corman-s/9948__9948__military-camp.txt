Subject: Military camp
File:Conway Camp-1911.jpg A '''military camp''' or '''bivouac''' is a semi-permanent wikt:facility for where an army stays when it is not at its military base. Camps are erected when a military force travels away from a major installation or fort during training. It is also used for military operations, and often form large campsites. In the Ancient Rome era the military camp served an entire Roman legion. Archaeological wikt:investigations have revealed many details of these Roman camps in places such as England and Scotland. == References == Category:Army Category:Military buildings and structures
