Subject: Pro bono
'''Pro bono''' is a Latin language phrase meaning "for [the] good", that is used to mean that a legal professional (lawyer) who would otherwise be paid money for their work, works charity (practice), for the benefit of society, typically on behalf of a person or organization who cannot pay for some reason, such as poverty. == Related pages == *Volunteering == References == Category:Legal terms Category:Giving
