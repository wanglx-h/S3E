File:Broadway Crowds (5896264776) crop.jpg '''Broadway''' is a -long main street in New York City. It is different from other streets in that it runs diagonally across the Grid plan of Manhattan, diagonally crossing newer streets. The street also goes through the Bronx. It continues north as U.S. Route 9 to Albany, New York, the capital of New York (state) State. Times Square, where Broadway meets 7th Avenue and 42nd Street, is a center of entertainment and is famous for its many Broadway theatres and Play (theatre). One of the New York City Subway lines, the BMT Broadway Line, runs under the street in Manhattan, south of Times Square.
