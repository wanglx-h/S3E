They formed in Bellevue, Washington, Washington in 1982. They have made 16 Album, one extended play (EP), and many DVD. When the band was made, its members were singer Geoff Tate, guitarist Michael Wilton and Chris DeGarmo, drummer Scott Rockenfield, and bassist Eddie Jackson. Queensrÿche has sold over 20 million albums. They were pioneers of power metal and later progressive metal in the 1980s. Many people say that ''Operation: Mindcrime'', an album they released in 1988, is one of the best heavy metal Concept album ever made.
