File:San Diego Air & Space Museum entrance 2009.jpg on the left and an A-12 Oxcart on the right.]] '''San Diego Aerospace Museum''' is an aircraft and space exploration museum in San Diego, California, USA. It contains many original and copies of old aircraft and spacecraft, including: *A Lockheed A-12 Oxcart *A Ford Motor Company Trimotor *A Supermarine Spitfire Mk.XVI *A North American Aviation P-51 Mustang *The Apollo 9 command module "Gumdrop" *A SPAD VII.c.1 *A Nieuport Nieuport 28 *A Curtiss JN4 Jenny *A Curtiss P-40 Warhawk *A North American Aviation F-86 Sabre *A Mikoyan-Gurevich MiG-15 Fagot *A Mikoyan-Gurevich MiG-17 Fresco *A McDonnell Douglas F-4 Phantom II *A Bell Aircraft Corporation AH-1 Cobra *A PBY Catalina == Other websites == *[http://www.aerospacemuseum.org San Diego Aerospace Museum website] Category:Museums in California Category:Aircraft museums Category:Buildings and structures in San Diego
