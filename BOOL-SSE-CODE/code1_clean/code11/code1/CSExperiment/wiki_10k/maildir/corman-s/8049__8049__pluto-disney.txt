Subject: Pluto (Disney)
'''Pluto''' is a fictional character non-Anthropomorphism dog that belongs to Mickey Mouse. He is a golden-yellow bloodhound dog with a green collar. He was introduced in 1930 and got his name from the dwarf planet Pluto (planet). Pluto taught many kids lessons such as sharing, not to have jealousy, and many others. Pluto was very energetic.
