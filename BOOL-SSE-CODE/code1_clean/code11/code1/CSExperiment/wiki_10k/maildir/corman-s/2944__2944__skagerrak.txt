Subject: Skagerrak
Image:Nseamap.gif '''Skagerrak''' is a sea in the northern part of Europe between Norway and Denmark. Skagerrak is a part of the North Sea. To the east is Kattegat. Close to the Norwegian coast is a deep trench (700 m), the rest of the sea is shallow. Oil and gas are produced from oil platform in the Skagerrak. Category:Seas of the Atlantic Ocean
