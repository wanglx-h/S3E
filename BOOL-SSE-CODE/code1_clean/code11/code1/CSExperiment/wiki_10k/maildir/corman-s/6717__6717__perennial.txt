Subject: Perennial
File:פארק הכרמל 6 - גנים לאומיים בצפון הארץ - אתרי מורשת 2016 (142).jpg A '''perennial''' is a plant that bears leaf and perhaps flowers every year. Some plants only bear leaves and flowers once, then they die. Such plants are called ''annual''. Other plants live for two years. They are called ''biennial''. Most trees and shrubs are perennial plants. Category:Perennials
