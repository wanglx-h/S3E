Subject: Otherkin
'''Otherkin''' is a subculture where people identify as a non-human thing. Some otherkin believe their identity comes from a non-human spirit, ancestry, or symbolism. People also believe that Otherkin get their identity from neurodivergence. The term has since been widespread, and it usually refers to many non-human identities, including therians. Otherkin originally came from Internet in the 1990s when people started identifying as elves or other fantasy creatures. == References == Category:Lifestyles Category:Illusions
