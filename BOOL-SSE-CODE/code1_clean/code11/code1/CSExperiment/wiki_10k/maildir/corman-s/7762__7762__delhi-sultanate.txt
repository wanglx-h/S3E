Most of these dynasties ruled from Delhi. This includes the '''Slave dynasty''' (1206-90), the '''Khilji dynasty''' (1290-1320), the '''Tughlaq dynasty''' (1320-1413), the '''Sayyid dynasty''' (1414-51), and the '''Lodi dynasty''' (1451-1526).
