Subject: Bewitched
[[File:Agnes Moorehead Dick York Elizabeth Montgomery Bewitched 1964.JPG|right|175px|thumb|Agnes Moorehead (Endora) [top], Dick York (Darrin) [left], and Elizabeth Montgomery (Samantha) [right] at the beginning of ''Bewitched''.]] '''''Bewitched''''' is an American situation comedy that was shown on TV by American Broadcasting Company from 1964 to 1972. It is about a man who is married to a witch. The lead characters are Darrin and Samantha Stephens. Samantha twitches her nose or moves her hands to make a spell happen. Samantha was played by Elizabeth Montgomery. Darrin was played by Dick York from 1964 to 1969. Because of illness, Dick York left the show and was replaced by Dick Sargent who played Darrin from 1969 to 1972. Samantha's mother Endora was played by Agnes Moorehead. Endora dislikes Darrin and often makes things difficult for him.
