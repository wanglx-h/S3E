File:Ncwikiquote2.png '''Wikiquote''' is a sister project of Wikipedia. It is one of many projects run by the Wikimedia Foundation. Wikiquote was based on an idea by Daniel Alston and made by Brion Vibber. The Task of the project is to produce a large reference of Quotation from famous people, Book, and Proverb, and to give details about them. ==Languages== The project was first created in English language. Meanwhile, in July 2004, more Language were added.
