They formed in Mesa, Arizona in 1994. To date, they have released nine studio albums. They have released many singles, including ''Sweetness (song)'', ''The Middle'', ''Bleed American'', ''Rockstar'', and ''Let It Happen''. They used to be part of the emo scene and many would still call them that. However, the band has never liked that term and do not even understand that.
