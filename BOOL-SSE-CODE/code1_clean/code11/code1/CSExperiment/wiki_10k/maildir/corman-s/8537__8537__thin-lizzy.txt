Subject: Thin Lizzy
'''Thin Lizzy''' are an Republic of Ireland hard rock band that started in Dublin in 1969. Up to 1984 the band were led by Bass guitar, songwriter, and singing Phil Lynott (1949-1986). The band's famous song "The Boys Are Back in Town" is still played a lot on classic rock radio stations. Since Lynott's death, other former members have carried on using the band name. Phil Lynott wrote most of the band's songs. The band's music showed many musical styles including: country music, psychedelic rock, Celtic music, and folk music which Lynott mixed with hard rock and heavy metal music styles.
