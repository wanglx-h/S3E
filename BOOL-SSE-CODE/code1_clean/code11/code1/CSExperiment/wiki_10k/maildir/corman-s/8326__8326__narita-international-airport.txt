Subject: Narita International Airport
File:Narita International Airport Terminal 1.JPG is a Japanese airport that is in Chiba Prefecture, near Tokyo.Ministry of Land, Infrastructure, and Transport (MLIT), [http://www.mlit.go.jp/koku/15_hf_000032.html Narita International Airport]; retrieved 2012-1-20. It is one of the two wikt:primary airports in the Tokyo region.Most international flights pass through Narita Airport. Almost all domestic flights to and from Tokyo pass through Tokyo International Airport which is also known as ''Tokyo Haneda''.
